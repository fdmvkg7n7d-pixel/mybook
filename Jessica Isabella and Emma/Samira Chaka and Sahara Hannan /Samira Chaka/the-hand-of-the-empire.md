# The Hand of the Empire — Sabrina’s Directives

**Purpose:** To demonstrate the terrifying efficiency of Sabrina Bryna’s power. This scene shows that while the RCMP follows a protocol, Sabrina controls the people who write the protocol.

**Characters:**
- **Sabrina Bryna Key:** The CEO. Her signs are sharp and economical.
- **The Interpreter:** Professional ASL-to-voice conduit.
- **Minister Julian Thorne:** A high-ranking government official who is a subordinate in this interaction.

**Setting:** 
- **Sabrina’s Command Center (Vaughan Estate):** Secure office with encrypted screens.

---

## Beat Sheet

### 1. The Preparation for War
- Sabrina prepares her screens: RCMP reports, Siberian maps, and the encrypted line to the government.
- The atmosphere is clinical and focused.

### 2. The Call: Cutting Through the Noise
- Minister Thorne answers with polished political warmth.
- Sabrina interrupts him immediately.
- She states that the RCMP's performance is a failure of leadership.

### 3. The Application of Leverage
- Sabrina mentions a specific, massive Key Corporation project currently "under review."
- She makes the link explicit: the project's viability depends on a breakthrough in the Samira Chaka case.

### 4. The Shift in Power
- The Minister's tone shifts to urgent professionalism.
- Sabrina issues three non-negotiable directives:
    1. Direct oversight of the Moscow operation by a high-level liaison.
    2. Escalation of resources and intelligence sharing.
    3. a requirement for locations, not "updates."

### 5. The Finality
- Sabrina's final word: *"I do not want promises, Minister. I want my niece."*
- She disconnects the call.

### 6. Closing Beat
- Sabrina activates the "Shadow Search" private intelligence team.
- Theme: The total inversion of power. The Corporation competes with the government.
