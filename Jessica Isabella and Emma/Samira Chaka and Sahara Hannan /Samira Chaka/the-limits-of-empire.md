# The Limits of Empire — The Failed Search for Samira

**Purpose:** To create a crushing emotional low point by demonstrating that even the unfathomable power of the Key Corporation has its limits. The state's erasure of Samira is more effective than the Corporation's ability to find her.

**Characters:**
- **Sabrina Bryna Key:** The CEO facing a problem that cannot be solved with money.
- **Jessica Isabella & Emma Fritz:** Devastated by the realization that Sabrina's mobilization isn't working.
- **Agent Miller (RCMP):** Caught in a vice between his superiors and the lack of leads.
- **The "Shadow" Lead:** Private intelligence rep delivering the news of the failure.

**Setting:** 
- **The Command Center (Vaughan Estate):** Blue light of screens and maps of the Siberian taiga.

---

## Beat Sheet

### 1. The Political Pressure (The RCMP's Panic)
- Agent Miller receives calls from his bosses, who are being squeezed by Sabrina's political pressure.
- Miller is pressured to find "something," but the information from Russia is non-existent.

### 2. The Shadow Search: The "Ghost" Reports
- The private intelligence lead reports on the "Black-Ops" search.
- They found "ghost" facilities—decoys planted by the state to waste time.
- Conclusion: Facility 12 does not exist on any map or record.

### 3. The Financial Failure (The Bribe Paradox)
- Sabrina reviews the results of her bribes to Russian officials.
- The state has used the bribes as a revenue stream, providing contradictory and false stories in exchange for money.

### 4. The Breaking Point (The Meeting of the Mothers)
- Jessica and Emma enter the command center and see the red markers of failed leads.
- The realization that the RCMP and the Corporation have both failed.
- Jessica's question: *"Is she gone? Did they actually erase her?"*

### 5. The CEO's Fury (The Cold Resolve)
- Sabrina's frustration turns into a cold obsession.
- She vows that the la search will not stop, and she begins to consider more radical, "war-like" options.

### 6. Closing Beat
- Final image: A vast, white, empty map of the Siberian taiga with a single blinking cursor.
- Theme: The insignificance of wealth in the face of total state control.
