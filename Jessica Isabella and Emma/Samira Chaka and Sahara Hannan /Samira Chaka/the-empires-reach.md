# The Empire's Reach — The Plea to Sabrina Bryna

**Purpose:** To transition the search for Samira from a failing diplomatic effort (RCMP) to a high-stakes corporate and political operation. 

**Characters:**
- **Jessica Isabella Key:** Driven by maternal instinct, unable to accept the RCMP's failure.
- **Emma Fritz:** The strategic co-mother.
- **Remi:** Caretaker for the boys in Halifax.
- **Sabrina Bryna Key:** CEO of the Key Corporation. The family's ultimate power-broker.

**Setting:** 
- **Jessica and Emma's Home (Halifax):** Hand-off of the children to Remi.
- **The Key Estate (Vaughan, Ontario):** The seat of the family's power.

---

## Beat Sheet

### 1. The Hand-Off (The Trust in Remi)
- Remi arrives to take care of Eliot and Kalvin.
- Jessica and Emma leave the boys in his care, allowing them to focus on the search.

### 2. Arrival in Vaughan
- Transition to the Key estate. The shift from domesticity to the imposing power of the family empire.

### 3. The Meeting with Sabrina
- Conversation conducted entirely in **ASL**. 
- Jessica describes the failure of the RCMP and the "blankness" Sahara feels.
- Emma requests a corporate intervention rather than a family favor.

### 4. The Reveal: The Power of the Network
- Sabrina reveals she knows exactly which government officials can push the police to find Samira.
- She explains that the Key Corporation is a pillar of the economy, and a call from her is a directive.

### 5. The Activation of the Empire
- Sabrina issues directives:
    - **Political Squeeze:** Contingent investment in exchange for a breakthrough.
    - **Shadow Search:** Activation of Black-Ops intelligence.
    - **Financial Siege:** Buying or coercing Russian officials.

### 6. Closing Beat
- Sabrina's final fact: *"The system is designed to be slow. I am going to make it fast. I am activating every resource I have."*
- Jessica and Emma leave the estate in awe.
