# The Cold Welcome — Meeting the Team and the Final Assessment

**Purpose:** To transition Samira from the "Seduction" phase to the "Operational" phase through a grueling, high-pressure psychological interview and a meeting with her new "team." The state is no longer courting her; they are assessing the structural integrity of her psyche.

**Characters:**
- **Samira Chaka Key (20):** Still wearing the mask of the "chosen" dancer, but beginning to feel the clinical weight of the state.
- **Colonel Irina Orlova:** The Lead Examiner. She manages the tempo of the questioning, shifting between cold professionalism and the remnants of her "mentor" persona.
- **Instructor Sofia:** The Psychological Mapper. She monitors micro-expressions, breathing, and pupil dilation.
- **Instructor Grigori:** The Physical Analyst. He probes Samira's relationship with pain and discipline.

**Setting:** 
- **The Briefing Room (KGB HQ):** A sterile, sound-dampened room with a single bright light over a metal table. No mirrors.

---

## Beat Sheet: The Interrogation and Introduction

### 1. The Arrival and the Atmosphere
- Samira is brought into the room and told to stand. She is not given a chair.
- Orlova, Sofia, and Grigori sit behind the table. The "mentorship" is dead; she is now "Candidate Four."

### 2. Phase I: The Surface and the Mask (Rapid-Fire)
- Rapid-fire questions about her daily routine, students, and Bolshoi corrections.
- The goal is to establish a baseline of truth and look for inconsistencies in her "performance."

### 3. Phase II: The Mapping of Liabilities (The Family Probe)
- Questions shift to her family. They probe her relationship with Sahara and her mothers in Halifax.
- They ask if she would prioritize a mission over the secret of her family, testing her loyalties.

### 4. Phase III: The Moral Pivot (The Price of Service)
- Hypothetical scenarios where Samira must choose between the state and a person.
- Orlova asks if her family's principle—'your body is yours'—is a truth or a luxury she can no longer afford.

### 5. Phase IV: The Physicality of Pain (The Grigori Probe)
- Grigori asks about her history with injury and pain.
- He probes whether she focuses on the source of pain or moves her consciousness away from the body.

### 6. The Verdict and the New Nomenclature
- Orlova declares the assessment complete. Samira is officially a state asset.
- She is informed she must leave for the taiga immediately.
- The final command: *"Leave your name, your family, and your vanity in this room. You are now a weapon in training."*
