# The Wall of Silence — The RCMP Interview of Andrei

**Purpose:** To create a critical failure in the search for Samira. By denying any knowledge of her disappearance, Andrei effectively shuts down the investigation. He believes that any official inquiry will put Samira in danger at the camp.

**Characters:**
- **Andrei:** The "Secret Keeper." He knows the truth but lies to the RCMP to stop them from investigating.
- **Agent Miller (RCMP):** Professional and increasingly frustrated. He has just come from St. Petersburg, where he interviewed Sahara and Vladislav.

**Setting:** 
- **Samira’s Moscow Apartment:** A place of absence. The red coat on the hook is the only thing in the room that tells the truth.

---

## Beat Sheet

### 1. The Arrival of a Clueless Investigator
- Agent Miller arrives in Moscow. He knows nothing about a seminar or the camp.
- He only has the reports from Halifax and Sahara's visceral "blankness" in St. Petersburg.

### 2. The Question of the Void
- Miller asks directly: *"Do you know where she is? Do you know why she left?"*
- Andrei's response: *"I don't know. She's gone, and I don't have any answers."*

### 3. The Denial of Everything
- Miller probes for notes, phone calls, or signs of distress.
- Andrei denies everything, framing the disappearance as an absolute mystery.
- He subtly suggests that Sahara's reaction is an over-extension of her intuition.

### 4. The Frustrated Confrontation
- Miller realizes he is being lied to. *"Andrei, if you are protecting her, you are doing it the wrong way. Why are you lying to me?"*
- Andrei maintains the mask of ignorance: *"I'm not lying, Agent Miller. I just don't know where she is."*

### 5. The Closing Beat
- Miller leaves defeated. The case is now a "cold" missing persons file with conflicting emotional reports.
- Andrei sinks into a chair, having successfully blocked the RCMP but leaving Samira alone and Sahara betrayed.
