# Samira Chaka's KGB Siberian Training Camp — Age Twenty (~2060)

**Purpose:** A detailed scene outline set in the freezing depths of a Siberian winter at Facility 12. The focus is on the severe physical and psychological trials designed to weaponize her Bolshoi discipline.

**Characters:**
- **Samira Chaka Key (20):** Subjected to extreme conditions. She learns to separate her physical "casing" from her internal "core."
- **Instructor Grigori:** The physical tormentor.
- **Instructor Sofia:** The psychological examiner.
- **Colonel Irina Orlova:** The overseer.

**Setting:** 
- **Facility 12:** A decommissioned penal sanatorium in the Krasnoyarsk taiga. Temperatures between -25°C and -40°C.

---

## Detailed Scene Beats

### 1. Arrival and the Strip-Down
- Transported in an unheated truck. Stripped of all Moscow clothes and issued a thin grey wool uniform.
- First shock of the concrete cell in the Isolation Wing.

### 2. The "Shattered Swan" Stamina Drill
- Holding an agonizing classical *arabesque* position on freezing pine planks for 45 minutes.
- Failure results in added time. The goal is to ignore frostbite and muscle failure.

### 3. Somatic Deception: The Ice Interrogation
- Strapped to a metal chair in -30°C night wind.
- Must use breath control to suppress shivering (somatic control) while being interrogated.
- Failure results in buckets of freezing well-water.

### 4. Spatial Evasion in the Deep Taiga
- Hunted by Grigori and guard dogs in waist-deep snow at midnight.
- Learns to move silently and leave no footprints.

### 5. Visual Memory Under Sensory Deprivation
- Locked in the "Cold Box" (dark metal container) for 30 hours.
- Given 5 seconds to view espionage documents, then forced to reconstruct them verbally in the dark.

### 6. The "Social Ballet" under Humiliation
- Forced to perform at a mock gala in thin evening wear while exhausted and bruised.
- Must maintain an elegant aristocratic poise while being physically and psychologically mocked.

### 7. The Injury and the Lack of Care
- Suffers a severe grade-two ankle sprain.
- Forced to run 6km uphill through snow on the sprained ankle, hiding the limp to avoid being discarded.

### 8. The Midnight Interrogation: The Break Point
- Sleep-deprived and exhausted. Orlova uses recordings of Sahara's voice to break her.
- Samira refuses to break, visualizing her mother Emma's *sabr*.

### 9. The Graduation Test
- Infiltration of a simulated consular building during a blizzard.
- Extracts documents with feral precision and escapes into the storm.

### 10. The Return
- Returns to her Moscow apartment. She is thinner and scarred.
- Realizes her body will never belong to her in the same way again.

## The Somatic Compromise Tests

### Test 1: Instructor Anton (The Clinical)
- A required test of sexual compromise.
- Samira survives through total dissociation: the "Casing" is left on the bed, while the "Core" retreats to memories of her family.

### Test 2: Lieutenant Zoya (The Mirror)
- A test with a female soldier.
- Samira faces the danger of mutual recognition.
- She chooses solidarity over extraction, kissing Zoya as a declaration of autonomy.

### Test 3: Candidate Polina (The Spectacle)
- A public act performed in front of nineteen peers.
- Samira uses choreography to shield Polina and manage the gaze of the audience.
- She turns the act into a performance, preserving her Core while the state sees only compliance.
