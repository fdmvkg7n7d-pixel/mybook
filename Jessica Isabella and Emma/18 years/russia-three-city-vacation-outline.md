## TONE & BOUNDARIES

## ITINERARY — THE SPINE OF THE TRIP

A **21-day, three-week July arc**, one week per city, in the order **St. Petersburg → Moscow → Sochi**. This order puts the **initial telling in St. Petersburg early**, gives Sahara the whole rest of the trip to process, and lands the **resolution in Sochi** at the very end.

**Beach rule honored:** **two beach/swim days in every city** (6 beach days total). Because St. Petersburg and Moscow are not on the sea, their beach days happen at the region's real **river/lake/Gulf beaches**; **Sochi** is true Black Sea coast.

> **Geography note (so the text stays accurate):** *St. Petersburg* beach days use the **Gulf of Finland** shore (e.g., the beaches at **Sestroretsk / Solnechnoye** on the Karelian Isthmus, or **Peterhof's** gulf-side shore). *Moscow* beach days use a **river/reservoir beach** (e.g., **Serebryany Bor**, the city's island beach on the Moskva River, or the **Khimki Reservoir** beaches). *Sochi* beach days are on the **Black Sea** (central Sochi pebble beaches / the **Imeretinsky** resort beach in the former Olympic Park).

### LEG 1 — ST. PETERSBURG (Days 1–7) — *Sahara's city; Vaganova*

- **Day 1 — Arrive & settle.** Fly in; check into a hotel near the historic center. Light first evening: unpack, *bismillah* over a quiet suite dinner, early night to beat jet lag. Sahara checks into her CGM routine after travel.
- **Day 2 — Sahara's world.** Visit the **Vaganova Academy** and the city it lives in; the family sees Sahara on her own turf — her exact, lyrical classicism, her teachers, her studios. **Mariinsky Theatre** in the evening (performance or a backstage look). *This grounds the trip in Sahara before the news lands.*
- **Day 3 — THE TELLING.** A day kept gentle on purpose. The mothers tell **both** twins, *before* any pregnancy, that they want another child (full beats in `telling-the-twins-scene-outline.md`). **Immediate split: Samira enthusiastic; Sahara thoughtfully resistant.** This sets the trip's emotional question. Evening: space, not pressure.
- **Day 4 — BEACH DAY #1 (Gulf of Finland).** Train out to **Sestroretsk / Solnechnoye** on the Gulf. Swimming, sun, a packed lunch. The twins are comfortable being undressed for swimming, per the family's **"your body is yours"** principle (shown plainly). **Sahara manages her diabetes in the water** practically (water-mode/pump-off as appropriate, CGM watched), no spectacle. First relaxed, body-positive beat where Sahara can start to *feel* the family's steadiness rather than just hear it.
- **Day 5 — BEACH DAY #2 (Peterhof gulf shore).** Combine the **Peterhof** fountains/gardens with a second gulf-side swim. A long, unhurried day. A **quiet one-on-one between Sahara and Emma** (the stayer) fits naturally here — listening, no pushing; "feelings are allowed to change."
- **Day 6 — City as a unit.** The four of them as tourists: Hermitage / Church of the Savior on Spilled Blood / a canal walk. Sunday observance worked in where possible (Catholic Mass for Jessica/the family; Emma's quiet faith carried alongside). **[TBD]** exact Mass logistics.
- **Day 7 — Travel to Moscow.** Day train (Sapsan) — built-in travel time for a **private twin-to-twin conversation** (see Leg 2). Evening arrival in Moscow.

### LEG 2 — MOSCOW (Days 8–14) — *Samira's city; Bolshoi*

- **Day 8 — Arrive & settle.** Hotel near the center; easy evening; suite dinner.
- **Day 9 — Samira's world.** Visit the **Bolshoi Academy** and the **Bolshoi Theatre**; the family sees Samira's bolder, dramatic, athletic style on *her* turf — the mirror to Sahara's Vaganova day. Samira is in her element; her enthusiasm for the new sibling visibly reinforced.
- **Day 10 — BEACH DAY #3 (Serebryany Bor).** The city's island beach on the Moskva River. Swimming, pine woods, a relaxed riverside day. The **private twin talk** can culminate or recur here: **Samira frames a new sibling through their own story** — twins who chose different schools and still hold each other — *"family can grow and support each other even apart."* Same bodily-autonomy ease; Sahara's diabetes handled practically in the water.
- **Day 11 — City as a unit.** Red Square, the Kremlin, St. Basil's, GUM; an ordinary, happy tourist day.
- **Day 12 — BEACH DAY #4 (Khimki Reservoir).** Second Moscow swim day, a little out of the center; paddle/relax. A **quiet one-on-one between Sahara and Jessica** (the reacher) lands well here — Sahara voicing fears (the change; her diabetes; the family shifting), Jessica listening without pushing.
- **Day 13 — Ballet immersion / rest.** A class observation, a rehearsal, or simply a slower day; *bismillah* dinners; room to let Sahara keep processing.
- **Day 14 — Travel to Sochi.** Fly south to the Black Sea coast. Evening arrival; the air changes — sea, palms, resort summer.

### LEG 3 — SOCHI (Days 15–21) — *shared city; Black Sea; former Winter-Olympics host city*

*Neutral ground belonging to neither twin's school — the right place for the resolution. Olympic-host-city atmosphere as a motif of **discipline, overcoming, achievement** (resonant for Sahara, who already lives disciplined because of diabetes). **Note:** this is the host-city legacy/atmosphere, **not** a live Games.*

- **Day 15 — Arrive & settle.** Check into a coastal hotel; first toes in the Black Sea at sunset; relaxed dinner.
- **Day 16 — BEACH DAY #5 (central Sochi / Black Sea).** A full sea day — swimming, the pebble beach, the warmth and ease of a true coast. The most openly joyful, body-positive beat of the trip; Sahara confident managing her diabetes in the sea by now.
- **Day 17 — Olympic Park & Krasnaya Polyana.** Visit the **Olympic Park (Adler/Imeretinsky)** and/or the mountain cluster. The discipline/overcoming motif made literal — venues, stories of athletes who pushed through. Quiet inspiration for Sahara.
- **Day 18 — BEACH DAY #6 (Imeretinsky resort beach).** Second sea day, by the Olympic Park shore. By now the family rhythm is easy; this is where Sahara's shift is **felt** before it's spoken.
- **Day 19 — Sea day / drift.** An unstructured, golden day — the four of them, no agenda. Space for the change of heart to surface naturally.
- **Day 20 — THE RESOLUTION (final full day).** On the final evening (sea-front dinner or a last walk on the shore), **Sahara tells her mothers she has come around.** **Jessica & Emma respond with relief and joy** — validating her honest initial resistance and thanking both daughters for being truthful the whole way. Both daughters now positive: Samira enthusiastic throughout, Sahara arrived thoughtfully. → see Resolution section below.
- **Day 21 — Depart for home.** Quiet sense of celebration. Medical steps begin only **after** the trip.
