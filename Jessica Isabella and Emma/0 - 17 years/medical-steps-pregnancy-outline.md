- Interfaith/family moments: Prayers, quiet reflections, perhaps involving Terri Anne or other sisters.

- The twins (Samira and Sahara) reacting to the news that it's twins (Samira enthusiastic from the start; Sahara, who initially resisted but came to like the idea by the end of the three-week Russia vacation visiting Moscow, St. Petersburg, Sochi Winter Olympics, is now positive; parents were so glad at the vacation's resolution after activities across cities, twin talks, diabetes reflection, quiet conversations, Olympics inspiration).
