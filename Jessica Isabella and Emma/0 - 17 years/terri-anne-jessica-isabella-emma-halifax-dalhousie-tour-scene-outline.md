# SCENE OUTLINE: TERRI ANNE TAKES JESSICA ISABELLA AND EMMA TO HALIFAX FOR DALHOUSIE UNIVERSITY TOUR

*Terri Anne travels with Jessica Isabella, Emma, and Jessica Isabella’s identical twin daughters — Samira Chaka and Sahara Hannan — to Halifax, Nova Scotia. The purpose of the trip is to tour Dalhousie University, where both Jessica Isabella and Emma have expressed serious interest in studying medicine. The tone is warm and bonding while also carrying educational seriousness.*

---

## CANON BACKDROP & PLACEMENT

- **Characters Present:**
  - **Terri Anne Williams-Key** (mother / grandmother)
  - **Jessica Isabella Key** (mother of the twins)
  - **Emma**
  - **Samira Chaka Key** and **Sahara Hannan Key** (identical twin daughters of Jessica Isabella)
- **Location:** Halifax, Nova Scotia (Dalhousie University)
- **Purpose:** University tour focused on medical programs
- **Tone:** Warm and bonding with educational seriousness

---

## SCENE PURPOSE & THEMATIC CORE

This scene highlights Terri Anne’s supportive role as both mother and grandmother. It shows Jessica Isabella and Emma taking a serious step toward their future as doctors, while also demonstrating the close, multi-generational bond within the family. The presence of the young twins adds warmth and grounding to the otherwise serious educational journey.

---

## SCENE OUTLINE — BEATS & PROSE CUES

### Beat 1 — Arrival in Halifax

Terri Anne, Jessica Isabella, Emma, and the twins (Samira Chaka and Sahara Hannan) arrive in Halifax. The twins, who are treated as one, stay close to their mother and grandmother. Jessica Isabella and Emma are visibly focused and excited about the upcoming tour. Terri Anne watches her daughter and Emma with quiet pride.

### Beat 2 — The Dalhousie University Tour

The group takes a guided tour of Dalhousie University. They visit the medical school facilities, lecture halls, and research areas. Jessica Isabella and Emma ask detailed questions about the medical program, admissions process, and clinical opportunities.

Terri Anne walks slightly behind them, listening attentively. She occasionally exchanges warm glances with Jessica Isabella. The twins remain well-behaved, holding hands as they walk through the campus.

### Beat 3 — A Serious Conversation

During a break in the tour, Terri Anne speaks privately with Jessica Isabella and Emma.

> “Both of you have thought very carefully about this. I can see how serious you are. Medicine is not an easy path, but if this is truly what you want, I will support you in every way I can.”

Jessica Isabella nods, her expression steady.

> “We know it won’t be easy. But we both want this.”

Emma adds quietly:

> “We’ve talked about it a lot. We want to do this together.”

Terri Anne places a hand on each of their arms.

> “Then we’ll make sure you’re prepared.”

### Beat 4 — Time with the Twins

Later in the day, the group spends time near the Halifax waterfront. Samira Chaka and Sahara Hannan play together while Jessica Isabella and Emma continue discussing the university. Terri Anne sits with the twins, watching them with affection.

The twins are treated as one unit — often referred to together and moving in sync. Their presence brings a sense of warmth and normalcy to the otherwise intense educational focus of the trip.

### Beat 5 — Evening Reflection

That evening, over dinner, Terri Anne speaks gently with Jessica Isabella.

> “You’re building something important — not just for yourself, but for your daughters too. They’re watching how you pursue your dreams.”

Jessica Isabella looks at her mother with gratitude.

> “I want them to see that it’s possible.”

Terri Anne smiles softly.

> “They already do.”
