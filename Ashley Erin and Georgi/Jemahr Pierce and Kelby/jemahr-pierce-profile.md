## BIRTH & ORIGINS

Jemahr Pierce is adopted as a **newborn** by Ashley Erin Key-Kupchenko and Georgi Kupchenko when they are **thirty-five**. He is adopted at the same time as his **identical twin, Mikkel James**.

**Biological background:** Their biological mother was very young — a teenager — when she gave birth to the twins. Unable to raise two newborn boys, she placed them for adoption, a decision that led them to Ashley Erin Key-Kupchenko and Georgi Kupchenko.
