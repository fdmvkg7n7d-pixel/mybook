# SCENE OUTLINE: FAO SCHWARZ (DAY 6)

*Part of the seven-day New York trip. This warm, bonding scene takes place on Day 6. After the professional commitments and the special dinner with Valerie on Day 5, the family spends a fun, light-hearted day in New York City centered around FAO Schwarz.*

---

## CANON BACKDROP & PLACEMENT

- **Day:** Day 6 of the trip
- **Time:** Daytime
- **Location:** FAO Schwarz, Fifth Avenue, Manhattan
- **Characters Present:**
  - Ashley Erin Key (7)
  - Andrea Jennifer Key (8)
  - Terri Anne Williams-Key
- **Tone:** Very warm, playful, and bonding

---

## SCENE PURPOSE & THEMATIC CORE

This scene gives the two sisters a joyful, child-focused day after several more serious and professional days. It emphasizes fun, sisterly excitement, and Terri Anne creating happy memories for her daughters.

---

## SCENE OUTLINE — BEATS & PROSE CUES

### Beat 1 — Arrival at FAO Schwarz

The private car drops them off in front of the iconic FAO Schwarz store on Fifth Avenue. The large, colorful storefront immediately excites the girls. Ashley Erin and Andrea Jennifer walk hand-in-hand as they enter the store.

The moment they step inside, they are surrounded by towering shelves of toys, giant stuffed animals, and the famous floor piano.

### Beat 2 — Exploring the Store

The three of them spend time wandering through different sections. Andrea Jennifer is drawn to the dolls and dress-up items, while Ashley Erin is fascinated by the musical instruments and the large stuffed animals.

Terri Anne stays close, smiling as she watches her daughters explore. She occasionally points out something she thinks they would like and lets them lead the way.

### Beat 3 — The Floor Piano

They spend a long time at the famous giant floor piano. Ashley Erin and Andrea Jennifer take turns stepping on the keys, laughing as they try to play simple songs together. Terri Anne records a short video on her phone of the two of them playing “Twinkle Twinkle Little Star” by jumping on the keys.

The girls are giggling and having a wonderful time.

### Beat 4 — Choosing Something Special

Before leaving, Terri Anne lets each girl pick one special item to take home. Ashley Erin chooses a small, soft stuffed lion (a subtle nod to *The Lion King*), while Andrea Jennifer picks a beautiful doll.

Terri Anne also buys them each a small bag of candy from the store’s candy section as a treat.

### Beat 5 — Lunch Nearby

After FAO Schwarz, they have a relaxed lunch at a nearby family-friendly restaurant. The girls talk excitedly about the toys they saw and the floor piano. Ashley Erin keeps hugging her new stuffed lion.

Terri Anne listens to them with warmth and affection, occasionally joining in their conversation.

### Beat 6 — Heading Back

On the drive back to the Connecticut mansion, the girls are happy and tired from the fun day. Ashley Erin falls asleep leaning against Andrea Jennifer, still holding her stuffed lion. Andrea Jennifer gently strokes her sister’s hair.

Terri Anne watches them in the rearview mirror with a soft, contented smile.
