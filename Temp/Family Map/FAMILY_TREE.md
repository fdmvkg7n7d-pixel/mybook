## LEGEND (relationship types)

- **BIO** biological child
- **ADOPTED** adopted
- **FOSTER** foster child / ward
- **STEP / SOCIAL** raised by a non-biological parent as their own
- **SURROGATE / EGG-DONOR** born via surrogacy and/or egg donation
- **DONOR** conceived via a known sperm donor
- **GUARDIANSHIP** child raised under a will/named guardian
- **TWIN / TRIPLET** multiple birth
- **✝ deceased** · **♥ partnered/married** · **→ see sub-branch**
