## Executive Itinerary Summary (~Summer 2041)

**The Travellers:** Mike Key, Terri Anne Williams-Key, and their eight homeschooled daughters (Patti Anne 18, Jessica 15, Rachel 15, Kiley 11, Sabrina 9 *with Cerebral Palsy perfectly accommodated*, Fallon 7, Andrea 6, and Ashley Erin 5).

### Week 1 — Paris Living & Ordinary Culture
* Sited in a high-walled luxury townhome compound in Le Marais or a private Seine-view penthouse.
* **Morning Market Runs:** The family walks every morning to authentic local street markets to purchase warm baguettes, wheels of Camembert, and fresh French strawberries.
* **Sketching Sessions:** Andrea Jennifer (6) and Fallon Glenda (7) spend their afternoons in the shaded courtyards of the Louvre and along the Seine, sketching stone bridges and gargoyles in their travel notebooks.

---

### Week 2 — Normandy Historical Pilgrimage
* A three-day road trip through Normandy via private, tinted Mercedes executive vans.
* **Juno Beach:** Sourcing Mike Key's Canadian Fiduciary roots and civic duty with the OPP, they walk the preserved bunkers and sandy coastline of Juno Beach.
* **ASL Duty Lesson:** Sabrina Bryna (9) rolls in her specialized all-terrain beach wheelchair across the Normandy sand. Mike stops by a massive concrete gun emplacement, kneels right in the wet sand, and signs expressive ASL about the ultimate historical price paid by young Canadians on June 6, 1944, to secure structural freedom.
* **Omaha Beach & Colleville-sur-Mer:** The eight sisters walk flawlessly among the rows of white marble crosses, absorbing solemn intergenerational reverence.

---

### Week 3 — Disneyland Paris (Magical Origin)
* A three-day VIP stay at the Disneyland Hotel.
* Immediate front-of-line walk-on access to all classic attractions (*Peter Pan's Flight*, *Pirates of the Caribbean*) and private fireworks viewing balconies.
* **Saga Canon Origin:** This spectacular excursion establishes the exact canonical precedent that inspires Gamaliel and Klára to make *"an annual, fixed Disneyland Paris trip for every grade-eight class"* an ironclad tradition for Pražská Baletní Akademie decades later.

---

### Week 4 — Eiffel Tower Date Night & Matriarchal Anchor
* **The Parents' Milestone:** Mike secures an exclusive window table at **Le Jules Verne** on the second floor of the **Eiffel Tower**, 125 meters above Paris. Terri Anne and Mike share an exquisite, intimate candlelit dinner celebrating twenty years of marriage and genuine duty.
* **Patti Anne's Overarching Anchor Role:** With their parents out in the tower, eighteen-year-old **Patti Anne Key** steps flawlessly into absolute Fiduciary leadership—babysitting the household without outsourced staff. She organizes a beautiful dinner of French cheeses and vegetable tarts (flawlessly supporting Sabrina Bryna's CP core), reads the younger girls bedtime stories, and walks little Ashley Erin (5) to the window to show her the sweeping golden golden beam of the Eiffel Tower: *"Right up there, little one. They're looking down over our roof right now."*
