## THE SECOND GENERATION (The Children)

- **~2040:** **Samira Chaka Key** and **Sahara Hannan Key** are born to Jessica Isabella (age 16).
- **April 2043:** **Gamaliel Galvin Key** is born to Patti Anne (age 21).
- **November 2043:** **Klára** (born Klára Drahoslav) is born — seven months after Gamaliel, same year.
- **December 25, 2044:** **Pandora Paige Key** and **Brandi Jessica Key** are born to Kiley Rose (age 16).
- **~2051:** **Klára joins the family** as Patti Anne's foster daughter (Gamaliel, 8, saved her life; four months earlier he had told Mike about her father's abuse). **From the start, Patti Anne or Aleksander takes Klára to a temple in Vaughan** to honor her great-grandmothers' faith — **and Gamaliel asks to come along from the first visit, to support her as she becomes a member of his family.** The temple's rabbi: Avram Loewy.
- **December 23, 2051:** **Brandi Jessica** is diagnosed with bilateral retinoblastoma at age 7. Pandora is screened and saved.
- **June 2056:** **Palmer Peter Key** and **Salena Sarai Key** are born to Patti Anne (age 34).
- **Late 2059 (November–December):** **Mike Key dies.** Gamaliel and Klára are both 16 — which, given the April/November birthdays, places the death after Klára's November birthday. **Rabbi Loewy attends the funeral Mass** (back row); on the church steps: *"May his memory be a blessing"* / *"Thursday still stands."* That night, over the melted banana split, Gamaliel says *zikhrono livrakha* as his own for the first time.
- **~2074:** **Palmer Peter and Salena Sarai move out** at age 18.
    - Palmer Peter moves to Saudi Arabia.
    - Salena Sarai moves to Perth, Australia.
    - Gamaliel is 31 years old.

## KEY MILESTONES & EVENTS

- **~2035:** Kiley Rose (7) and Jessica Isabella's kitchen accident.
- **~2039:** Kiley Rose (11) touches Jessica's scars for the first time.
- **~2050:** Jessica Isabella and Emma marry.
- **~2055:** Jessica Isabella and Emma have identical twin sons, **Eliot David and Kalvin LaMarcus**.
- **~2055:** Gamaliel (age 12) — the wanting begins: watching Klára carry her great-grandmothers' heritage, he realizes he wants to be Jewish. He carries it quietly through Patti Anne's pregnancy with the twins.
- **Late 2056:** Gamaliel (13) **asks Patti Anne and Aleksander at the kitchen table — Klára present, Patti Anne nursing the infant Salena Sarai — if he can convert to Judaism. They support his decision:** *"Then we'll help you do it properly."*
- **Early 2057:** The family takes Gamaliel (13) to **meet Rabbi Avram Loewy (mid-fifties)** at the Vaughan temple they already bring Klára to. Loewy sets honest terms — no conversion for a minor, but real study: the weekly hour that runs alongside the barre until the leaving at nineteen.
- **June 2061:** Gamaliel and Klára graduate from Victoria Ballet Academy — Gamaliel 18 (April birthday), Klára 17 (she turns 18 that November, when she also ages out of foster care).
- **April–October 2062:** Klára (18) visits her father at Kent Institution, BC; three recovery days in Vancouver with Gamaliel (19); **farewell week in Vaughan** (Dr. Hartwell's and Rabbi Loewy's letters given to Klára; the last Friday — Kabbalat Shabbat at the temple, then the farewell barbecue as Se'udat Shabbat); then **Orlando — eight days with Terri Anne at Cypress Cove** (the naturist community she moved to after Mike's death), including **Discovery Cove and Walt Disney World** (where they decide their academy's trip traditions: **an annual, fixed Disneyland Paris trip for every grade-eight class**, and **a city chosen by each grade-twelve class** — decades later, Klárka's class picks Paris and Simonka's picks Rome); they ask to name the academy for Mike Key — **she talks them out of it** (*a ballet academy needs a hip name* — it becomes **Pražská Baletní Akademie**) **and gives them the theater instead: the Mike Key Theatre**; then **Gamaliel and Klára move to Prague permanently** (no university for either). They carry two letters with them, both given to Klára: **Dr. Naomi Hartwell's letter to her conference colleague Dr. Tereza Dvořáková of Prague, and Rabbi Loewy's letter to his distant cousin, Rabbi Eliáš Löwy.** **Klára contacts neither until she and Gamaliel are settled** — then she reaches out to both (Gamaliel and Klára attend both first meetings), and the two rooms are rebuilt in the new city. They open their ballet storefront soon after arriving — and hire **a good business manager** to handle the academy's financials.
- **~2062–2063 (est.):** Gamaliel's formal conversion to Judaism completes **in Prague's Jewish community, under Rabbi Eliáš Löwy** (beit din, mikveh) — begun under Rabbi Avram Loewy in Vaughan at thirteen, carried to Prague by Loewy's letter to his cousin at the leaving.
- **~2075:** Palmer Peter (age 19) decides he wants to be Muslim, about a year into his Saudi life.
- **~2076:** Palmer Peter converts to Islam (at age 20).
- **~2078:** Palmer Peter begins his plural marriages/partnerships.
