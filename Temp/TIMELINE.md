## KEY SAGA ANCHORS & RITUALS

- **~2035:** Kiley Rose (7) and Jessica Isabella's kitchen accident.

While Terri Anne helps Fallon Glenda (7) and Sabrina Bryna (9)—whose Cerebral Palsy is beautifully accommodated by her custom ergonomic desk chair—with their math schoolwork in the study (using ASL for Sabrina), Mike works with Andrea Jennifer (6) and Ashley Erin (5) after their bath together. Wearing matching pink pyjamas bottoms (with their little feet bare), the game establishes the unbreakable safeword (*"Stop, Daddy"*) and the wide, five-minute unhunted buffer before they voluntarily climb into his lap to be read a bedtime story and tucked into their bed, demonstrating the flawless parental teamwork holding the sprawling Carlisle estate together.
- **Summer 2041 (or Summer 2042):** The Two-Month European Migration. Because all eight daughters are actively homeschooled by Mike and Terri Anne, their learning is continuous and does not stop for institutional terms. Before leaving, Mike and Terri Anne sit their eight homeschooled daughters down at the Carlisle kitchen table to transparently explain their itinerary. In July (Phase 1), their homeschool relocates to Paris, France, for a month of cultural immersion, historical D-Day pilgrimages in Normandy (Juno and Omaha Beach), magical VIP days at Disneyland Paris, and an extraordinary parents' date night at *Le Jules Verne* in the Eiffel Tower while eighteen-year-old Patti Anne steps up to babysit the household. In August (Phase 2), they migrate down to Cap d'Agde, France, for their first naturist vacation sanctuary. Reaching the premier European naturist village on the Mediterranean, they go swimming daily in the Mediterranean Sea and swimming pools, establishing an unblemished emotional room for Jessica Isabella (15), and accommodating Sabrina Bryna's Cerebral Palsy with an all-terrain beach wheelchair and Mike's personal core-support in the surf. On the first Saturday in August, before the late-night catch scene, the younger daughters are entered into Cap d'Agde's Beauty Junior Contest. On the morning of the contest, Terri Anne and her daughters share a lengthy conversation around the dining room table where Kiley Rose, Sabrina Bryna, and Fallon Glenda make an absolute inter-sibling pact to not participate if Ashley Erin wouldn't be allowed; Terri Anne completely validates their solidarity. Phase 2 culminates late one night in Cap d'Agde when Mike walks into their naturist compound finding eighteen-year-old Patti Anne involved with this eighteen-year-old fellow naturist traveler. Mike demonstrates heavy paternal disappointment and sends the boy home. When the eighteen-year-old leaves, Mike and Patti Anne have a lengthy conversation about boundaries, Mike naming his core worry about the younger girls walking into an open room if she would be involved.
