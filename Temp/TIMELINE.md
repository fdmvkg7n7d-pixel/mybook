## THE SECOND GENERATION (The Children)

- **~2040:** **Samira Chaka Key** and **Sahara Hannan Key** are born to Jessica Isabella (age 16).
- **April 2043:** **Gamaliel Galvin Key** is born to Patti Anne (age 21).
- **~2043:** **Ashley Erin Key (7)** travels to New York City accompanied by matriarch **Terri Anne** and her eight-year-old sister **Andrea Jennifer** to sign with **Valerie Sterling** of *Sterling Management*—an elite talent and modeling agent. Sourcing her deeply grounded calm, Terri Anne verifies that Valerie holds an absolute, unblemished Fiduciary perimeter around her client's genuine self-determination and trans youth representation, beautifully establishing the overarching early-childhood origin of her professional modeling and acting trajectory.
- **November 2043:** **Klára** (born Klára Drahoslav) is born — seven months after Gamaliel, same year.
- **December 25, 2044:** **Pandora Paige Key** and **Brandi Jessica Key** are born to Kiley Rose (age 16).
- **~2051:** **Klára joins the family** as Patti Anne's foster daughter (Gamaliel, 8, saved her life; four months earlier he had told Mike about her father's abuse). **From the start, Patti Anne or Aleksander takes Klára to a temple in Vaughan** to honor her great-grandmothers' faith — **and Gamaliel asks to come along from the first visit, to support her as she becomes a member of his family.** The temple's rabbi: Avram Loewy.
- **December 23, 2051:** **Brandi Jessica** is diagnosed with bilateral retinoblastoma at age 7. Pandora is screened and saved.
- **2053:** **Carlota Delicia Key** is born to Sabrina Bryna Key (age 23) and Carlos Reyes (age 24).
- **June 2056:** **Palmer Peter Key** and **Salena Sarai Key** are born to Patti Anne (age 34).
- **Late 2059 (November–December):** **Mike Key dies.** He suffers a massive myocardial infarction in his study at the Vaughan mansion. Terri Anne performs CPR; Patti Anne delivers the news to the sisters in birth order, going in person to sign to Sabrina Bryna. Gamaliel and Klára are both 16. **Rabbi Loewy attends the funeral Mass** (back row); on the church steps: *"May his memory be a blessing"* / *"Thursday still stands."* That night, over the melted banana split, Gamaliel says *zikhrono livrakha* as his own for the first time. At the celebration of life, six-year-old **Carlota Delicia** sits on Sabrina's lap and signs *family* to Gamaliel and Klára after their Pas de Deux — one of her last fluent signs before her kidnapping.
- **June 2061:** Gamaliel and Klára graduate from Victoria Ballet Academy — Gamaliel 18 (April birthday), Klára 17 (she turns 18 that November, when she also ages out of foster care).
- **October 2062:** Klára (18) visits her father at Kent Institution, BC; three recovery days in Vancouver with Gamaliel (19); **farewell week in Vaughan** (Dr. Hartwell's and Rabbi Loewy's letters given to Klára; Kabbalat Shabbat at the temple, then the farewell barbecue as Se'udat Shabbat).
- **Late October 2062:** **Gamaliel and Klára's Orlando Visit** — eight days with Terri Anne at Cypress Cove (the naturist community she moved to after Mike's death). They visit Discovery Cove and Walt Disney World (establishing their future academy's trip traditions: **an annual, fixed Disneyland Paris trip for every grade-eight class**, and **a city chosen by each grade-twelve class**). They ask to name their future Prague academy for Mike Key; Terri Anne talks them out of it (*a ballet academy needs a hip name* — it becomes **Pražská Baletní Akademie**) and gives them the theater instead: the **Mike Key Theatre**.
- **November 2062:** **Gamaliel and Klára move to Prague permanently** (no university for either). The **$25,000,000 Key Legacy Arts Trust** grant (matriarchal capital from Terri Anne out of Mike's estate, held in trust since graduation) is fully deployed to acquire the prime real estate for Pražská Baletní Akademie. They open their storefront studio and hire a highly skilled business manager. They rebuild their two rooms in the new city under Dr. Tereza Dvořáková and Rabbi Eliáš Löwy.
- **~2062–2063:** Gamaliel's formal conversion to Judaism completes in Prague under Rabbi Eliáš Löwy (beit din, mikveh) — begun under Rabbi Avram Loewy in Vaughan at thirteen.
- **Spring 2062:** **Carlota Delicia (9) is kidnapped.** Carlos Reyes takes her from Toronto under the pretense of a father-daughter trip to Mexico and never returns. She is held and exploited at a sleazy bar in Tijuana for forty-two months (3.5 years).
- **Autumn 2065:** **Carlota Delicia (12.5) is rescued.** An OPP/RCMP cross-border operation—coordinated by Patti Anne utilizing the direct law-enforcement channels Mike established—successfully locates and recovers Carlota from Tijuana. Within weeks of returning to Toronto, her SickKids medical workup reveals her **HIV-positive status**. Managed flawlessly on antiretroviral therapy (ART) coordinated by William, her viral load becomes completely undetectable.
- **~2074:** **Palmer Peter and Salena Sarai move out** at age 18.
    - Palmer Peter moves to Saudi Arabia.
    - Salena Sarai moves to Perth, Australia.
    - Gamaliel is 31 years old.
- **~2075:** Palmer Peter (19) decides he wants to be Muslim, about a year into his Saudi life. He officially converts in ~2076 (age 20) and begins his plural marriages/partnerships in ~2078.

## KEY SAGA ANCHORS & RITUALS

- **~2035:** Kiley Rose (7) and Jessica Isabella's kitchen accident.
- **~2040–2041:** The Parallel Domestic Evening at the Carlisle mansion. While Terri Anne helps Fallon Glenda (7) and Sabrina Bryna (9)—whose Cerebral Palsy is beautifully accommodated by her custom ergonomic desk chair—with their math schoolwork in the study (using ASL for Sabrina), Mike works with Andrea Jennifer (6) and Ashley Erin (5) after their bath together. In their bedroom, on Ashley Erin's bed, with their long strawberry blonde hair wet and freshly brushed out because Mike helped them shampoo it, Mike initiates his foot-tickling game. Wearing matching pink pyjamas bottoms (with their little feet bare), the game establishes the unbreakable safeword (*"Stop, Daddy"*) and the wide, five-minute unhunted buffer before they voluntarily climb into his lap to be read a bedtime story and tucked into their bed, demonstrating the flawless parental teamwork holding the sprawling Carlisle estate together.
- **Summer 2041 (or Summer 2042):** The Two-Month European Migration. Because all eight daughters are actively homeschooled by Mike and Terri Anne, their learning is continuous and does not stop for institutional terms. Before leaving, Mike and Terri Anne sit their eight homeschooled daughters down at the Carlisle kitchen table to transparently explain their itinerary. In July (Phase 1), their homeschool relocates to Paris, France, for a month of cultural immersion, historical D-Day pilgrimages in Normandy (Juno and Omaha Beach), magical VIP days at Disneyland Paris, and an extraordinary parents' date night at *Le Jules Verne* in the Eiffel Tower while eighteen-year-old Patti Anne steps up to babysit the household. In August (Phase 2), they migrate down to Cap d'Agde, France, for their first naturist vacation sanctuary. Reaching the premier European naturist village on the Mediterranean, they go swimming daily in the Mediterranean Sea and swimming pools, establishing an unblemished emotional room for Jessica Isabella (15), and accommodating Sabrina Bryna's Cerebral Palsy with an all-terrain beach wheelchair and Mike's personal core-support in the surf. On the first Saturday in August, before the late-night catch scene, the younger daughters are entered into Cap d'Agde's Beauty Junior Contest. On the morning of the contest, Terri Anne and her daughters share a lengthy conversation around the dining room table where Kiley Rose, Sabrina Bryna, and Fallon Glenda make an absolute inter-sibling pact to not participate if Ashley Erin wouldn't be allowed; Terri Anne completely validates their solidarity. On stage, the contest is a highly cherished summer tradition of natural self-expression where little Ashley Erin (5) shines and where Mike personally booms an absolute defense against two transphobic outside families who complain she is *"like a boy outside"*. Phase 2 culminates late one night in Cap d'Agde when Mike walks into their naturist compound finding eighteen-year-old Patti Anne involved with this eighteen-year-old fellow naturist traveler. Mike demonstrates heavy paternal disappointment and sends the boy home. When the eighteen-year-old leaves, Mike and Patti Anne have a lengthy conversation about boundaries, Mike naming his core worry about the younger girls walking into an open room if she would be involved. Eighteen-year-old Patti Anne promises that she will try her behavior better, but intimately discloses that she sometimes hears voices in her head, asking Mike if this is normal. Mike behaves with profound love and care toward how Patti Anne feels, receiving her disclosure with absolute steadiness and promising that when they return home to Carlisle, he personally will take her to their family doctor to secure the exact room and tools she deserves. The entire two-month campaign sows the exact matriarchal seed for Terri Anne's later adult move to Cypress Cove in Orlando, Florida.

- **~2055:** Jessica Isabella and Emma conceive identical twin sons, **Eliot David and Kalvin LaMarcus**, after five months of a structured donor arrangement with their colleague Rémi.
