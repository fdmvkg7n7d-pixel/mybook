# Sabrina Bryna and William Visit Orin and Danica During March Break — Scene Outline

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Timing:** Friday, March 16, 2077 (Case 1 Chronology — traveling on the Friday before Ontario March Break).
  - *Context:* Because Quebec's March Break is two weeks earlier, Danica is attending her regular classes as a day student at Bishop's College School (BCS). McGill University does not have a March Break, meaning Orin has a full Friday schedule of pre-med lectures and organic chemistry labs in Montreal. Both Orin and Danica have just returned to Ferme Clé from their school days when the family arrives.
- **Location:** Ferme Clé (the horse farm located between Montreal and Sherbrooke, Quebec).
- **Core Characters:**
  - **Orin Lorin Junior** — 19 years old, second-year McGill pre-med student.
  - **Danica** — 17 years old (having turned seventeen on Valentine's Day). She is finishing her BCS autumn-semester classes under youth protection (DPJ) supervision.
  - **Sabrina Bryna Key** — 43 years old. Orin's adoptive mother; she **runs Key Corporation** and communicates primarily in ASL; she has Cerebral Palsy, but **she does not need anything to help her walk** and possesses complete physical independence.
  - **William** — Sabrina's husband and Orin's blood uncle; premier **child ophthalmologist** at SickKids, calm, deeply paternal.
- **Sibling Presence (Traveling Group from Vaughan):**
  - **Taylor Raven Key** — 14 years old.
  - **Sun-Young "Liz" Key** — 14 years old.
  - **Roberta Katherine Key** — 8 years old (twin of William Jr.).
  - **William Timothy Junior Key** — 8 years old (twin of Roberta).
- **Explicitly Absent Sibling:**
  - **Carlota Delicia Key** — 23 years old. Carlota does **not** live with Sabrina Bryna and William at the Vaughan mansion. She lives in Orlando, Florida, with her partner Keith Holiday and their nine-month-old baby Madeleine Jaycee Holiday Key, while attending UCF's Rosen College of Hospitality Management. Her absence from this trip is standard and expected, as she has established her own independent adult life in Florida.

---

## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene coordinates the high-stakes moment where Orin's secret relationship with Danica is finally revealed to his parents. Sabrina Bryna (who runs Key Corporation) and William (a child ophthalmologist) arrive believing Orin is alone at Ferme Clé after having spent Christmas "helping a grieving friend whose parents died." This scene outlines the choreography of their arrival, Orin's foyer confession, their introduction to Danica in the kitchen, and the family's ultimate commitment to protecting her safety and legal welfare.

## SCENE ARC & BEATS

### Beat 1: The Kitchen Refuge
- Orin and Danica return to Ferme Clé in the late afternoon from McGill and BCS. The tension in the house is palpable. Sabrina Bryna and William are arriving within the hour, and they still believe Orin's Christmas cover story—that he was merely supporting a grieving friend whose parents died. They have no idea he is in a serious relationship, let alone that his girlfriend has been living at the farm for three months.
- Orin makes the executive decision that the secrecy must end today. He wants to protect Danica from the sudden shock of a family invasion, and he needs to explain the situation to his parents privately first.
- He has Danica wait in the kitchen, out of sight from the entrance, while he prepares to receive his parents in the mansion's grand foyer. 
- In the kitchen, Danica nervously adjusts her clothes and practices her fingerspelled ASL, her heart hammering against her ribs as she waits behind the kitchen island.

### Beat 2: The Limousine Arrival & Sibling Stampede
- The family has traveled up from Vaughan, flying from **Toronto Pearson International Airport (YYZ)** to **Montreal-Trudeau International Airport (YUL)** on **one of Key Corporation's private planes**, before being driven to the farm in the corporate limousine.
- The massive Key Corporation **limousine** pulls up the gravel driveway of Ferme Clé and **stops at the fork in the driveway**.
- The moment the passenger doors of the limousine open at the fork—**without the driver needing to get out of the vehicle**—the four younger children (Taylor, Liz, Roberta, and William Jr.) do not even look at the house. Breathless with excitement, they **jump out of the limousine at the fork and run straight toward the barn** to see the horses, their voices fading into the snowy paddocks.
- With the children safely en route to the stables, the limousine continues up the remaining stretch of the driveway and pulls up directly in front of the mansion's steps. 
- This structured, quiet arrival clears the stage, leaving Sabrina Bryna and William to walk up the steps alone. Sabrina, demonstrating her characteristic physical independence, **does not need anything to help her walk**—she climbs the stairs on her own two feet without forearm crutches, canes, or any other assistive devices. William walks beside her in quiet peace, and they enter the mansion's front doors.

### Beat 3: The Boots in the Foyer & The Confession
- Orin meets Sabrina Bryna and William in the mansion's foyer. He welcomes them with warm hugs, but his posture is stiff, his face carrying a solemn weight.
- As they step further into the foyer, **Sabrina Bryna and William immediately notice a pair of small winter boots** sitting neatly near the doorway. These are clearly Danica's boots (the only boots she has, which she walked off her parents' porch in). The physical clue immediately catches their attention and tips them off that someone else is living at the farm before Orin even begins to speak.
- Before they can unpack their bags or settle in, Orin stops them in the middle of the foyer. He looks at his mother and signs with quiet, deliberate gravity, while speaking aloud for William: *I need to tell you the truth. There is no grieving friend.*
- Orin confesses the entire three-month-old secret:
  - He has a girlfriend named Danica, who is 17 and attends BCS.
  - Before Christmas, her parents made horrific racist comments to him in their living room.
  - Danica made the radical decision to walk out of her home immediately with nothing but the clothes on her back.
  - Because she left without her passport, she could not fly to Orlando for Christmas, and Orin could not leave her alone.
  - To protect her privacy, he made up the "friend" cover story, choosing to stay with her at Ferme Clé. While she initially stayed in the guest suite, **since Valentine's Day (when they slept together for the first time), they have been sharing a bed**, cementing their serious and committed relationship.
- Sabrina and William stand in the foyer, absorbing the immense emotional weight of their son's confession. Sabrina's maternal eyes immediately read Orin's absolute moral sincerity; she realizes he carried this heavy burden alone to preserve a vulnerable girl's dignity.

### Beat 4: Many Questions & Deep Parental Care
- Before meeting Danica, **Sabrina Bryna and William have many serious, protective questions** for Orin. 
- William (drawing on his analytical, clinical background as a **child ophthalmologist**) and Sabrina Bryna (sourcing her sharp leadership instincts as the woman who **runs Key Corporation**) press Orin on the logistical and safety details of their situation:
  * How has Danica been coping emotionally after leaving her home?
  * How have they been managing her youth protection (DPJ) file and her school attendance?
  * What are her parents doing, and have they attempted to contact or harass her at BCS?
  * How has Orin been balancing his rigorous McGill pre-med studies while supporting her?
- Orin answers every question with steadfast honesty, explaining how they have structured their days, how Gérard has helped with school pickups, and how they have committed to their shared studies on the couch. Sabrina and William are deeply satisfied by his mature, protective responsibility.

### Beat 5: The Introduction in the Kitchen & The Sibling Meet
- Having resolved their questions, Orin leads his parents from the foyer into the kitchen.
- Danica stands waiting behind the kitchen island. Her face is pale, but she steps forward to meet Orin's parents.
- Raising her hands, Danica executes her newly practiced fingerspelling: *D-A-N-I-C-A*, followed by the signs for *nice to meet you*.
- Sabrina Bryna’s initial surprise instantly melts. She steps forward completely independently and pulls Danica into a deep, maternal embrace, signing with rapid, emotional fluid speed to Orin: *She has your quiet, and she has courage. She is our daughter now.*
- Just as they are embracing, the younger children return from the stables to meet Orin and Danica in the kitchen. Sibling introductions are warm, laughing, and immediate, and the younger children instantly accept Danica as their older sister.

### Beat 6: The Indoor Pool & Cozy Pyjamas Dinner
- Sourcing the Key family's long-established, body-positive naturist values (rooted in their Cap d'Agde vacations and Cypress Cove lifestyle), **Sabrina Bryna and William allow the children to swim completely without swimsuits** in the mansion's indoor heated pool. 
- To show absolute solidarity, remove any lingering self-consciousness, and welcome Danica into their deepest circle of trust, **Orin Lorin Junior and Danica also swim without swimsuits**, joining the family in the warm water.
- This shared, natural, and completely non-sexualized experience of vulnerability washes away the last barriers of secrecy and anxiety. Swimming together in the warm pool, Danica experiences an overwhelming wave of safety and absolute, lifelong belonging.
- After their swim, everyone gets dressed into their cozy sleepwear, and **they are just wearing their pyjamas at the massive dining room table** for a comforting, authentic Québécois dinner prepared by Hélène: warm yellow split pea soup (*soupe aux pois*), a rich deep-dish *Tourtière du Lac-Saint-Jean*, and a large platter of fresh poutine with squeaky white curds and dark veal gravy.
- After dinner, **the children gather outside to make s'mores over an outdoor fire pit**, laughing, talking, and enjoying the sweet treat together under the crisp, starry March night sky.

### Beat 7: Legal & Relational Sanctuary (Danica's Apology)
- While the children are making s'mores outside around the fire pit with Orin, Sabrina, William, and Danica gather in the study. Sabrina sits in a custom pelvic-stabilizing chair, and William rests his hand heavily on her shoulder, anchoring her CP spasms.
- **Sabrina Bryna and William express many deep concerns about Danica's safety.** They are highly worried about the threat of her racist, hostile parents in Sherbrooke retaliating or attempting to forcibly reclaim her, as well as her legal vulnerability as an unaccompanied minor under Quebec's youth protection (DPJ) jurisdiction.
- During this serious discussion, **Danica quietly voices her deep anxiety and apologizes for having to go to school at Bishop's College School (BCS) on Monday morning while they are still visiting at the farm.** She feels incredibly bad about being a "bad guest" and neglecting her hosts.
- **Sabrina Bryna signs and William speaks to instantly reassure her, letting her know that her education is her absolute priority, they are incredibly proud of her academic dedication, and they want her to focus entirely on her studies.**
- Sourcing her massive executive authority as the leader who **runs Key Corporation**, Sabrina signs with absolute steel: *We do not let anyone threaten our children's safety. Danica is ours now. Your school is your primary job, and we will protect your right to do it.*
- William explains that Key Corporation's senior legal counsel in Montreal will assume representation for Danica's DPJ and emancipation filings (*art. 168 C.c.Q.*) on Monday morning. Her tuition at BCS is fully funded, and her residence at Ferme Clé is secure.
- The scene ends with Sabrina, William, and Danica standing by the floor-to-ceiling living room window, watching the children happily roasting marshmallows over the outdoor fire pit with Orin. Sabrina loops her arm through Danica's, physically welcoming her into the family's unbreakable circle of love.
