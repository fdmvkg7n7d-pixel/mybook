## ORIGIN — HOW SHE COMES TO THE KEY FAMILY

Sun-Young's entry into the Key family is entirely due to **Taylor Raven**. 

Sun-Young was a patient at **Toronto Sick Children's Hospital (SickKids)**, where William works. She was being treated for **Leukemia** as a child. At the time, she was a ward of the state with no family visitors. Five-year-old Taylor Raven snuck onto Sun-Young's inpatient floor and befriended her. Taylor was struck by Sun-Young's loneliness and told William that Sun-Young "didn't have a flamingo" (Taylor's way of saying she lacked a family and the joy that came with it).

Moved by his daughter's bond with the girl and Sun-Young's isolated situation, William and Sabrina Bryna began the process of becoming her foster parents. Sun-Young, later nicknamed **Liz**, was eventually integrated into the family as their **fifth child**. When Sun-Young is **sixteen years old**, the adoption papers come through; Sabrina Bryna and William give her the papers on **Christmas Day in Sao Paulo**, and she becomes very emotional when she sees them. Orin Lorin Junior (21) and Danica (18) accompany them to Sao Paulo. Adelisa, Carlota Delicia, Keith, Madeleine Jaycee, and Caesarius are with them. Adelisa is Keith's stepsister, Caesarius is Adelisa's son and is deaf too, and Adelisa and Caesarius live with Carlota Delicia and Keith because Adelisa has Juvenile Arthritis. Sabrina Bryna and William ask Sun-Young if she noticed that they did not give the Canadian Border Service Agency a letter from her worker to cross the border, and Sun-Young tells them she did not notice. After opening presents, William and Keith take the children swimming in the Atlantic Ocean; the children can be shirtless while swimming. While they swim, Carlota Delicia, Sabrina Bryna, and Adelisa prepare Christmas dinner. From that point forward, she is their adopted daughter, while her foster-care history remains part of her origin. See `sun-young-adoption-papers-christmas-age-sixteen-sao-paulo-scene-outline.md`.

## KEY FAMILY EVENTS

### South Korea Trip with William — Age Ten

When Taylor Raven and Sun-Young are **ten years old**, **William takes them to South Korea**. For Sun-Young, the trip is a Korean-heritage homecoming; for Taylor Raven, it is a sister trip and a chance to see the country connected to Liz. They travel with William and use Sabrina Bryna's Seoul apartment as a home base during the Seoul legs. During this trip, **Sun-Young finds a lump / swollen lymph node**, creating a frightening relapse-scare thread because she is a **Leukemia survivor**. They are in the girls' bedroom at Sabrina's Seoul apartment. William asks Sun-Young if he can check the lump. Taylor Raven is in the room and holds Liz's right hand during the check. The moment is **very emotional**: Sun-Young is frightened because of her Leukemia history and very nervous that Sabrina Bryna and William will not want to be her foster parents anymore. Taylor Raven is protective and tearful, and William keeps his voice gentle while taking the discovery seriously. After the check, **William hugs Sun-Young to calm her down** while Taylor keeps holding Liz's right hand. William reassures Sun-Young that he and Sabrina Bryna still want her to be their foster daughter because they love her so much. William quickly calls one of his colleagues on the **twentieth day of the South Korea trip** and arranges medical follow-up rather than dismissing it. That night at the mansion, **Devon Smythson has her own bedroom at the mansion and does not need to leave**; **Taylor Raven and Sun-Young sleep together for comfort** after the emotional medical scare. The next morning at Sabrina Bryna and William's Vaughan mansion, **Devon Smythson watches the twins** while Sabrina Bryna and William take Taylor Raven and Sun-Young to **SickKids / Toronto Sick Children's Hospital** for follow-up. At SickKids, the ultrasound looks bad, and William and his colleague have a conversation outside the examination room. William tells the family that Sun-Young is required to have surgery sooner than later; his colleague is checking the schedule. See `../../Temp/Vacations/south-korea-trip-william-taylor-liz.md` and `sun-young-hospital-follow-up-after-south-korea-lump-next-morning-scene-outline.md`.
