## BEAT 2 — THE WELCOME AND THE GRAND TOUR WITH THE STUDIO PRESIDENT

*Danica steps into the Key Porn Studios foyer on Saturday morning. Because Sabrina Bryna is not at the facility this weekend, the President of Key Porn Studios personally welcomes Danica and gives her an extensive tour of the building.*

Sabrina Bryna is not there today; instead, **the President of Key Porn Studios** is waiting personally at the entrance to welcome Danica. The President greets her warmly, expressing how delighted the entire executive team is to have her join as their weekend front desk receptionist.
- **Tour of the Twenty Studios:** Before taking her behind the reception console, the President leads Danica on a comprehensive tour of the massive Montreal facility. The President explains and shows Danica that the building houses **twenty different studios and shooting stages under one roof**—each one acoustically isolated, state-of-the-art, and custom-designed for different production setups, lighting rigs, and artistic aesthetics.
- **The Wall Photographs (Fully Dressed Porn Stars):** As they walk through the wide central corridors connecting the twenty soundstages, Danica observes that the walls are lined with hundreds of beautifully framed portraits of Key Porn Studios' adult film performers. However, Danica notices something striking: **every single porn star in the wall photographs is completely and elegantly dressed**—wearing formal evening gowns, tailored suits, casual designer wear, and dignified attire.
- **Danica's Question about the Photographs:** Curious about the artistic choice, Danica politely asks the President a question about the hallway portraits: *Why are all the adult film performers in the wall photographs fully dressed instead of in costume or undressed?*
- **The President's Explanation — The Daycare Facility & Holiday Parties for Children:** The President smiles with deep pride and opens a pair of double doors leading into **the studio's on-site Daycare Facility**—a bright, secure, fully staffed children's center filled with books, educational games, and children of performers and production crew members. The President explains the studio's foundational family-first philosophy:
  - **Protecting the Children:** Because performers, administrative employees, and technical crew bring their children to the studio daycare every single day, children walk through the main corridors daily. Therefore, the President explains, **it would be completely inappropriate to have undressed women or explicit imagery displayed on the walls**.
  - **Children's Holiday Parties:** Furthermore, every **Christmas, Halloween, and Easter**, Key Porn Studios transforms its foyers and community spaces into magical, joyful holiday parties specifically for the employees' and performers' children—complete with Santa Claus, costume parades, and Easter egg hunts.
  - **Honoring Parental Dignity:** Displaying fully dressed, exquisite portraits ensures that when performers' children walk down the hallway to daycare or holiday parties, they see their parents honored with total dignity and pride in a safe, family-friendly environment [Note 5]. Danica is deeply moved by this profound care and respect for family life.
- **Notice of Mike Key's Photograph & Danica's Question:** Continuing down the main corridor near the executive wing, Danica notices a prominent, beautifully framed **photograph of Mike Key** hanging on the wall. Curious about the man featured so prominently in the hallway, Danica turns to the President and asks simply: *"Who is the man in that photograph and what is his history with the studio?"*
- **The President's Explanation of Mike Key:** The President's voice fills with immense reverence and respect [Note 4]. The President explains to Danica that **Mike Key** (`Mike Key.md`) is the visionary billionaire founder/owner of Key Porn Studios and the father of studio executive Sabrina Bryna Key. The President tells Danica about Mike Key's uncompromising belief in bodily autonomy, parental dignity, and the protection of children [Note 5]. The President explains that Mike Key personally demanded and established the on-site daycare facility and the children's holiday traditions (`Christmas, Halloween, and Easter`), insisting that every performer at Key Porn Studios be treated not as a commodity, but as a multifaceted artist, parent, and human being.
