# Danica’s Barn and Staff Tour — Scene Outline

## Placement

**When:** Saturday afternoon, after returning from shopping in Montreal.  
**Location:** Sabrina Bryna and William’s horse and rabbit farm.  
**Focus:** Orin Lorin Junior giving Danica a tour of the barn and introducing her to the staff.

---

## Characters

- **Danica (Xochitl)** — 16 years old.
- **Orin Lorin Junior** — 19 years old.
- **Barn Staff** — Several workers who care for the horses and rabbits.
- **House Staff** — A few people who help maintain the mansion.

---

## Scene Outline

### Beat 1: After Returning from Montreal

After returning from their shopping trip in Montreal, Orin and Danica unload the bags from the car and bring them into the house. Danica is now wearing her new blue jeans and red sweater.

Once everything is inside, Orin turns to her.

> “Would you like to see the rest of the farm? I can show you the barn and introduce you to everyone.”

Danica nods.

> “Yes, please.”

### Beat 2: The Barn Tour

Orin takes Danica to the large barn. Inside, several horses are in their stalls. The barn is clean and well-organized. Orin explains how the farm operates and shows her the different areas — the horse stalls, the feed room, the tack room, and the rabbit hutches outside.

Danica listens quietly, clearly interested.

When they reach the rabbit hutches, Orin shows her the baby bunnies. Danica gently touches their long, soft fur and smiles.

> “They’re so soft,” she says quietly.

Orin nods.

> “Sabrina Bryna and William raise long-haired rabbits. They sell the fur to make wool.”

### Beat 3: Meeting the Barn Staff

Orin introduces Danica to the barn staff. He tells them her name and explains that she will be staying at the farm for a while. The workers are polite and welcoming. Some of them shake her hand or nod respectfully.

Danica smiles shyly but greets everyone.

### Beat 4: Meeting the House Staff

After the barn, Orin brings Danica back to the mansion and introduces her to the house staff. He shows her the kitchen, the laundry area, and the main living spaces while explaining who works where.

The house staff are also polite and professional. One of the older staff members smiles warmly at Danica and says:

> “It’s nice to have another young face around here.”

### Beat 5: Returning to the House

After the tour, Orin and Danica walk back toward the main house. Danica looks around at the property with a calmer expression than she had the night before.

> “It’s really beautiful here,” she says quietly.

Orin nods.

> “It’s home.”

---

## Emotional Notes

- This scene helps Danica feel more grounded and included in her new environment.
- Orin’s introductions show that he is proud to have her there.
- The tour marks another step in Danica becoming part of life at the farm.
