## ORIGIN — THE HOUSE FIRE

Orin Lorin Junior is the son of **William's brother** (name TBD). His life was upended when his parents died in a **devastating house fire**. Orphaned and grieving, he was taken in by his uncle William and Sabrina Bryna. Despite his trauma, he immediately became the eldest sibling in the household.

## FAMILY CONTEXT & MEETING DANICA

Orin Lorin Junior lives in the Vaughan mansion as part of a large blended household. He is William's nephew by blood but is raised as a son. He shares the home with his four younger siblings (Taylor Raven, Sun-Young "Liz", and twins Roberta Katherine and William Timothy Junior) and his half-sister-by-adoption Carlota Delicia. Ten years ago, Sabrina Bryna and William bought Ferme Clé, a sprawling horse farm between Montreal and Sherbrooke, as an investment property; Orin is given the opportunity to live at this farm as his personal sanctuary in Quebec.

Nineteen-year-old Orin is attending **McGill University** in Montreal. In the middle of November, while walking through the Montreal Underground City (RÉSO) wearing his favorite crimson McGill University hoodie, he spots sixteen-year-old **Danica** eating french fries near the entrance of the food court and finds her deeply interesting. Danica, whose family is from Japan, attends **Bishop's College School** as a day student; her mother has driven her into Montreal for early Christmas shopping and to meet her BCS girlfriends, Geneviève and Chloe. Orin approaches her table and strikes up a polite conversation in English without speaking Japanese. Their immediate connection turns playfully romantic when Orin charmingly steals some french fries from Danica's tray during their chat. 

Following this chance encounter in mid-November, they establish a dedicated routine of calling each other every weekend. They do not see each other again in person until the weekend before Christmas Day, when Danica invites Orin over to her family home in Sherbrooke for dinner to meet her parents before he departs for Orlando to spend the holidays with his family. As they gather in the living room before dinner, Danica's parents, who harbor intense racial prejudice against Black people and assume Orin only speaks English, utter four nasty, disparaging sentences in Japanese. While Danica isn't fully fluent in Japanese, she understands enough to catch the cruel meaning of what they are saying to her boyfriend and becomes incredibly emotional. Orin maintains his quiet, dignified composure; turning to her parents, he informs them in flawless Japanese that he understood every word because he spent four years studying Japanese in high school. Orin Lorin Junior lets Danica's parents know exactly where he has been living for sixteen months (at Ferme Clé) because he doesn't want them to think racist thoughts about him. However, they remain uncompromising and say another thing that is racist. Overwhelmed with emotion and mortified by her parents' behavior, Danica makes the radical decision to leave with Orin Lorin Junior instead of living in her parents' home. She doesn't pack anything; walking out together into the winter night exactly as she is (see [orin-danica-how-they-meet-scene-outline.md](orin-danica-how-they-meet-scene-outline.md)). 

Orin drives Danica directly back to Ferme Clé. When they arrive at the farm, Orin Lorin Junior gives Danica a grand tour of the mansion. When they get to his bedroom during the tour, Danica, overwhelmed by the evening's intense emotion, starts kissing him very passionately. However, Orin gently pushes her away and explains to her that he would take advantage of her if they sleep together right now, recognizing her intense emotional vulnerability after leaving her home without any belongings. Because the guest bedroom doesn't have anything for her to sleep in, Orin literally gives her the shirt off his back to sleep in, along with his housecoat. Danica accepts his care, but chooses to leave her ivory tights on to sleep in with his shirt. The next morning at exactly seven o'clock, two policemen and a policewoman from the Sûreté du Québec—Officer Marc Bouchard, Officer Yves Tremblay, and Officer Chantal Roy—knock on the front door, dispatched by Danica's furious parents. The police insist on interviewing Orin Lorin Junior and Danica separately, ensuring they don't match their stories together. Orin faces the inquiry with his characteristic steadfast dignity downstairs, while upstairs, Danica shows Officer Roy the separate bedrooms they used, providing undeniable physical proof of Orin's honorable conduct and clear boundaries. Satisfied, Officer Roy gives Danica her business card before the police depart (see [ferme-cle-mansion-tour-scene-outline.md](ferme-cle-mansion-tour-scene-outline.md)). 

Following the inquiry, Orin asks Danica what she wants for breakfast; she requests bagels with cream cheese, and Orin drives out to St. Viateur Bagels to provide the iconic Montreal tradition. Because she left without packing anything, Danica can't travel out of Canada without a passport. Facing the prospect of flying to Florida alone, Orin Lorin Junior decides to stay with Danica, and she decides to stay with Orin Lorin Junior at Ferme Clé. Meanwhile, Sabrina Bryna and William are still in Vaughan, Ontario, preparing to fly over to Montreal in the Key Corporation plane to pick Orin up before continuing to Florida. On Saturday, Orin calls Sabrina Bryna and William in Vaughan to let them know he is staying in Quebec; he makes the excuse that a friend needs him because his friend's parents died. Because Orin was orphaned by a house fire at age sixteen, his adoptive parents completely understand the sacred weight of this duty and give him their full blessing to stay, cancelling the Montreal stop for the Key Corporation plane. That afternoon, Orin gives Danica a tour of the barn to meet the workers. While she begins in the guest suite, in time, she will move her stuff into Orin Lorin Junior's bedroom, cementing Ferme Clé as their unshakeable permanent home (see [ferme-cle-bagels-and-barn-tour-scene-outline.md](ferme-cle-bagels-and-barn-tour-scene-outline.md)).

## RELATIONSHIPS

- **Sabrina Bryna Key (Adoptive Mother):** Deaf. Communicates in ASL. Orin forms a powerful, tactile bond with her through his dedication to mastering sign language.
- **William (Uncle / Adoptive Father):** His biological uncle; the "anchor" who brought him into the family after the fire.
- **Danica (Wife / Partner):** Attends Bishop's College School; Orin's ultimate source of stillness and emotional sanctuary. Her steadfast loyalty to Orin is cemented when she stands by him against her parents' prejudice during their pre-Christmas gathering in the living room. When she leaves immediately with him without packing anything rather than remaining in her parents' home, Orin drives her to Ferme Clé and gives her a grand tour of the mansion. When she passionately kisses him in his bedroom, Orin gently pushes her away, explaining he would be taking advantage of her if they slept together then. Because the guest room has no clothes, he gives her the shirt off his back and his housecoat to sleep in, which she wears alongside her ivory tights. Following a 7:00 AM police inquiry by Officers Bouchard, Tremblay, and Roy—where the police interview them separately so they won't match stories, Danica shows Officer Roy the separate bedrooms they used, and Roy leaves her business card—Orin picks up St. Viateur bagels with cream cheese for breakfast. Left without a passport to leave Canada, Orin chooses to stay behind with her at Ferme Clé rather than traveling to Orlando. He calls Sabrina Bryna and William, who are still in Vaughan preparing to pick him up in Montreal in the Key Corporation plane, making the excuse that a friend needs him because his friend's parents died. After a Saturday barn tour to meet the workers, Danica establishes her residence at the farm; in time, she moves her stuff permanently into Orin Lorin Junior's bedroom.
- **Taylor Raven and Sun-Young "Liz":** His sisters, both twelve years old when Orin is nineteen. During Orin's university studies in Montreal, Sun-Young (originally from South Korea) is not in the picture because she is living full-time with Sabrina Bryna and William in Vaughan, Ontario. Orin was scheduled to join them in Orlando for Christmas but chooses to stay with Danica at Ferme Clé.
- **Roberta Katherine and William Timothy Junior (Siblings):** Younger twins in the household; he occupies a protective, older-brother role for them.
