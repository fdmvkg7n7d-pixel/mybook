# Danica Arrives at the Farm — Scene Outline

## Placement

**When:** Friday evening before Christmas Day.  
**Location:** Sabrina Bryna and William’s horse and rabbit farm.  
**Focus:** Orin Lorin Junior bringing Danica to the farm and their first evening together.

---

## Characters

- **Danica (Xochitl)** — 16 years old.
- **Orin Lorin Junior** — 19 years old. Lives and works at the farm.

---

## Scene Outline

### Beat 1: The Drive

After leaving Danica’s house, Orin Lorin Junior drives her to the farm in silence. Danica sits in the passenger seat, still crying quietly. Orin keeps one hand on the steering wheel and occasionally glances at her with concern.

The drive takes about forty minutes.

### Beat 2: Arrival at the Farm

Orin turns onto a long gravel driveway. It is eight o’clock in the evening, so all the horses are already inside the barn for the night. Rabbit hutches are visible near one of the smaller buildings.

The main house is modest but well-kept. Orin parks the car in front of it.

### Beat 3: Tour of the Mansion and Tea

Orin turns off the engine and looks at Danica.

> “This is where I live,” he says gently. “It’s an investment property owned by my aunt and uncle — Sabrina Bryna and William.”

He leads her inside and gives her a quiet tour of the mansion. After the tour, Orin prepares a warm cup of **hojicha** (Japanese roasted green tea) and places it in front of her at the kitchen table.

> “Drink this,” he says softly.

### Beat 4: Supper and the Phone Call

While Orin is in the middle of cooking the miso soup, he wipes his hands and takes his iPhone out of his pocket. He hands it to Danica.

> “Here,” he says gently. “You don’t have anything from your house. You should call Geneviève and Chloe. Let them know you’re okay.”

Danica looks at the phone for a moment, then nods. She steps into the hallway to make the call while Orin continues preparing their supper.

After the call, she returns to the kitchen and sits at the table. Orin finishes making **oyakodon** (chicken and egg rice bowl) with miso soup on the side. The meal is modest but warm and comforting.

They eat together. Danica eats slowly, still visibly shaken. Orin watches her with quiet concern but doesn’t push her to talk.

### Beat 5: The Kiss

After they finish eating, Danica suddenly stands up and walks over to Orin. Without warning, she kisses him very passionately, her hands gripping his shirt. She is clearly overwhelmed and seeking comfort.

Orin gently places his hands on her shoulders and slowly pulls away.

> “Danica… not tonight,” he says softly. “You’re emotional and upset. I don’t want to take advantage of you like this.”

Danica looks at him, breathing heavily, her eyes still wet with tears. She doesn’t argue. She simply nods and lowers her head.

Orin pulls her into a gentle hug instead.

> “I’m here,” he whispers. “But not like that. Not tonight.”

### Beat 6: Danica’s First Night

After the hug, Orin shows Danica to a spare bedroom. He sets her bag down and stands in the doorway.

He goes to his own bedroom and returns with his housecoat. He hands it to her along with the shirt he took off.

> “Here,” he says quietly. “You can sleep in these tonight.”

Danica takes the shirt and housecoat with both hands. She keeps her ivory tights on and does not take them off for sleeping.

> “If you need anything, I’m right down the hall,” Orin adds.

Danica nods, looking exhausted.

Before he leaves, she speaks softly:

> “Thank you… for bringing me here.”

Orin gives her a small, sad smile.

> “You’re safe now.”

He closes the door gently.

---

## Emotional Notes

- This scene shows Danica’s first steps into a safer environment.
- The farm represents peace and protection after the trauma of the evening.
- Orin’s quiet care is emphasized through small actions (tea, food, giving her space).
