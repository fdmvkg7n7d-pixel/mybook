# Danica Shopping in Montreal — Scene Outline

## Placement

**When:** Saturday morning, after eating the bagels from Saint Viateur.  
**Location:** Montreal Underground City (RÉSO).  
**Focus:** Orin Lorin Junior taking Danica shopping because she left home with nothing.

---

## Characters

- **Danica (Xochitl)** — 16 years old. Has nothing with her.
- **Orin Lorin Junior** — 19 years old. Taking care of her.

---

## Scene Outline

### Beat 1: After Breakfast

After finishing the bagels with cream cheese, Danica and Orin sit quietly at the kitchen table for a moment. Danica looks down at the shirt and housecoat she is wearing.

She speaks softly:

> “I don’t have anything. No clothes, no toothbrush, nothing.”

Orin nods.

> “We’ll go to Montreal. You can get everything you need.”

### Beat 2: The Drive to Montreal

Orin drives Danica into Montreal. The weather is cold but clear. During the drive, Danica remains quiet, still processing everything that happened the night before.

Orin keeps the conversation light, occasionally asking her what she thinks she might need.

### Beat 3: Arriving at the Underground City

They arrive at the Montreal Underground City (RÉSO). Orin parks and they enter through one of the many access points. The vast network of underground shops and walkways stretches out before them.

Danica looks around, slightly overwhelmed.

> “I really need everything,” she says quietly.

Orin gives her a small, reassuring smile.

> “Then let’s get everything.”

### Beat 4: Shopping

Orin stays with Danica as she shops. They move through several stores. She buys basic clothing, toiletries, a suitcase, and a winter coat.

At one point, Danica is still wearing Orin’s shirt and housecoat. Orin approaches a salesperson and politely asks:

> “Can she wear one of the outfits out of the store? She doesn’t have anything else with her.”

The salesperson agrees. Danica chooses **blue jeans and a red sweater**. She changes into the new outfit in the fitting room and leaves the store wearing it. She looks visibly more comfortable.

Orin carries all the shopping bags without complaint. He never rushes her or makes her feel like a burden.

### Beat 5: Returning to the Car

After several hours of shopping, they return to the car with many bags. Danica looks visibly lighter, both physically and emotionally.

As Orin loads the bags into the trunk, Danica walks up to him. She has to get on her tiptoes to reach him. She lightly kisses him on his right cheek.

> “Thank you,” she says softly.

Orin pauses for a moment, then smiles gently.

> “You’re welcome.”

They get into the car and begin the drive back to the farm.

---

## Emotional Notes

- This scene shows Orin’s consistent care and support.
- Danica slowly begins to feel more secure and grounded.
- The shopping trip marks a practical step toward building a new life.
