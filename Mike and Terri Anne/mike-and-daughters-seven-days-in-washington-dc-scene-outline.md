# SCENE OUTLINE: MIKE AND DAUGHTERS — SEVEN DAYS IN WASHINGTON, DC

*Following Terri Anne’s departure to Toronto, Mike Key spends seven full days in Washington, DC with his six daughters: Rachel Darcy (17), Kiley Rose (15), Sabrina Bryna (13), Fallon Glenda (10), Andrea Jennifer (~8.5), and Ashley Erin (7). The trip combines warm family bonding with meaningful educational and cultural experiences, highlighted by a private tour of the White House made possible through Mike’s political connections.*

---

## CANON BACKDROP & PLACEMENT

- **Timing:** Immediately after Terri Anne flies back to Toronto
- **Location:** Washington, DC
- **Characters Present:** Mike Key + the six daughters listed above
- **Tone:** Warm and bonding, with educational and cultural depth
- **Key Highlight:** Private White House tour

---

## SCENE PURPOSE & THEMATIC CORE

This extended stay in Washington, DC allows Mike to create lasting memories with his daughters while exposing them to American history, politics, and culture. The White House visit stands as the centerpiece, underscoring Mike’s influence and the family’s privileged access. The seven days balance structured activities with relaxed, affectionate family time.

---

## SEVEN-DAY DAY-BY-DAY PLAN

### Day 1 — Arrival & National Mall Introduction
Mike and the six daughters settle into their hotel. They spend a relaxed afternoon walking the National Mall. The girls are excited to be together with their father. They visit the Lincoln Memorial at sunset. Mike tells them stories about American history as they sit on the steps. The mood is light and bonding.

### Day 2 — Smithsonian Museums (Natural History & Air and Space)
They spend the full day exploring two major Smithsonian museums. Rachel Darcy and Kiley Rose are particularly engaged at the Air and Space Museum, while the younger girls enjoy the dinosaurs and gems at the Museum of Natural History. Mike encourages each daughter to choose one exhibit that interests her most. They end the day with ice cream on the Mall.

### Day 3 — The White House (Private Tour)
This is the highlight of the trip. Thanks to Mike’s political connections, the family receives a private, after-hours tour of the White House. They visit the East Room, the State Dining Room, and the Blue Room. The tour guide explains the history of each space. Mike watches his daughters with quiet pride as they absorb the experience. Rachel Darcy is especially thoughtful during the visit. After the tour, Mike takes them to a nice dinner nearby to celebrate.

### Day 4 — Monuments & Memorials Day
They visit the Jefferson Memorial, the Martin Luther King Jr. Memorial, and the Vietnam Veterans Memorial. Mike speaks gently with his daughters about the meaning behind each monument. Fallon Glenda asks many questions. Andrea Jennifer and Ashley Erin stay close to their father. The day feels reflective and meaningful.

### Day 5 — Georgetown & Potomac River
A lighter, more relaxed day. They explore the historic Georgetown neighborhood, walk along the Potomac River, and enjoy lunch at a waterfront restaurant. The older girls enjoy the shops, while the younger ones are happy just being with their father and sisters. Mike rents a small boat for a short ride on the river in the afternoon.

### Day 6 — Library of Congress & Capitol Area
They visit the Library of Congress, one of the most beautiful buildings in Washington. Mike arranges a special viewing of the Main Reading Room. Later, they walk near the Capitol. Mike talks with Rachel Darcy about the importance of education and public service. The conversation is serious but warm.

### Day 7 — National Zoo & Farewell Evening
On their final day, they visit the Smithsonian’s National Zoo. The girls are excited to see the animals. Afterward, Mike takes them to a special farewell dinner. He gives each daughter a small gift from the trip and thanks them for spending the week with him. The evening ends with the family walking back to the hotel together under the city lights.

Mike leans down and says quietly to Rachel Darcy:

> “Not many people get to see this. I want you girls to remember that access is a responsibility.”

Rachel Darcy nods thoughtfully.

Ashley Erin looks up at the statue and whispers:

**On the final evening (Day 7):**
Mike gathers his daughters and says:

> “Your mother and I are very proud of all of you. These days together matter more than you know.”
