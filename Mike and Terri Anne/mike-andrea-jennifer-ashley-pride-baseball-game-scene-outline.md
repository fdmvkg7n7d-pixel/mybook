# SCENE OUTLINE: MIKE TAKES ANDREA JENNIFER AND ASHLEY ERIN TO THE TORONTO BLUE JAYS PRIDE DAY GAME (EXTENDED WEEKEND)

*This scene captures a rare, joyful father-daughter outing during Pride Month. It highlights Mike’s role as a present, inclusive patriarch and creates a cherished “first” memory for the two youngest daughters. The weekend includes the Pride Day game, meeting players, running the bases, and a full Toronto adventure — with a last-minute change on Monday.*

---

## CANON BACKDROP & HEART HISTORY

* **Mike as the Family Anchor:** Despite his demanding work as an OPP consultant and his global business empire, Mike makes deliberate time for his younger daughters. He has a soft spot for Andrea Jennifer (9) and Ashley Erin (8), often taking them on special outings to create normal childhood memories amid the family’s complex dynamics.
* **The Girls’ Ages & Personalities:** Andrea Jennifer is curious and slightly bossy; Ashley Erin is quieter but fiercely loyal. Both are old enough to remember this day vividly but young enough for it to feel magical.
* **Toronto Blue Jays Pride Day:** Held during June Pride Month at Rogers Centre. The stadium is awash in rainbow colors—pride flags, rainbow jerseys, special Pride Night promotions, and a family-friendly atmosphere celebrating love and inclusion. The team has a long-standing partnership with local LGBTQ+ organizations.
* **Mike’s Connections:** Mike’s wealth and high-profile status give him access to a private suite and the opportunity to meet players before the game.
* **The “First Baseball Game” Milestone:** This is the girls’ very first professional baseball game, making every detail new and exciting.

---

## BEAT 1 — THE MORNING PLANNING: EXCITEMENT AT THE MANSION

* **The Setting:** The large kitchen at the Mike and Terri Anne mansion on a sunny Saturday morning in June.
* **The Conversation:** Mike, in his casual weekend clothes (a simple blue polo), sits at the island with the two girls. He shows them the tickets on his phone and explains they’re staying in a hotel for the whole weekend.
  * Andrea Jennifer: “Dad, is it true the whole stadium turns rainbow?”
  * Ashley Erin (quietly): “Will we see the players up close?”
* **The Details:** Mike explains Pride Day in simple terms: “It’s a day the Blue Jays celebrate that everyone is welcome and loved, no matter who they are.” He packs a small backpack with sunscreen, hats, and a rainbow pin for each girl. He also mentions they have a special private box.
* **The Drive:** They leave in Mike’s black SUV. The girls sit in the back, chattering about what they hope to see.

---

## BEAT 2 — ARRIVAL AT ROGERS CENTRE & MEETING THE PLAYERS

* **The Exterior:** The massive stadium gleams under the June sun. Enormous rainbow banners hang from the building. Pride flags line the walkways. Families of all kinds stream toward the entrances.
* **Private Suite Access:** Instead of regular seats, they are taken to a private luxury box with the best view of the field. The suite is decorated with rainbow accents.
* **Meeting the Players:** Because of Mike’s connections, a few Toronto Blue Jays players come up to the suite before the game to say hello. The players are warm and kind, giving the girls high-fives and signing small baseballs. Ashley Erin’s eyes light up when one player compliments her rainbow pin.
* **The Girls’ Reactions:** Andrea Jennifer asks a hundred questions. Ashley Erin is especially moved by how openly and joyfully the team celebrates Pride Day — the rainbow everything, the inclusive messages, and the welcoming atmosphere.

---

## BEAT 3 — THE GAME ITSELF: WONDER AND JOY (PRIVATE BOX)

* **The Atmosphere:** From the private box, they have an incredible view. The Jumbotron displays rainbow graphics and messages of inclusion. A pre-game ceremony features local LGBTQ+ youth groups.
* **Ashley Erin’s Happiness:** Ashley Erin is visibly glowing. She keeps pointing out all the rainbow details and says quietly to Mike, “They made the whole stadium rainbow just to say everyone is welcome. That’s really nice.”
* **The Experience:**
  * They enjoy catered snacks and drinks in the suite.
  * When the crowd does the wave, the girls stand up and cheer, laughing.
  * A foul ball lands several rows away — the girls scream with excitement.
* **Pride Elements:** Rainbow-wrapped bats during a special promotion. Fans waving pride flags. A kiss-cam that shows families of all kinds.
* **The Highlight:** In the 5th inning, a Blue Jays player hits a home run. The girls jump up and down, hugging Mike. He lifts them both for a better view.
* **No Scorekeeping:** They don’t keep score. They just enjoy the game, the colors, and the energy.

---

## BEAT 4 — AFTER THE GAME: HOTEL CHECK-IN, EMOTIONAL MOMENT & THANK YOU HUGS (FRIDAY NIGHT)

* **Post-Game:** They stay until the end. The Blue Jays win. The stadium plays celebratory music with rainbow lights.
* **Souvenirs:** On the way out, Mike buys each girl a small rainbow Blue Jays cap and a foam finger.
* **Hotel Check-In:** Instead of driving home, they check into a nice downtown Toronto hotel (with a view of the CN Tower). The girls are thrilled to have a “sleepover” with Dad.
* **Emotional Moment in the Hotel Room:** Later that evening, after getting ready for bed, Ashley Erin becomes very emotional. She sits on the edge of the bed, still wearing her rainbow cap, and starts to cry quietly. The day’s joy — meeting the players, seeing the entire stadium celebrate Pride so openly and happily — overwhelms her. She whispers, “I didn’t know a baseball team could be so nice to everyone.”
* **Andrea Jennifer’s Hug:** Andrea Jennifer immediately goes over and wraps her arms around her little sister. She doesn’t say much at first — just holds her. After a moment she says softly, “It’s okay to feel happy like this. I’m happy too.” The two sisters stay like that for a while, with Andrea Jennifer gently patting Ashley Erin’s back until she calms down. Mike watches quietly from the doorway, giving them space.
* **The Thank You Hugs (Friday Night):** Once Ashley Erin has calmed down, both girls turn to Mike. Andrea Jennifer and Ashley Erin run over and give him a big, tight hug. They each kiss him on the cheek and say together, “Thank you for the most perfect weekend ever, Daddy.” Mike smiles and hugs them back, clearly moved.

---

## BEAT 5 — SUNDAY: RUNNING THE BASES

* **The Event:** On Sunday morning, they return to Rogers Centre for the special “Kids Run the Bases” event after the game.
* **The Experience:** Andrea Jennifer and Ashley Erin get to run the bases just like the players. They’re both laughing and out of breath by the time they finish. Photos are taken at home plate.
* **Ashley Erin’s Joy:** She keeps talking about how proud the team is of Pride Day and how happy it made her feel.
* **Afternoon:** They explore more of downtown Toronto, grabbing ice cream and relaxing at the hotel.

---

## BEAT 6 — MONDAY: TORONTO ISLAND, CN TOWER & THE SPLIT (ALDOMA PICKS UP ANDREA JENNIFER)

* **Toronto Island (Morning):** In the morning, Mike takes both girls on the ferry to Toronto Island. They rent bicycles and ride around, visit the beach, and have a picnic with a view of the city skyline.
* **CN Tower (Afternoon):** In the afternoon, they go up the CN Tower. The girls are amazed by the height and the glass floor. They take lots of photos.
* **The Split:** While they are at the CN Tower, Mike receives a call. He has an unexpected opportunity to take Ashley Erin to Santiago for a modeling gig. Because of the timing, he has to leave immediately with Ashley Erin.
* **Aldoma Arrives:** Aldoma comes to the CN Tower to pick up Andrea Jennifer and bring her home. Andrea Jennifer is not disappointed — she knows she will be meeting Mike and Ashley Erin in Natal at the end of the week.
* **Mike’s Goodbye:** Mike explains the situation to both girls. Andrea Jennifer gives Ashley Erin a quick hug and waves excitedly, already looking forward to seeing them again in Natal. Mike kisses both daughters goodbye before leaving with Ashley Erin for the airport.

---

## BEAT 7 — THE RETURN HOME

* **Back at the Mansion:** Terri Anne greets them at the door on Tuesday. The girls immediately show off their new hats, signed baseballs, and tell her everything about the weekend.
* **The Memory:** That night, Mike tucks the girls in. They fall asleep still wearing their rainbow caps.
* **The Quiet Moment:** Later, Mike tells Terri Anne how special it was to give them this normal, happy extended weekend memory.

---

## WHAT THIS OUTLINE ADDS TO CANON

1. **Character Development:** Shows Mike as a warm, hands-on father who prioritizes creating joyful memories for his younger daughters.
2. **Sister Relationship:** Highlights the bond between Andrea Jennifer and Ashley Erin, with Andrea Jennifer stepping into a comforting big-sister role.
3. **Family Tone:** Provides a light, positive contrast to the heavier dramatic scenes in the story.
4. **Pride Month Integration:** Establishes that the Key family participates in and supports Pride celebrations in a natural, age-appropriate way. Ashley Erin’s strong positive (and emotional) reaction to the Blue Jays’ Pride Day is highlighted.
5. **Milestone Memory:** Creates a specific, treasured “first baseball game + weekend in Toronto” story the girls can reference in future scenes.
6. **Toronto Setting:** Grounds the family in the city with a fun, multi-day local adventure (Rogers Centre, Toronto Island, CN Tower).
7. **Mike’s Connections:** Reinforces his ability to provide special experiences through his status and network.
8. **New Dynamic:** Introduces the modeling opportunity for Ashley Erin, Aldoma’s role in the family logistics, and the upcoming trip to Natal.

---

*This scene can stand alone as a heartwarming interlude or be referenced in later conversations between the sisters about their father.*
