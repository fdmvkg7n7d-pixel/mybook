## 4-WEEK MASTER PARISIAN ITINERARY

### Week 1 — Settling the Overarching Classroom & Ordinary City Life
* **Acclimatization:** The long flights from Toronto conclude. The family settles into their Parisian compound.
* **Morning Market Runs:** True to Terri Anne's promise (*"In July, we are going to be Parisians"*), the family walks every morning to local authentic street markets. Patti Anne, Jessica, and Rachel lead the younger girls to purchase warm morning baguettes, wheels of Camembert, and punnets of fresh French strawberries.
* **Artistic Sketching:** Andrea Jennifer (7) and Fallon Glenda (8) spend their afternoons in the shaded sculpture courtyards of the Louvre and along the stone banks of the Seine, sketching historical gargoyles and bridges in their travel notebooks.

---

### Week 2 — The Historical Pilgr pilgrimage: Normandy & The D-Day Beaches
A convoy of private, tinted Mercedes executive vans transports the entire household from Paris through the lush apple orchards of Normandy for a profound three-day historical pilgrimage.

* **Juno Beach (The Canadian Blueprint):** Because Mike Key is a deeply grounded Canadian patriarch who works with the OPP out of profound civic duty, their first stop is specifically **Juno Beach** (Courseulles-sur-Mer) and the Canadian memorials. Mike walks the preserved bunkers and sandy coastline with his eight daughters. 
* **The ASL Duty Lesson:** Sabrina Bryna (11) rolls in her specialized all-terrain beach wheelchair across the Normandy sand. Mike stops by a massive concrete gun emplacement, kneels in the sand beside her, and signs fluidly in ASL. He explains to Sabrina and her seven sisters the massive historical price paid by young Canadians on June 6, 1944, to secure structural freedom and combat global tyranny.
* **Omaha Beach & The Normandy American Cemetery:** The family visits the sprawling, immaculate green lawns of Colleville-sur-Mer. The eight sisters walk flawlessly and quietly among the rows of white marble crosses, absorbing a solemn intergenerational reverence for sacrifice.

---

### Week 3 — The Magical Excursion: Disneyland Paris
The family returns from Normandy and transitions to the east of Paris for a magical, three-day VIP stay at **Disneyland Paris** (Marne-la-Vallée).

* **Private Compound / Disneyland Hotel Stay:** Mike secures the ultimate VIP luxury suites. They have exclusive private guides, immediate front-of-line walk-on access to every classic attraction (*Pirates of the Caribbean*, *Peter Pan's Flight*), and private viewing balconies for the spectacular evening castle fireworks.
* **Ashley Erin's Joy:** Little Ashley Erin (5) wears sparkly Mickey ears and tumbles into Mike's arms, her face aglow with unblemished magical wonder.
* **Sabrina Bryna's Ride Engineering:** Sabrina (11) is fascinated by the intricate mechanical show-writing and animatronics of the ride systems, prefiguring her eldest daughter **Carlota Delicia's** eventual triumphant adult career at **Walt Disney Imagineering**.
* **Saga Canon Origin:** This spectacular, joy-filled excursion sets the exact intergenerational precedent that inspires Gamaliel and Klára to make *"an annual, fixed Disneyland Paris trip for every grade-eight class"* an ironclad tradition for Pražská Baletní Akademie decades later.

---

### Week 4 — The Eiffel Tower Date Night & Patti Anne's Fiduciary Anchor
The crowning emotional milestone of their Parisian month. After weeks of hands-on, deeply devoted parenting across museums, Normandy beaches, and theme parks, Mike and Terri Anne take a rare, exquisite romantic evening entirely for themselves.

#### Beat 1 — The Parents Depart for the Tower
* Mike secures an exclusive, highly sought-after window table at **Le Jules Verne**, the Michelin-starred gastronomic restaurant located on the second floor of the **Eiffel Tower**, 125 meters above Paris.
* Terri Anne wears a spectacular, elegant evening gown; Mike wears a remarkably sharp, tailored midnight-blue suit. They kiss their eight daughters goodbye in the foyer of their gated Le Marais townhome.

#### Beat 2 — Patti Anne Steps Up as Family Head
* With their parents out for the night, eighteen-year-old **Patti Anne Key** steps flawlessly into the overarching matriarchal and Fiduciary leadership role.
* She does not call staff. She manages the evening with absolute internal discipline and relational warmth.
* She organizes the evening meal at the long wooden dining table—serving authentic French cheeses, warm vegetable tarts, and fresh fruit. She ensures **Sabrina Bryna's custom core-stabilizing ergonomic chair** is perfectly padded and adjusted, sitting right beside her so Sabrina’s hands are completely free from spastic strain as they sign and laugh together.

#### Beat 3 — Bedtime Stories & Relational Security
* In the cozy bedtime room, Patti Anne gathers all seven of her younger sisters around the massive mattress.
* Sixteen-year-old Jessica and Rachel assist her, brushing out Andrea Jennifer (7) and Ashley Erin's (5) long strawberry blonde hair.
* Patti Anne reads them a beautiful Parisian bedtime story. When little Ashley Erin asks *"Is Mommy and Daddy high up in the lights?"*, Patti Anne walks her to the balcony window, pointing to the glittering, sweeping golden beam of the Eiffel Tower cutting across the night sky. *"Right up there, little one. They're looking down over our roof right now."*

#### Beat 4 — The Parents' Romantic Dinner in the Tower
* Intercut to Mike and Terri Anne sitting at their candlelit window table inside the iron lattice of the Eiffel Tower.
* Below them, the sprawling City of Light glitters along the curves of the Seine.
* Terri Anne reaches across the crisp white tablecloth and covers Mike's immense, warm hand with her palm. They share a quiet, deeply intimate toast—celebrating twenty years of their marriage, the immense wealth they’ve grounded in genuine duty, and the magnificent eight daughters sleeping safely under Patti Anne's watch.

#### Beat 5 — Closing Image: Tucked In & The Matriarchs Return
* Back in the penthouse, Patti Anne gently tucks Fallon Glenda (8), Andrea Jennifer (7), and Ashley Erin (5) under their soft pink duvets, kissing their brows exactly the way Mike does.
* Late at night, Mike and Terri Anne return from the Eiffel Tower. They step into the dim hallway and find Patti Anne sitting in the study, writing in her travel notebook, her bipolar mind perfectly steady, anchored in genuine Fiduciary protection. Mike rests a massive, warm, deeply proud palm on Patti Anne's shoulder: *"Masterpiece, eldest daughter."*
