# Kiley Rose and Julia Meet Jamal at Brandi Jessica’s Penthouse — Scene Outline

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Characters:** Brandi Jessica Key (21), Jamal Al-Otaibi (21), Pandora Paige Key (21), Kiley Rose Key, Julia
- **Offstage / referenced:** Mohammed, Imam Kareem, Bassem
- **Onstage animal:** Burden, Brandi Jessica’s Labernese guide dog
- **Location:** Brandi Jessica’s penthouse, Las Vegas, Nevada
- **When:** UNLV years, ~2065–2066 — the evening after Brandi tells Kiley Rose and Julia about her new relationship
- **Status:** Scene outline
- **Tone:** Cautious, direct, intimate, domestic, gradually warming

---

## SCENE PURPOSE

Kiley Rose and Julia meet Jamal for the first time at **Brandi Jessica’s Las Vegas penthouse**.

The meeting happens during Kiley Rose and Julia’s visit with Brandi and Pandora, after Brandi has already ended her relationship with Mohammed and told her mothers the truth about Jamal. Jamal did not participate in that disclosure. Brandi spoke for herself, with Pandora beside her.

Now Brandi invites Jamal to the penthouse so Kiley Rose and Julia can meet him face to face. Pandora remains present. The penthouse gives Brandi control of the meeting: it is her home, she knows every room by memory, and she can end the evening if it becomes too much.

Kiley Rose and Julia do not offer instant forgiveness or an immediate welcome into the family. They ask Jamal direct questions, watch how he treats Brandi, and decide that they are willing to know him beyond the betrayal that began the relationship.

---

## SCENE ARC / BEATS

### Beat 1: Brandi Prepares Her Own Home

- Late afternoon at the penthouse.
- Kiley Rose and Julia are staying with Brandi through the next morning. Pandora has extended her Las Vegas stay and will fly to Los Angeles after the family breakfast.
- Brandi tells her mothers that Jamal will arrive at six.
- Kiley asks whether Brandi is certain she wants the meeting tonight.

> “Yes. Yesterday was for me to tell you. Tonight is for you to meet him.”

- Brandi chooses the penthouse rather than a restaurant because she does not want their first meeting shaped by strangers, noise, or a public performance.
- She knows the space completely. She can move through it without assistance and can step away whenever she chooses.
- Pandora helps set the table, but Brandi decides where everyone will sit.
- Julia notices the intention behind the seating:
  - Brandi and Jamal will not sit fused together as a defensive pair.
  - Kiley and Julia will not sit across from Jamal like an interrogation panel.
  - Everyone will share the same table.
- Burden is off harness and resting near Brandi’s chair.

### Beat 2: Jamal Rings the Bell

- Jamal has access to the penthouse, but he does not use it tonight. He rings the bell and waits to be invited in.
- Brandi opens the door herself.
- Jamal greets her quietly and asks, *“Still okay?”*
- Brandi answers, *“Still okay.”*
- She takes his hand—not because she needs guidance in her own home, but because she wants contact—and leads him into the living room.
- Kiley Rose, Julia, and Pandora are waiting.
- Brandi makes the introduction:

> “Mom, Julia—this is Jamal Al-Otaibi. Jamal, this is my mother, Kiley Rose, and this is Julia.”

- Jamal says:

> “Thank you for agreeing to meet me.”

- Kiley answers:

> “We came to Las Vegas to see our daughters. Meeting you is what comes next.”

- The line is not hostile, but it makes the order clear: Brandi and Pandora are the center of the visit.

### Beat 3: First Impressions

- Jamal has brought dinner from a halal restaurant: several dishes so no one is forced into his preferences, plus something simple Brandi already likes.
- Julia takes the bags and thanks him.
- Jamal does not reach automatically for Brandi’s elbow or begin guiding her around her own penthouse.
- When Brandi asks where one hot container has been placed, Jamal answers clearly: *“At eleven o’clock, just beyond your left hand.”*
- Kiley notices that he gives information rather than taking over.
- Pandora notices Kiley noticing.
- Brandi asks everyone to sit in the living room before they eat.
- Jamal chooses the open chair Brandi indicates rather than placing himself protectively between Brandi and her mothers.

### Beat 4: Kiley Begins With Mohammed

- Kiley does not spend ten minutes on polite conversation before addressing the reason the meeting is difficult.

> “Brandi told us how this began. I want to hear whether your version changes when she isn’t the one speaking.”

- Jamal says it does not change.
- He tells them:
  - Mohammed was his closest friend and brother through the mosque.
  - He knew Brandi was still in a relationship with Mohammed.
  - He became involved with her anyway.
  - Mohammed discovered them at the penthouse.
  - Jamal and Brandi later went to Imam Kareem and admitted what they had done.
- Jamal does not describe Mohammed as deserving the betrayal, even though he knows Mohammed treated Brandi badly.

> “He hurt Brandi. I still betrayed him. Both things are true.”

- Kiley asks whether Jamal understands why she cannot treat their affair as a romantic beginning.
- Jamal answers:

> “Yes. I’m not asking you to make it beautiful.”

### Beat 5: Julia Asks About “Fresh”

- Julia refers to Brandi’s description of the relationship as new and fresh.

> “What does fresh mean to you?”

- Jamal does not say it means the past no longer matters.

> “No more hiding. No asking Brandi to carry my excuses. Ordinary days where we tell the truth before it becomes convenient not to.”

- Julia asks what happens when honesty threatens the relationship.
- Jamal says:

> “Then the relationship has to survive the truth or it shouldn’t survive.”

- Brandi hears the answer without treating it as a perfect speech. She tells her mothers that Jamal will have to prove it through repetition, not one evening.
- Jamal agrees.

### Beat 6: Kiley Rose Protects Brandi’s Independence

- Kiley asks Jamal what he understands about loving a blind woman who was raised to do anything and everything.
- Jamal answers that Brandi does not need him to make her life smaller or safer on his terms.
- Kiley gives him the rules she and Julia have lived by since Brandi lost her sight:
  - Ask before helping.
  - Give information instead of commands.
  - Never move Brandi’s belongings without telling her.
  - Never use concern as a reason to overrule her.
  - Believe her when she says she can do something herself.
- Jamal says he understands.
- Brandi interrupts:

> “And I have rules too. I have to ask instead of expecting him to guess. I have to listen when he says no. I have to be good to him too.”

- Julia nods. She is pleased that the conversation does not cast Jamal as a caretaker or Brandi as a responsibility.

### Beat 7: Faith and Family

- Julia asks Jamal about his Muslim faith and Brandi’s Catholic faith.
- Jamal says he does not expect Brandi to convert.
- He explains that Brandi accompanied him to speak with Imam Kareem out of respect for his community. He intends to show the same respect when Brandi asks him to attend Mass or a Catholic family event.
- Kiley asks about his family.
- Jamal tells them he was raised in Reno. His mother is Chinese Uyghur, his father is Saudi Arabian, and he grew up speaking Uyghur Turkic, Arabic, and English.
- He does not turn the meeting into a full account of family trauma, but he answers honestly when Kiley asks why cultural continuity matters so much to him.
- Brandi listens closely. There are parts of Jamal’s history she is still learning too.
- Pandora sees that Kiley and Julia are no longer only assessing a boyfriend; they are beginning to see a whole person.

### Beat 8: Dinner at Brandi’s Table

- The group moves to the dining table.
- Brandi navigates independently, one hand briefly touching the back of her chair to confirm its position.
- Jamal waits rather than pulling the chair out without asking.
- Brandi says, *“You can move it back two inches.”* He does exactly that and stops.
- Julia serves the hot dishes while Pandora names each one.
- Jamal describes Brandi’s plate using the clock method only after she asks him to.
- Kiley watches how little drama surrounds the accommodations. Jamal helps without making the help the center of Brandi’s identity.
- Conversation gradually becomes more ordinary:
  - UNLV classes.
  - Jamal’s childhood in Reno.
  - Pandora’s flight to Los Angeles the next day.
  - Kiley and Julia’s plans for the rest of their Las Vegas visit.
- Jamal makes Julia laugh unexpectedly. The tension in the room loosens.

### Beat 9: Pandora’s Question

- Pandora has already given Jamal her private approval, but she asks one question in front of the mothers:

> “What happens if Brandi decides this isn’t right for her?”

- Jamal looks toward Brandi before answering.

> “Then I will be hurt. And she will still be allowed to leave.”

- Brandi’s hand finds Pandora’s beneath the table.
- Kiley hears the importance of the answer. Lazaro ran when Kiley chose her daughters; other partners in the family have treated love as possession. Jamal’s answer does not prove everything, but it is the right foundation.

### Beat 10: Kiley’s Decision

- After dinner, Kiley asks to speak without anyone interrupting.
- She tells Jamal:

> “I do not approve of how you and Brandi began. I may never look back at that part and feel anything but disappointment.”

- Jamal answers, *“I understand.”*
- Kiley continues:

> “But I can see that she is herself around you. I can see that you listen. I am willing to know you beyond the worst choice the two of you made.”

- Julia adds:

> “That is not instant trust. It is another meeting.”

- Jamal says another meeting is enough.
- Brandi does not ask her mothers whether they *like* him. She understands that willingness is the honest answer available tonight.

### Beat 11: Jamal Is Invited Back

- As the evening ends, Jamal begins gathering the empty containers.
- Julia tells him to leave them; the family will clean up.
- Kiley asks what time he is free the next morning.
- Jamal says he has no class until the afternoon.
- Kiley tells him:

> “Come back for breakfast at nine. If you want us to know you, ordinary mornings count too.”

- Jamal accepts.
- It is the first genuine invitation Kiley offers him.
- At the door, Jamal asks Brandi quietly whether she wants him to stay a few more minutes.
- Brandi says no. She wants to finish the evening with her mothers and Pandora.
- Jamal accepts the answer without disappointment becoming pressure.

> “Nine o’clock.”

> “Ring the bell.”

- He smiles. *“I will.”*

### Beat 12: After the Door Closes

- Brandi closes the door and returns to the living room.
- Pandora asks Kiley what she thinks.
- Kiley answers:

> “I met him. I’m willing to meet him again.”

- Pandora grins because she recognizes this as significant progress.
- Julia tells Brandi that Jamal answered well, but the family will watch what he does over time.
- Brandi says, *“So will I.”*
- Kiley reaches for Brandi’s hand.

> “Good. Fresh is not something he gives you. It is something both of you keep choosing.”

- The five-person meeting is over. The four women remain in the penthouse together, exactly as they did before Jamal arrived.
- Jamal has not been welcomed into the family yet, but he has been welcomed back to Brandi Jessica’s door.
