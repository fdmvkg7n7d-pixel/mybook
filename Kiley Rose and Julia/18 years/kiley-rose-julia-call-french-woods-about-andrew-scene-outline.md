# Kiley Rose and Julia Call French Woods About Andrew — Scene Outline

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Characters:** Kiley Rose Key (twenty-three years old), Julia (twenty years old), French Woods Director / Head of Admissions or Head Counselor (on the phone)
- **Offstage / referenced characters:** Brandi Jessica Key (eight years old), Pandora Paige Key (eight years old), Mike Key
- **Locations:** Kiley Rose and Julia’s townhouse in New York City (phone call); French Woods administrative office in Hancock, New York
- **When:** April of Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige's eighth year. Kiley Rose and Julia call French Woods about Andrew. The twins attend French Woods in July of their eighth year.
- **Status:** New scene outline
- **Tone:** Serious, calm, determined, protective, matter-of-fact, “without any single doubt,” family philosophy forward

## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene establishes that Kiley Rose and Julia proactively and privately informed French Woods about Andrew’s kidnapping of Brandi Jessica (December 2051) well in advance of the twins attending French Woods.

Kiley Rose and Julia call French Woods about Andrew in **April of Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige's eighth year**. The twins attend French Woods in **July of their eighth year**.

It ensures the camp staff are fully aware of the recent trauma so they can support Brandi Jessica appropriately without the twins overhearing sensitive details at drop-off.

The call reinforces the family’s core philosophy: they are sending the girls “without any single doubt,” blindness is an adjustment never an excuse, and there are no “no’s,” only “hows.”

It sets up continuity for the first-arrival scene, where staff already know the history and the twins proceed with full independence and Pandora’s constant help. The call occurs in April of the twins' eighth year, before they attend camp in July.

## BEATS

### Beat 1: The Decision to Call
- In April of Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige's eighth year (post-rescue, post-bilateral enucleation in February 2052, and in the midst of early blindness adjustment), Kiley Rose and Julia are planning the twins attending French Woods in July of their eighth year.
- They decide, without any single doubt, that Brandi will attend and experience the full camp like any other child.
- Before registering or sending the girls, they agree they must inform the camp leadership privately about Andrew so staff can be prepared for potential triggers (references to strangers, being taken away, closed rooms, unfamiliar men, etc.).
- Pandora is not part of this call; the mothers handle it together while the twins are at school or with family.

### Beat 2: Placing the Call
- Kiley Rose and Julia sit together at the kitchen table in the townhouse.
- Julia takes the lead on the call (calm, structured, professional), with Kiley Rose beside her providing details and emotional weight.
- They dial the main French Woods administrative number and ask to speak with the Director or Head of Admissions / Head Counselor.
- After a brief introduction and confirmation of the upcoming registration for the twins (Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige Key, age seven), they request a private conversation.

### Beat 3: Explaining the Kidnapping
- Calmly and without hesitation, they explain the events of December 2051:
  - Brandi Jessica (then six, turning seven) was kidnapped from their family townhouse Christmas party on December 26 by a man named **Andrew**, a wealthy guest who was there that night.
  - Andrew’s motive: financial vengeance against Mike Key (he lost $500 million in a bad deal involving Mike).
  - Brandi was held in a luxury hotel room outside NYC for a short time; she was drugged with liquid Ketamine to keep her compliant.
  - She recognized Andrew’s voice from the party.
  - The family and police recovered her; the kidnapper was dealt with.
- They emphasize that the kidnapping happened **after** the December 23, 2051 retinoblastoma diagnosis but **before** the life-saving bilateral enucleation (which was pushed to February 2052 because of the disappearance).
- Brandi is now completely blind from the surgery and wears custom ocular prosthetics. She uses a white cane. Pandora is her primary helper and visual translator.
- The girls are now eight years old and will attend camp that July.

### Beat 4: Request for Staff Awareness + Philosophy Statement
- Kiley Rose and Julia request that the head staff (director, head counselor, and any key activity leaders) be privately briefed.
- They list potential triggers: mentions of “being taken,” strangers, kidnappings, closed or unfamiliar rooms, unfamiliar men in authority, or anything that could evoke the captivity.
- They stress that Pandora Paige will be Brandi’s constant co-pilot at camp (navigation, physical adjustments in ballet/gymnastics/aerial, visual descriptions, daily logistics, performances).
- Kiley Rose states clearly and firmly:
  > “We are sending both girls without any single doubt. Blindness is an adjustment, never an excuse. There are no no’s, only hows. Brandi can do anything the other campers can do. We want her to have the complete, unshielded French Woods experience.”
- Julia adds the practical request: “We do not want the twins to overhear these details at check-in or arrival. Please handle any additional questions or preparations discreetly.”

### Beat 5: Staff Response and Confirmation
- The French Woods representative listens with professional seriousness and respect.
- They confirm the camp’s experience supporting campers with trauma histories and medical needs (including blindness).
- They ask clarifying questions only as needed (e.g., current age, any ongoing medical needs, preferred communication).
- They agree to privately brief the necessary staff before the summer and to treat the information with confidentiality.
- They reassure the mothers that Brandi and Pandora will be supported in participating fully.
- The call ends with registration details being confirmed and a note that the camp will follow up with any forms if needed.

### Beat 6: After the Call — Planning to Tell the Girls
- Kiley Rose and Julia hang up.
- They sit in silence for a moment, holding hands.
- Kiley: “It’s done. They know. Now we get to tell the girls.”
- Julia: “Without any single doubt.”
- They decide to tell Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige that same evening, during bedtime in the girls’ bedroom.
- They want the news to feel warm and exciting, not rushed.

### Beat 7: The Evening Telling (Leads Into Separate Scene)
- Later that evening, after the call, Kiley Rose and Julia go to the twins’ bedroom while the girls are getting ready for bed.
- They sit with Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige and share the news that the twins will attend French Woods in July of their eighth year.
- (See the separate scene outline: `kiley-rose-julia-tell-twins-about-french-woods-bedtime-scene-outline.md` for the full emotional conversation and the girls’ joyful reaction.)
