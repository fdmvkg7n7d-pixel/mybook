# The Rescue at Shakib's Compound — Scene Outline

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Characters:** Brandi Jessica Key (seven years old), Kiley Rose Key (twenty-one years old), Mike Key, Terri Anne Key, Prince Ali Javan, Shakib, UAE Police
- **Offstage / referenced characters:** Julia, Pandora Paige Key, Andrea Jennifer, Ashley Erin, Andrew, Greenlee
- **Location:** Shakib's compound in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates
- **When:** January 31, 2052 — the day after Prince Ali Javan's call to Mike
- **Status:** New scene outline
- **Tone:** Resolution, rescue, confrontation, the intersection of royal power and family desperation

---

## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene takes place on January 31, 2052, the day after Prince Ali Javan called Mike on January 30. Kiley Rose, Mike, and Terri Anne immediately flew to the UAE after receiving the call. They are accompanied by UAE police as they go to Shakib's compound to rescue Brandi Jessica. The rescue is successful.

Andrew and Greenlee are not at the compound. They are back in New York City.

Prince Ali Javan had already taken Shakib's daughter to be his wife before making the call to Mike. This means his help is not motivated by a future marriage offer — it is purely about the relationship he built with Mike and Terri Anne over ten years, and the respect they showed him.

## SCENE ARC / BEATS

### Beat 1: The Arrival at the Compound
- It is January 31, 2052, the day after Prince Ali Javan called Mike on January 30.
- Kiley Rose, Mike, and Terri Anne immediately flew to the UAE after receiving the call. They could not wait. They could not stay behind. Their daughter is there.
- Julia stayed home with Pandora Paige, Andrea Jennifer, and Ashley Erin.
- They arrive at the compound in Sharjah with the UAE police.
- The compound is surrounded by walls. It has a gate. It is a private property that has operated as its own enclosed world.
- The police have the authority to enter. Prince Ali Javan's influence ensures this. His uncle is the king.
- The gate opens. The police enter. The compound is no longer private property — it is now under investigation.
- Kiley Rose is with the police. She is not staying behind. She is going in.

### Beat 2: Entering the Compound
- The police move through the compound. They check the buildings, the rooms, the household.
- Kiley Rose, Mike, and Terri Anne follow behind the police, or beside them. They are not waiting outside. They are going in to find their daughter.
- The compound is large. It has multiple buildings, rooms, a courtyard. It is a household with three wives, twenty children, and staff.
- The police are methodical. They search the compound. They are looking for Brandi Jessica. They are looking for evidence.

### Beat 3: Finding Brandi Jessica
- Kiley Rose finds Brandi Jessica in a bedroom within the compound.
- Brandi Jessica is seven years old. She has been held here for 21 days.
- The bedroom has a bucket for her bathroom. She has been using a bucket. She has been living in conditions that are dehumanizing, that treat her like less than human.
- The bucket is a detail that will haunt Kiley Rose, Mike, and Terri Anne forever. It is a detail that shows what Brandi Jessica has been living through.
- Brandi Jessica is pretty out of it and very nervous, just as Prince Ali Javan described.
- Kiley Rose sees her daughter. She sees that she is alive. She sees that she is there. She sees the bucket. She sees the conditions.
- Kiley Rose takes Brandi Jessica. She holds her. She holds her daughter for the first time in 21 days.

### Beat 4: The Rescue
- Brandi Jessica is rescued. She is brought out of the compound by Kiley Rose, Mike, and Terri Anne.
- She is with her family. She is free.
- The details of her physical and emotional condition are not fully specified here. The reader understands that she has been through 21 days of captivity, abuse, and dehumanization.

### Beat 5: The Ambulance and Hospital Examination
- The ambulance takes Brandi Jessica to the hospital.
- The hospital checks her all over.
- Doctors conduct a comprehensive medical examination, assessing the full extent of what she has endured during 21 days of captivity.
- The doctors inform Kiley Rose, Mike, and Terri Anne everything they found during the examination.
- Kiley Rose breaks down upon hearing what the doctors tell her about what was done to her daughter.
- Terri Anne holds Kiley Rose up.
- Terri Anne tells Kiley Rose that Brandi Jessica is safe and alive.
- Terri Anne tells Kiley Rose that they will find the best psychologist to help her out.

### Beat 6: Shakib
- Shakib is at the compound. He is confronted by the police.
- He is the man who was holding Brandi Jessica. He is the man Prince Ali Javan identified. He is the man who likes young girls.
- Shakib is arrested by the UAE police. His household is disrupted. His compound is under investigation.

### Beat 7: Prince Ali Javan's Marriage
- It is revealed that Prince Ali Javan had already taken Shakib's daughter to be his wife before calling Mike.
- This means his help is not motivated by a future marriage offer or by protecting his own interests.
- His help is purely about the relationship he built with Mike and Terri Anne over ten years.
- It is about the respect they showed him — Terri Anne wearing the full outfit, the girls respecting his compound and playing with his children.
- It is about the trust that lasted beyond the business deal.
- Prince Ali Javan helped because of respect, not because of self-interest.

### Beat 8: Andrew and Greenlee
- Andrew and Greenlee are not at the compound. They are back in New York City.
- Their fate will be determined separately.

### Beat 9: The Aftermath
- Brandi Jessica is rescued. She is with Kiley Rose, Mike, and Terri Anne. She is free.
- Shakib is in police custody. His compound is under investigation.
- The family has their daughter back. The investigation has produced a result. The respect has paid off. The relationship has given them what they needed.
- Kiley Rose holds Brandi Jessica. She does not let go. She has been waiting for this moment for 21 days.

### Beat 10: Returning Home
- Kiley Rose, Mike, and Terri Anne prepare to return home with Brandi Jessica.
- They have their daughter back. They are going home.
- Julia, Pandora Paige, Andrea Jennifer, and Ashley Erin are waiting at home.
- The family is reunited. The crisis is over. The investigation is complete.
