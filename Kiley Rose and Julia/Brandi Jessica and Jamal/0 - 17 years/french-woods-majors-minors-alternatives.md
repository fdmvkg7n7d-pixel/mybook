# French Woods Majors & Minors — Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige (Age 8, First Summer 2053)

## Session Structure

The twins attend **three back-to-back sessions** each summer at French Woods: Session 2, Session 3, and Session 4.

**Parent Visiting Day:** On the last day of each session, parents can visit their child at camp. This means Kiley Rose and Julia visit the twins three times over the summer — at the end of Session 2, the end of Session 3, and the end of Session 4.

**Meals:** Each cabin eats at its own assigned table in the cafeteria. Birch Cabin's table includes Brandi Jessica, Pandora Paige, Sloan, Rilee, and the other Birch Cabin girls, with their counselor Maya at the end.

**Daily order:** Breakfast → Major 1 → Minor 1 → Minor 2 → Lunch → Major 2 → Minor 3 → Major 3 → Dinner

**Minors are chosen daily** — each camper picks different minors every day from the available options. They are not fixed across the session.
