# Prince Ali Javan Calls Mike — Scene Outline

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Characters:** Mike Key, Terri Anne Key
- **Offstage / referenced characters:** Prince Ali Javan, Brandi Jessica Key (seven years old), Shakib, Andrew, Greenlee, Kiley Rose Key, Julia
- **Location:** Mike and Terri Anne's residence, New York City (or their own investigation base)
- **When:** January 30, 2052 — twenty-six days after Brandi Jessica's kidnapping
- **Status:** New scene outline
- **Tone:** A breakthrough in the investigation. The beginning of knowing where Brandi Jessica is. The intersection of business, respect, and family desperation.

---

## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene takes place on January 30, 2052. Prince Ali Javan calls Mike Key with information about Brandi Jessica's location. Prince Ali Javan is a businessman in the UAE who lives there. He has business dealings with Shakib, but he knows Mike and Terri Anne much better — ten years ago, around 2042, Mike was buying oil from Prince Ali Javan, and since then Prince Ali Javan has been making a lot of money from Key Corporation.

Ten years ago, during that oil business, Mike and Terri Anne visited Prince Ali Javan's compound in the UAE. Their children accompanied them, including Kiley Rose. Terri Anne showed respect for his religion by wearing the full outfit when they visited. This respect — this recognition of his faith and his culture — established a relationship of trust between Terri Anne and Prince Ali Javan, and between the Key family and him. That respect lasted beyond the business deal. Prince Ali Javan saw their children learning respect by watching their parents.

Now, during Mike and Terri Anne's own investigation into Brandi Jessica's kidnapping (the one Mike told Kiley Rose and Julia he was already doing in the airport video scene), Prince Ali Javan is calling Mike. He is telling him that Brandi Jessica is with Shakib. The investigation has a name. The search has a location. The family knows where she is.

The scene establishes the moment the family's own investigation produces a result — not through the police, not through official channels, but through the respect and relationships they built over a decade ago. Terri Anne's respect for Prince Ali Javan's religion ten years ago is what made this possible now.

## SCENE ARC / BEATS

### Beat 1: The Call
- It is January 30, 2052. Twenty-six days since the kidnapping. Twenty-six days since the family has been searching.
- Mike's phone rings. The caller ID shows a number from the UAE.
- Mike answers. It is Prince Ali Javan.
- Prince Ali Javan is calling from the UAE. He has information. He has been watching. He has been listening. He has been paying attention to what is happening in his country.
- Mike listens. Terri Anne is beside him. She is part of this call. She is part of this investigation. She has been part of every step.

### Beat 2: The Business Relationship
- Prince Ali Javan and Mike have had a business relationship for over a decade. Ten years ago, around 2042, Mike was buying oil from Prince Ali Javan as part of the family's business interests.
- Since then, the relationship has grown. Prince Ali Javan has been making a lot of money from Key Corporation. The business connection is deep, long-standing, and profitable for both parties.
- Prince Ali Javan also has business dealings with Shakib. He knows Shakib. He knows what Shakib does. He knows the compound in Sharjah. He knows the household.
- But Prince Ali Javan knows Mike and Terri Anne much better. The relationship with the Keys is deeper. The trust is stronger. The respect is mutual. It's been built over more than ten years of doing business together, of mutual profit, of understanding each other's values.
- Prince Ali Javan's uncle is the king. This gives him significant power and influence in the UAE.

### Beat 3: The Visit to the Compound
- Ten years ago, during the oil business, Mike and Terri Anne visited Prince Ali Javan's compound in the UAE. They traveled there to discuss their business deal. Their children accompanied them, including Kiley Rose.
- When they arrived at Prince Ali Javan's compound, Terri Anne wore the full outfit — the full traditional dress that showed respect for his religion. She covered herself the way his faith requires. She did not do this as a performance. She did not do this as a strategy. She did it because she respected his religion, because she understood that this was what respect looked like in his culture, and because she was willing to show that respect even though it was not her own faith.
- The girls respected Prince Ali Javan's compound. They played with his children. They were respectful of his household and his culture.
- The visit established a relationship of trust that lasted beyond the business deal. Prince Ali Javan saw that the Keys were serious. He saw that they were respectful. He saw that they were not just business contacts, but people who understood the value of faith and family.
- That respect ten years ago is what made Prince Ali Javan willing to help now, when Mike and Terri Anne are searching for their kidnapped granddaughter.

### Beat 4: The Information
- Prince Ali Javan tells Mike that Brandi Jessica is with Shakib.
- He tells Mike that Brandi Jessica is at Shakib's compound in Sharjah. She is there. She is alive. She is in his household.
- Mike knows about Shakib. He knows how Shakib likes young girls. This is not new information to him, and it terrifies him.
- Mike asks Prince Ali Javan how Brandi Jessica looks. He needs to know. He needs to understand what state she is in.
- Prince Ali Javan tells Mike that Brandi Jessica is pretty out of it and very nervous. She is not in a good state. She has been through something. She is visibly distressed.
- Prince Ali Javan does not know everything that is happening at the compound. He does not know the details of what is being done to Brandi Jessica. He does not know about the dance wear, about the dancing, about the two children being offered to Andrew and Greenlee. But he knows that Brandi Jessica is there. He knows that she is with Shakib. He knows that she is being held. He knows she is pretty out of it and very nervous.
- Prince Ali Javan tells Mike what he knows. He tells him what he has seen. He tells him what he has heard. He tells him the location. He tells him the name. He tells him that Brandi Jessica is in Sharjah, at Shakib's compound, and that she is not free to leave. He tells him she is pretty out of it and very nervous.
- Mike listens. Terri Anne listens. They hear the information. They hear the name. They hear the location. They hear that their granddaughter is alive, but she is not free. She is being held by a man named Shakib in a compound in Sharjah, in the UAE, in a country where they have no jurisdiction, no authority, no way to simply walk in and take her back. They hear that she is pretty out of it and very nervous.

### Beat 5: The Relationship of Respect
- Prince Ali Javan is not telling Mike this because it is his job. He is not telling Mike this because the police asked him to. He is not telling Mike this because it is part of his business with Shakib.
- He is telling Mike this because of the relationship. Because of the respect. Because Terri Anne wore the full outfit when she visited his compound. Because Mike has been a trustworthy business partner. Because the Keys are family, and Prince Ali Javan understands the value of family.
- He is telling Mike this because he respects them. Because he sees their desperation. Because he understands that they are searching for their granddaughter, and he has information that can help, and he is choosing to share it.
- This is not a transaction. This is not a business deal. This is not a quid pro quo. This is respect. This is trust. This is the result of Terri Anne showing respect for Prince Ali Javan's religion, of Mike being a trustworthy business partner, of the Keys being people who understand the value of faith and family and respect.

### Beat 6: What Comes Next
- Prince Ali Javan has told Mike that Brandi Jessica is with Shakib. He has told him the location. He has told him what he knows.
- Mike and Terri Anne now have the information. They know where Brandi Jessica is. They know who has her. They know what country she is in.
- But they do not know how to get her out. They do not know how to reach her. They do not know how to bring her home. The UAE is a foreign country. Shakib is a powerful man. The compound is private property. The jurisdiction is not theirs.
- They have to figure out what to do next. They have to decide how to act. They have to decide whether to involve the police, whether to try to go themselves, whether to find another way.
- But they have the information. They have the name. They have the location. The investigation has produced a result. The respect has paid off. The relationship has given them what they needed.
- They know where Brandi Jessica is. Now they have to figure out how to bring her home.

### Beat 7: Prince Ali Javan Offers Help
- Prince Ali Javan offers to help Mike and Terri Anne out.
- He is not just giving information. He is offering his assistance, his resources, his help in whatever they need to do next.
- Prince Ali Javan understands their situation. He understands that they are desperate. He understands that they need to act quickly. He is offering to be part of whatever comes next.

### Beat 8: Telling Kiley Rose and Julia
- Mike and Terri Anne tell Kiley Rose and Julia about Brandi Jessica.
- They tell them that Prince Ali Javan has called. They tell them that Brandi Jessica is with Shakib, at his compound in Sharjah. They tell them what Prince Ali Javan said — that she is pretty out of it and very nervous.
- They tell them that Prince Ali Javan has offered to help.

### Beat 9: The Decision
- Mike and Terri Anne decide to fly to the UAE quickly to find Brandi Jessica.
- They fly to the UAE without informing the police.
- Julia stays home with Pandora Paige, Andrea Jennifer, and Ashley Erin.
