# Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige Choose Their French Woods Majors and Minors (Age 8)

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Characters:** Brandi Jessica Key (8), Pandora Paige Key (8), Maya (counselor, mid-20s)
- **Onstage animal:** None (Burden does not arrive until age 14)
- **Locations:** Birch Cabin, French Woods (Hancock, New York) — lower bunks area during quiet time/orientation
- **When:** July of Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige's eighth year (2053). The evening of the first full day at camp, right after the twins' first arrival. The twins are attending three back-to-back sessions — Session 2, Session 3, and Session 4 — the full summer. On the last day of each session, parents can visit their child; Kiley Rose and Julia will visit at the end of each session.
- **Status:** New scene outline
- **Tone:** Excited, collaborative, empowering, twin synchronization, “anything and everything” philosophy in action

## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene captures the exact moment the twins **choose their own French Woods schedules** for their first summer.

It establishes:
- The camp’s signature freedom: campers select three majors and three minors from a thick printed catalog.
- Pandora Paige’s role as Brandi’s eyes and co-pilot **at camp only** — she reads the catalog aloud, describes every option in rich detail, and helps Brandi weigh the choices.
- Brandi Jessica’s full agency and enthusiasm despite her blindness.
- The “without any single doubt” family philosophy in practice: the girls are trusted to pick for themselves.
- The specific disciplines that will define their French Woods experience for years to come.

The scene directly feeds the activities described in the twins’ character profiles and sets up future camp scenes (first ballet class, first aerial session, photography projects, etc.).

## BEATS

### Beat 1: Quiet Time in the Cabin
- After a full first day of check-in, cabin tour, and free swim, Maya announces quiet time before dinner.
- Most girls are outside or writing letters home. Brandi and Pandora sit on Brandi’s lower bunk with the thick French Woods catalog open between them.
- Pandora has already claimed the top bunk “so I can see everything and tell you what’s happening.”
- The cabin smells of pine and sunscreen. Sunlight filters through the screen windows.

### Beat 2: Pandora Reads the Catalog
- Pandora opens the catalog and begins reading the options aloud in her excited, descriptive voice.
- She describes majors and minors in vivid detail so Brandi can picture them:
  - “Ballet majors get the big sprung-floor pavilion with mirrors and barres…”
  - “Pointe class is in the same pavilion but they bring out the portable barres…”
  - “Swimming lessons are in the pool with the lane lines and the painted bottom…”
  - “Gymnastics majors work in the big gym with mats and beams…”
- Brandi listens intently, asking questions and running her fingers over the raised texture of the catalog cover.

### Beat 3: The Choosing Process
- Pandora reads section by section. Brandi asks clarifying questions and they discuss every possibility.
- They decide together, bouncing ideas back and forth.
- Pandora helps Brandi visualize physical requirements (e.g., “For pointe you have to be up on your toes a lot — but you already love the feeling of being balanced from ballet”).
- Brandi makes the final call on her own classes; Pandora makes hers.
- They write their choices on the official schedule form with a marker (Pandora guides Brandi’s hand for the signature line).

### Beat 4: Final Selections
- **Brandi Jessica’s choices** (three majors + three minors):
  - Majors: Ballet, Pointe, [Major 3 — TBD]
  - Minors: Yoga, Waterfront Recreation, Swimming Lessons
- **Pandora Paige’s choices** (three majors + three minors):
  - Majors: Gymnastics, Circus, [Major 3 — TBD]
  - Minors: Gymnastics, Waterfront Recreation, [Minor 3 — TBD, different from Brandi's]

**Note on minors:** Campers can choose new minors every day. The twins' minor selections are different from each other. Brandi chooses Swimming Lessons as one of her minors (in the swimming pool). They can always try different ones on other days.

### Beat 5: Pandora’s Help in Action
- When Brandi hesitates about choosing the same Yoga minor twice, Pandora laughs and says, “It’s allowed because you can pick new minors every day. You love yoga — do it twice if you want.”
- For their shared Swimming Lessons major and Waterfront Recreation minor, Pandora immediately starts talking about how she can help Brandi with alignment and spatial awareness at the lake and pool.
- They talk about how they can sometimes be in the same pavilion even if their majors are different.

### Beat 6: The Moment It Becomes Real
- Maya returns and collects the completed forms.
- Pandora reads the final list back to Brandi one more time.
- Brandi smiles and says something like, “We’re really going to do all of it.”
- Pandora replies, “Every single one. No one’s going to tell us we can’t.”

### Beat 7: Quiet Excitement
- The girls lie on their bunks whispering about the classes until lights-out.
- Pandora promises to describe every costume, every set, every audience reaction.
- Brandi promises to let Pandora take as many photos as she wants.
