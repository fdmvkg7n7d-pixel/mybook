# The Airport Video — Family Viewing

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Characters:** Andrea Jennifer, Ashley Erin, Kiley Rose Key, Julia, Pandora Paige Key, Mike Key, Terri Anne Key
- **Offstage / referenced characters:** Brandi Jessica Key (seven years old), Andrew (the kidnapper), Greenlee (Andrew's cousin)
- **Location:** Kiley Rose and Julia's townhouse, New York City
- **When:** January 5, 2052 — just after Brandi Jessica's flight to the UAE
- **Status:** New scene outline
- **Tone:** Tense, desperate, the first confirmed visual proof that Brandi Jessica is alive, the horror of seeing her in transit, the identical twin recognition

---

## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene takes place in Kiley Rose and Julia's townhouse, just after the flight to the UAE. The police send a video to Mike. The video is ten seconds long. It shows Brandi Jessica at the airport, in transit, with Andrew and Greenlee hiding their faces.

The family is gathered: Andrea Jennifer, Ashley Erin, Kiley Rose, Julia, Pandora Paige, Mike, and Terri Anne.

Pandora Paige — Brandi Jessica's identical twin — notices Brandi Jessica's face in the video. As identical twins, they share the same face. Pandora recognizes her own face on Brandi Jessica, even in a ten-second clip, even at an airport, even with the captors hiding their own faces.

The scene establishes the family's first visual confirmation that Brandi Jessica is alive and being transported somewhere. It also establishes the identical twin bond — Pandora seeing her own face on her sister, recognizing her even when others might not.

## SCENE ARC / BEATS

### Beat 1: Before the Video — Should Pandora Go to School?
- Before the police send the video, Kiley Rose, Mike, and Terri Anne have a conversation about Pandora Paige's schooling.
- Pandora has been missing school since the kidnapping ten days ago. She has been inconsolable, clinging to Kiley Rose and Julia, completely out of it.
- Kiley Rose is struggling with whether to send Pandora back to school. She doesn't want to leave her alone, doesn't want her to be away from the family while Brandi Jessica is still missing. But she also doesn't want Pandora to fall behind academically, doesn't want her to lose the stability that school provides.
- Mike and Terri Anne are visiting, trying to support the family through this crisis. They weigh in on the conversation about whether Pandora should return to school.
- The conversation is interrupted when the police send the video to Mike.

### Beat 2: The Video Arrives
- The police send the video to Mike Key.
- The video is ten seconds long. It was captured at the airport — surveillance footage, or footage from another source.
- The video shows Brandi Jessica at the airport, in transit. She is walking between two adults — Andrew and Greenlee — who are hiding their faces from the camera.
- Brandi Jessica is visible. She is wearing regular clothes. She is holding an adult's hand. She looks like a normal child traveling with her parents.
- But she is not with her parents. She is with her captors.

### Beat 3: The Family Gathers
- Mike calls the family together at Kiley Rose and Julia's townhouse.
- Andrea Jennifer, Ashley Erin, Kiley Rose, Julia, Pandora Paige, and Terri Anne are all present.
- They gather around a screen — a laptop, a tablet, a television — to watch the video the police sent.
- The mood is tense. They have been searching for Brandi Jessica for ten days. They have had no visual confirmation that she is alive.
- This is the first video they have seen of her since the kidnapping.

### Beat 4: The Video Plays
- The video is ten seconds long.
- It shows Brandi Jessica at the airport. She is walking between two adults. The adults are hiding their faces from the camera — they are turned away, or using bags, or positioning themselves so their faces are not visible.
- Brandi Jessica is visible. She is wearing regular clothes — not the black dress, not the nylons. She is dressed like a normal child traveling with her parents.
- She is holding an adult's hand. She does not look like she is being forced. She does not look like she is crying. She looks like a child being led through an airport.
- But she is not with her parents. She is with her captors.

### Beat 5: The Reactions
- Kiley Rose sees Brandi Jessica. She sees her daughter. She sees that she is alive. She sees that she is being transported somewhere.
- Julia sees Brandi Jessica. She sees the same thing. She sees that she is alive. She sees that she is being taken somewhere.
- Mike sees the video. He sees his granddaughter. He sees the captors hiding their faces. He sees that the police have confirmed she is in transit.
- Terri Anne sees the video. She sees the captors. She sees the airport. She sees the family's granddaughter being taken somewhere.
- Andrea Jennifer and Ashley Erin see the video. They see their niece. They see that she is alive.
- The family's reaction is a mix of relief and horror. Relief that Brandi Jessica is alive. Horror that she is being taken somewhere. Horror that the captors are hiding their faces. Horror that they cannot stop it.
- The family does not recognize the captors. They see two adults hiding their faces, but they cannot identify who they are. They don't recognize them as people they know. They don't connect them to anyone from the family's past or present. The captors remain anonymous in the video.

### Beat 6: Pandora Paige Notices Brandi Jessica's Face
- Pandora Paige has been pretty out of it — numb, dissociated, checked out — since her sister was taken. Ten days of not knowing, of holding onto Kiley Rose or Julia, of being inconsolable. She has been clinging to her mothers, not speaking much, not engaging with the world around her. She is there physically but not fully present.
- Then the video plays. And Pandora Paige snaps to attention.
- She is Brandi Jessica's identical twin. They share the same face.
- Pandora notices Brandi Jessica's face. She sees her own face on her sister. She recognizes it immediately, even in a ten-second clip, even at an airport, even with the captors hiding their own faces.
- Pandora speaks up. She says something — she points out Brandi Jessica, or she says her sister's name, or she says something that makes everyone else realize what they're seeing. Her voice cuts through the numbness.
- The out-of-it-ness falls away. Pandora is suddenly sharp, focused, fully present. She sees her sister. She sees her own face on her sister's body.
- Pandora sees something in Brandi Jessica's face that the others might not see. She sees the way Brandi Jessica is holding herself. She sees the way she is walking. She sees the way she is holding the adult's hand.
- Pandora recognizes her sister. She recognizes her own face on her sister. She recognizes that Brandi Jessica is alive, and she recognizes that Brandi Jessica is being taken somewhere, and she recognizes that Brandi Jessica is not okay.
- The identical twin bond is absolute. Pandora sees her own face on Brandi Jessica, and she knows — she knows that Brandi Jessica is alive, and she knows that Brandi Jessica is being taken somewhere, and she knows that Brandi Jessica is not okay.

### Beat 7: What Comes Next
- The video is ten seconds long. It ends.
- The family has seen it. They have seen Brandi Jessica. They have seen that she is alive. They have seen that she is being transported somewhere.
- The police tell Mike that they will continue investigating.
- Kiley Rose and Julia are frustrated with the investigation. They want answers, they want action, they want their daughter back. The police saying they'll "continue investigating" is not enough.
- Kiley Rose and Julia ask Mike and Terri Anne to do their own investigation to find Brandi Jessica. They need more than the police can provide.
- Mike tells Kiley Rose and Julia that he is already doing their own investigation. He and Terri Anne are not just waiting for the police — they are actively working to find Brandi Jessica through their own channels.
- But the family does not know where Brandi Jessica is going. They do not know who the captors are. They do not know what will happen to her.
- They only know that she is alive. They only know that she is being taken somewhere. They only know that they cannot stop it.
