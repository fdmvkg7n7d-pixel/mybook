# Brandi Jessica Tells Kiley Rose and Julia — Las Vegas Visit Scene Outline

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Characters present:** Brandi Jessica Key (21), Pandora Paige Key (21), Kiley Rose Key, Julia
- **Only people present:** Brandi Jessica, Pandora Paige, Kiley Rose, and Julia
- **Offstage / referenced only:** Jamal Al-Otaibi, Mohammed, Diederik, Imam Kareem, Bassem
- **Location:** Brandi Jessica’s penthouse, Las Vegas, Nevada
- **When:** UNLV years, ~2065–2066 — after Brandi Jessica ends her relationship with Mohammed
- **Situation:** Kiley Rose and Julia travel from New York City to Las Vegas to visit Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige. Pandora remains in Las Vegas long enough to be present for the family conversation.
- **Status:** Scene outline
- **Tone:** In-person, private, twin-centered, honest, protective, cautiously hopeful

---

## SCENE PURPOSE

After Brandi Jessica ends her three-year relationship with Mohammed, **Kiley Rose and Julia visit Brandi Jessica and Pandora Paige in Las Vegas**.

During the visit, Brandi tells her mothers that she has begun a **new and fresh relationship with Jamal**. Pandora sits beside her and supports her, but Brandi speaks for herself.

Jamal is not present. He does not come to the penthouse, join by phone, or introduce himself. This first family disclosure belongs only to the four women: Kiley Rose and Julia visiting their two daughters in person.

Brandi does not pretend the relationship began cleanly. She admits that she became involved with Jamal before ending things with Mohammed. Kiley Rose and Julia love their daughter without excusing the betrayal. Their concern is whether Brandi can make the new relationship genuinely fresh through honesty, accountability, respect, and better choices.

---

## SCENE ARC / BEATS

### Beat 1: Kiley Rose and Julia Arrive in Las Vegas

- Kiley Rose and Julia fly from New York City to Las Vegas after Brandi tells them that her relationship with Mohammed has ended.
- Brandi has not yet told them about Jamal. She said only that she was safe, Mohammed was gone, and she wanted to explain everything in person.
- Pandora extends her Las Vegas stay so she can remain with Brandi during the visit; she will fly to Los Angeles afterward.
- Kiley Rose and Julia arrive at Brandi’s penthouse with small suitcases. They are staying for several days, not dropping in for an hour.
- Brandi opens the door and immediately reaches for both women. Kiley hugs her first; Julia folds herself around Brandi and Kiley together.
- Pandora stands close until Kiley pulls her into the embrace too.
- No one else is in the penthouse. **Only Kiley Rose, Julia, Brandi Jessica, and Pandora Paige are present.**
- Kiley holds Brandi’s face between her hands and asks:

> “Are you safe?”

- Brandi answers:

> “Yes. Mohammed is gone. I ended it.”

- Kiley says, *“Then we can talk when you’re ready.”* She does not demand the entire story at the door.

### Beat 2: The Four-Woman Evening

- The first hours of the visit are deliberately ordinary.
- Julia unpacks groceries she and Kiley picked up after landing.
- Pandora makes coffee despite the late hour because travel has confused everyone’s internal clock.
- Kiley walks through the penthouse with Brandi, listening while Brandi describes what has changed since their last visit.
- The four women eat together at the kitchen island. There is no restaurant crowd and no boyfriend present to influence the conversation.
- Kiley and Julia ask after both daughters:
  - Brandi’s UNLV classes and mobility routines.
  - Pandora’s work trip and upcoming flight to Los Angeles.
  - Whether Mohammed has a key or any belongings left in the penthouse.
- Brandi confirms that Mohammed no longer has access. The door code has been changed.
- Julia notices Brandi and Pandora exchanging small, silent twin signals. She understands that the breakup is not the entire reason they were invited.
- When the dishes are cleared, Julia asks gently:

> “What haven’t you told us yet?”

### Beat 3: Brandi Asks Everyone to Sit

- Brandi asks them to move to the living room.
- Kiley Rose and Julia sit together on one couch. Brandi and Pandora sit opposite them, close enough that their shoulders touch.
- Pandora takes Brandi’s hand but says nothing.
- Brandi begins:

> “Mohammed and I are finished. That part is final. But there is someone else.”

- Kiley’s expression changes. Julia remains still and attentive.
- Brandi says the name clearly:

> “Jamal Al-Otaibi.”

- Kiley asks whether Jamal is the reason Brandi ended the relationship with Mohammed.
- Brandi answers carefully:

> “He helped me understand that I wanted out. But I should have ended it before anything happened with him. I didn’t.”

### Beat 4: The Complete Truth About Mohammed

- Julia asks the direct question:

> “Were you with Jamal before you ended things with Mohammed?”

- Brandi tightens her grip on Pandora’s hand.

> “Yes.”

- She tells them that Jamal was Mohammed’s closest friend and brother through their mosque.
- She admits that Mohammed caught her and Jamal together in the penthouse.
- Brandi does not provide intimate details. The emotional betrayal is the point.
- She tells Kiley Rose and Julia that Mohammed left and that their relationship ended that night.
- She also tells them that she and Jamal went to Imam Kareem afterward. They admitted what they had done instead of waiting for community gossip to reach him first.
- Brandi says:

> “Mohammed was awful to me. I should have left him much earlier. But what he did to me does not make what I did right.”

- Pandora remains beside her without interrupting. Support does not mean rescuing Brandi from accountability.

### Beat 5: Kiley Rose Responds as a Mother Who Has Been Betrayed

- Kiley Rose has lived through betrayal in her own relationships. Brandi’s confession hurts her.
- She asks once more, making certain no one is hiding behind gentle language:

> “You cheated on Mohammed with his best friend?”

- Brandi answers, *“Yes.”*
- Kiley takes a breath before speaking.

> “I love you. That has not changed. It cannot change. But loving you does not require me to pretend that was right.”

- Brandi says, *“I know.”*
- Kiley tells her that Mohammed’s cruelty may explain why Brandi was desperate to leave, but it does not excuse how she and Jamal began.
- She is not rejecting Brandi or demanding that she return to Mohammed. The relationship with Mohammed is over, and Kiley accepts that ending it was necessary.
- Her concern is that Brandi not build a future by rewriting the past.

### Beat 6: “It’s New, and It Feels Fresh”

- Julia asks what Brandi wants them to understand about Jamal now.
- Brandi’s voice becomes lighter, though she remains serious.

> “We’re together now. It’s new, and it feels fresh.”

- Kiley asks what *fresh* means when the relationship began with secrecy.
- Brandi answers:

> “It doesn’t mean the beginning was clean. It means Mohammed and I are over. Jamal and I aren’t hiding anymore. I told Pandora. I’m telling both of you. I want the next part to be honest.”

- She explains how this relationship feels different from Diederik and Mohammed:
  - Jamal asks before helping her.
  - He does not treat her blindness as helplessness.
  - He respects her independence.
  - He listens when she speaks about her Catholic faith.
  - He does not expect her to convert to Islam.
  - He admits when he is wrong instead of making Brandi doubt her own memory.
  - Brandi feels calm with him rather than controlled or diminished.
- Brandi says:

> “I feel like myself with him. Not someone difficult he has agreed to tolerate. Myself.”

### Beat 7: Pandora Tells Them What She Saw

- Kiley asks Pandora whether she has met Jamal.
- Pandora says she met him properly over dinner in Las Vegas the previous evening.
- Pandora does not promise that one good dinner proves the relationship will last.

> “I can’t tell you who he’ll be in five years. I can tell you who he was with her last night.”

- Pandora describes what she observed:
  - Jamal described the restaurant without controlling Brandi’s movements.
  - He read the menu when Brandi asked and waited for her decision.
  - He asked before touching or guiding her.
  - He spoke directly to Brandi rather than asking Pandora to answer for her.
  - His care seemed ordinary and practiced, not performed to impress Brandi’s twin.
- Pandora says:

> “He is gentle with her without making her small.”

- Kiley asks Pandora:

> “Is he good to her? Really good?”

- Pandora answers:

> “From what I have seen, yes.”

- Julia then asks an equally important question:

> “Is Brandi good to him?”

- Pandora looks at her twin before answering.

> “She wants to be. She knows she has to prove that in what she does next.”

- Brandi accepts the answer: *“She’s right.”*

### Beat 8: Questions About the New Relationship

- Because Jamal is not present, Brandi must answer every question herself.
- Kiley Rose and Julia ask:
  - Jamal’s age: twenty-one.
  - His home: raised in Reno, now living in Las Vegas.
  - His background: Uyghur; his mother is Chinese Uyghur and his father is Saudi Arabian.
  - His faith: Muslim.
  - Whether he expects Brandi to convert: no.
  - Whether Mohammed knows the relationship is continuing: yes.
  - Whether Jamal has accepted responsibility for betraying Mohammed: yes; he spoke honestly with Imam Kareem.
  - Whether Brandi trusts Jamal: she is beginning to, but she knows trust must be built after a secret beginning.
- Brandi does not make promises about marriage or forever.

> “It’s fresh, Mom. It isn’t finished. I’m not asking you to believe in forever tonight.”

- Julia appreciates the answer because it treats the relationship as new rather than magically complete.

### Beat 9: Kiley Rose and Julia Set Their Expectations

- Kiley tells Brandi that freshness must be demonstrated through choices.

> “Fresh does not mean untouched, sweetheart. It means you stop carrying the old rot forward.”

- Kiley’s expectations:
  - No more hiding.
  - No using Mohammed’s cruelty to excuse betrayal.
  - No allowing Jamal to decide what Brandi can or cannot do.
  - No shrinking Brandi’s Catholic faith to make the relationship easier.
- Julia’s expectations:
  - Brandi must ask clearly for what she needs.
  - She must respect Jamal’s Muslim faith as she expects him to respect hers.
  - Conflict must be spoken before secrecy becomes attractive.
  - Brandi must remain responsible for her own choices.
- Brandi agrees without arguing.
- Kiley tells her:

> “You are my daughter. You do not lose your family because you made a bad choice. But you do have to become better than the choice.”

- Brandi’s composure breaks. She begins to cry quietly.
- Pandora puts an arm around her but allows Brandi to answer for herself.

> “I’m trying.”

- Julia moves from the other couch and kneels in front of Brandi.

> “Then keep trying. We love you.”

### Beat 10: The Visit Becomes a Family Embrace

- Kiley joins Julia beside Brandi. Pandora remains pressed against Brandi’s other side.
- The four women fold into a close family embrace in the Las Vegas living room.
- Kiley holds both twins, remembering them as newborns sharing one crib and as seven-year-olds facing Brandi’s cancer together.
- Julia tells Brandi that ending an unhealthy relationship was the right decision even though the way she began the next one caused harm.
- Kiley tells Pandora she is grateful that she stayed in Las Vegas for her sister.
- Pandora answers:

> “She asked me to be here. So I’m here.”

- No one suggests calling Jamal. No one invites him over. This evening remains only for Kiley Rose, Julia, Brandi Jessica, and Pandora Paige.

### Beat 11: The Meeting Is Set for the Penthouse

- Once everyone settles, Julia says she and Kiley are willing to meet Jamal during their Las Vegas visit—but not on the same evening as Brandi’s disclosure.
- Brandi agrees that she does not want him there tonight.

> “Tonight is ours. I needed to tell you without him speaking for me.”

- Kiley says that was the right choice.
- Brandi asks whether Jamal can come to the penthouse the following evening. Meeting at home gives Brandi control of the space and keeps the introduction private.
- Kiley and Julia agree.
- Pandora says she will remain in Las Vegas and attend the meeting too.
- Kiley answers, *“Both my girls at the table.”*
- The next evening at Brandi Jessica’s penthouse becomes the agreed first meeting with Jamal.

### Beat 12: The Four of Them Stay Up Late

- The emotional conversation gives way to a quiet family night.
- Julia makes tea.
- Kiley orders late-night food for the four of them.
- Brandi and Pandora sit together under a light blanket while Kiley and Julia tell stories from New York.
- The mothers are not leaving immediately. They will stay through the next evening’s meeting with Jamal and the following morning’s breakfast, then fly to Panama City.
- Before bed, Kiley kisses Brandi’s forehead and says:

> “The relationship is new. Our love for you isn’t.”

- Brandi reaches for Pandora’s hand again.
- The secret is gone. Mohammed’s relationship with Brandi is over. Jamal is not present, but the family now knows that Brandi has chosen to begin something new with him.
