## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene follows **the Imam scene** (`brandi-jessica-and-jamal-tell-the-imam-scene-outline.md`).

**Pandora Paige is in Las Vegas for work with Key Porn Studios, performing under her professional name Violet, staying with Brandi Jessica for the week.** She lives with her boyfriend Bassem in **Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates**.

Pandora and Brandi share a bed together during the visit, like when they were babies — per canon, Kiley Rose nursed the twins and used the same crib so they would be close from the beginning.

Late at night, in bed together, Brandi tells her identical twin sister everything: the end of Mohammed, the beginning with Jamal, the catch in the shower, going to the Imam together.

Pandora is supportive immediately. She knows exactly how Diederik and Mohammed hurt Brandi. She suspected something was happening with Jamal — twins know — and she is not angry, she is relieved. This is the sister confirmation beat that locks Brandi/Jamal in as real within the Key family, before Brandi tells Kiley Rose and Julia.

The next evening, Jamal takes both women out to dinner at a Saudi restaurant in Las Vegas, where Pandora meets Jamal as Brandi's boyfriend, publicly, for the first time.

## SCENE ARC / BEATS

### Beat 1: The Penthouse — Sisters Sharing a Bed
- Night, Brandi Jessica's Las Vegas penthouse — privately purchased by Kiley Rose and Julia after Brandi completed her first year in a UNLV dormitory; it is not university housing.
- Pandora Paige is in Las Vegas for a Key Porn Studios shoot, performing under her professional name Violet, flying in from Abu Dhabi where she lives with her boyfriend Bassem, and is staying with Brandi for the week. She is not sleeping in the guest room — the twins share Brandi's bed together, like when they were babies. Kiley Rose nursed them together and kept them in the same crib so they would be close from the beginning; twenty-one years later, in Las Vegas, they still fit together the same way.
- The penthouse is quiet. Burden is off harness, curled on his bed on the floor beside them. Brandi's white cane is folded and stowed by the front door — she does not use cane and guide dog simultaneously.
- Pandora has been in town for three days for her Key Porn Studios shoot. Brandi has been waiting for the right quiet moment to tell her.

### Beat 2: Telling Pandora — In Bed
- Late, lights out, both twins on their backs staring at a ceiling only one of them can see.
- Brandi tells her everything, plain and unvarnished, the way twins talk in the dark: Mohammed was awful to me, same as Diederik was. I met Jamal. He's Mohammed's best friend, his temple brother. I fell for him while I was still with Mohammed. Mohammed caught us in the shower here, three nights ago. He blew up, he left. Jamal and I went to see Imam Kareem yesterday.
- Brandi is calm. No tears, no apology tour. This is not a confession — this is a report to her other half.
- Pandora listens in the dark, holding Brandi's hand under the blanket. She nods against the pillow — not surprised.

### Beat 3: Supportive Immediately — "I Know"
- Pandora squeezes Brandi's hand in the dark.
- She tells Brandi: *I know how Diederik hurt you. I know how Mohammed hurt you. I watched both of them do it.*
- Pandora suspected about Jamal. She heard it in Brandi's voice the last two months on calls — a different lightness, a different laugh. Twins know. She didn't ask because it was Brandi's to tell.
- She is not angry about the cheating. She is relieved Brandi is out of the Mohammed relationship. *"You deserve someone who isn't awful to you."*
- She asks one question about Jamal: *"Is he good to you? Really good?"* Brandi: *"Yes."* Pandora: *"Then I'm good."*
- No lecture about the overlap, no moralizing about the temple brother betrayal. That was for Imam Kareem. Pandora's job is sister, not judge.
- They fall asleep side by side, like the crib, like babies. Burden sighs from his bed on the floor.

### Beat 4: Twin Processing — Morning After
- Morning, kitchen. Coffee.
- Pandora walks Brandi through what happens next, practical twin-mode: Have you told Mom and Julia yet? Brandi has told them only that her relationship with Mohammed is over, that she is safe, and that she wants to explain the rest in person.
- Kiley Rose and Julia are flying from New York City to visit both daughters in Las Vegas. Pandora rearranges her travel so she can remain for the visit and fly to Los Angeles afterward: *"You tell them. I’ll sit beside you. I’m not leaving before they get here."*
- They talk about what to tell the family, how to frame it, and why they cannot hide the painful overlap with Mohammed. Pandora will back Brandi completely because she is her twin and knows the full history of Brandi’s relationships—not because she owes Brandi a medical debt. Pandora never had retinoblastoma. She tells Brandi: *"I’ll sit beside you. You tell them what happened, and I’ll help them understand what I saw."*
- Burden nudges his head into Brandi's lap. Both sisters pat him — Brandi scratching behind his ears by touch, Pandora running her hand down his back. Quiet grounding moment, same as after the Mohammed confrontation.
- Pandora asks to meet Jamal properly before her Las Vegas stay ends and she flies to Los Angeles. Brandi smiles: *"He's taking us out tonight. You're going to love him."*

### Beat 5: Dinner — Saudi Restaurant, Las Vegas
- That evening, Jamal takes Brandi and Pandora out to dinner at a Saudi restaurant in Las Vegas.
- [Author's note — real-world 2026 Las Vegas: there is no dedicated top-ranked Saudi restaurant in the city in current listings. Closest highly-rated Halal Middle Eastern options include: Shawarma Vegas – 4.8★, 8665 W Flamingo Rd [2](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Restaurants-g45963-c30-Las_Vegas_Nevada.html), Shish Kabob House – 4.5★, 6620 W Flamingo Rd [2](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Restaurants-g45963-c30-Las_Vegas_Nevada.html), and Hafez Persian in Chinatown, often recommended by locals [1](https://www.reddit.com/r/vegas/comments/1kac63x/zaytoon_is_a_hidden_gem_for_middle_eastern_food/). For the 2065 story setting, using a fictional Saudi restaurant — e.g. 'Al Najd, Las Vegas' — is recommended, or swap in one of the real Halal spots above and treat as Saudi-owned in canon.]
- In story: Jamal guides Brandi through the restaurant — describing the room, reading the menu aloud for her, plating food so she can find everything by touch. Pandora watches: this is how a man should treat her sister. This is the proof.
- Pandora meets Jamal as Brandi's boyfriend, publicly, for the first time — not as Mohammed's best friend, as Brandi's partner.
- The three of them eat together, easy conversation, laughter. Burden lies quietly under the table at Brandi's feet — guide dog, no cane in use.
- End beat: Back at the penthouse, Brandi and Pandora prepare for Kiley Rose and Julia’s visit. Pandora confirms her changed flight; she will remain in Las Vegas until after Brandi tells their mothers in person. Jamal will not be present.
- The sister blessing is given. Next up: Kiley Rose and Julia arrive from New York to visit Brandi and Pandora in Las Vegas.
