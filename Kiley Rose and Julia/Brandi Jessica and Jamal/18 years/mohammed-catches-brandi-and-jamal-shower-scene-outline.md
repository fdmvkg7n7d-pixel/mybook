- **Tone:** Adult, intimate, explosive confrontation, betrayal, liberation

## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene outlines **the end of the Mohammed / Jamal overlap in Brandi Jessica's relationship arc**, when Mohammed catches Brandi Jessica and Jamal together in her Las Vegas penthouse.

Brandi Jessica, age 21 and attending UNLV, has been dating Mohammed since she was eighteen. Mohammed is described in canon as "awful to Brandi Jessica." While still with Mohammed, Brandi began a secret relationship with Jamal at age twenty-one. 

Mohammed and Jamal are best friends and temple brothers — they pray at the same temple and grew up together. This makes the betrayal twofold.

The scene takes place in Brandi Jessica's penthouse in Las Vegas — **privately purchased by Kiley Rose and Julia after Brandi’s first year in a UNLV dormitory, for her later university years; it is not university housing.** Her guide dog Burden is home in the penthouse with her.

Mohammed is supposed to be out of town on a family trip and is not due back until the following day. Brandi and Jamal take advantage of the empty penthouse. While they are in the shower together, Mohammed comes home early. The running water and bedroom music mask his entry — Brandi, who navigates the world by sound, does not hear him come in. Burden knows Mohammed well from three years of dating and does not give an alert bark at the door.

Mohammed discovers them together in the shower, leading to an explosive confrontation centered on the temple brother / best friend betrayal. This is the hard break that ends the Mohammed relationship and transitions Brandi into her public relationship with Jamal — the father of all three of her future children: Iyeshia Caesarea Key (~2071), Jamal Mohammed Junior Key (~2074), and Kacy Kaniesa Key (~2077).

## SCENE ARC / BEATS

### Beat 1: The Penthouse — Mohammed Out of Town
- Late afternoon, Brandi Jessica's penthouse, Las Vegas, Nevada.
- The penthouse was purchased by Kiley Rose and Julia after Brandi completed her first year in a UNLV dormitory. It is her later-university home and is not university housing. Brandi knows every acoustic corner by memory — tile echo in the bathroom, the way the bedroom door clicks, the hum of the AC.
- Her guide dog Burden is home in the penthouse with her.
- Mohammed is supposed to be out of town — family obligation — not due back until tomorrow. Brandi knows his flight schedule, his key code, his routine.
- This is why Brandi let Jamal stay over. For weeks their secret relationship has been confined to quick meetups, his place, car rides. Tonight they have the penthouse to themselves.

### Beat 2: The Shower — Intimacy
- Brandi Jessica and Jamal in the master shower together. Adult consensual intimacy.
- Water running full. Steam. Music low from the bedroom speaker — covers small sounds.
- This is not their first time — the affair has been ongoing. There's ease between them, laughter, real affection. Contrast with Mohammed, who canon establishes was "awful to Brandi Jessica."
- For Brandi, who navigates by sound, the shower is a rare white-noise blind spot. She does not hear the front door.
- Jamal's hands guiding her in the slick tile space she knows by touch. Brandi's comfort and trust with him is total.

### Beat 3: The Discovery
- Mohammed comes home early. His trip was cut short.
- He has a key / code to Brandi's penthouse — three-year boyfriend access. Burden knows Mohammed well and does not give an alert bark.
- Mohammed enters quietly, expecting to surprise her. Calls her name. No answer over the shower / music.
- He follows the sound of running water to the master bath. The bathroom door is ajar, steam spilling out.
- He pushes it open and sees them together in the glass shower — Brandi and Jamal.
- The betrayal is doubled:
  1. His girlfriend cheating
  2. The man she's with is Jamal — his best friend, his temple brother. They pray at the same temple. They grew up together.

### Beat 4: Explosive Confrontation
- Mohammed erupts. Not quiet devastation — explosive.
- The core wound he screams: *"My brother. My temple brother. In her shower."*
- Jamal kills the water, grabs a towel, steps between Brandi and Mohammed — protective.
- Brandi, blind, dripping wet, disoriented — she heard the bathroom door slam before she understood what was happening. She can't see Mohammed's face, only hears his voice breaking with rage.
- Mohammed's rage is aimed primarily at Jamal — the friend betrayal cuts deeper than the girlfriend betrayal. Accusations of violating their faith, their brotherhood, the temple community trust.
- Jamal doesn't deny it. Doesn't make excuses. "It happened. It's real."
- Brandi, wrapped in a towel, cane nowhere in reach, stands her ground: she doesn't beg Mohammed to stay. She tells him the truth — Mohammed was awful to her, Jamal wasn't, and she's not sorry she chose him.
- Mohammed throws Jamal's clothes at him, tells them both they're dead to him, storms out. Slams the penthouse door hard enough that Brandi feels the vibration through the tile floor.

### Beat 5: Aftermath
- Silence in the wet bathroom except the drip of the showerhead.
- Jamal helps Brandi find her cane. He's shaking. She's calm — calmer than he expected.
- Brandi: "He was supposed to be gone until tomorrow."
- Jamal: "He knows now."
- Brandi: "Good."
- Burden comes to Brandi in the bathroom doorway, nudging her wet hand with his nose. Brandi drops to her knees on the tile and pats him, running her hands through his fur, grounding herself. Jamal kneels beside her and pats Burden too — quiet, steady, together.
- The secret is out. The overlap is over.
- No more hiding. Brandi and Jamal are public from this night forward.
- This is the hard break that transitions Brandi from the Mohammed relationship into the Jamal relationship that will produce Iyeshia Caesarea (~2071), Jamal Mohammed Junior (~2074), and Kacy Kaniesa (~2077).
