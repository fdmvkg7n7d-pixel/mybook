## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene outlines **Patti Anne Key and her six-year-old son, Gamaliel Galvin’s complete twelve-day Christmas vacation in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.** 

Despite carrying the heavy anxiety of Brandi Jessica's retinoblastoma diagnosis in New York and her classmate Klára's dying mother back in Canada, Patti Anne is committed to giving Gamaliel his dream vacation to see where his biological father came from. 

Spanning **twelve days**, this outline establishes a detailed itinerary of their journey. Because **Aleksander is completely removed and not present during this period**, Patti Anne navigates this vacation with absolute, single-maternal strength. 

Crucially, **they fly out of Toronto Pearson Airport (YYZ) on an overnight flight on December 23, arriving in Rio de Janeiro in the morning on December 24,** allowing Gamaliel to sleep through the long journey and wake up to the sun rising over the tropical Brazilian coastline.

## TWELVE-DAY VACATION ITINERARY

### Day 1 (December 23): Evening Departure from Toronto YYZ (Overnight Flight)
- **Evening:** After receiving the heavy diagnostic phone call from Terri Anne at her mansion, Patti Anne decides to proceed with the trip to protect Gamaliel's dream. She tucks Klára in safely with a trusted caretaker/relative, packs their bags, and heads to Toronto Pearson International Airport (YYZ).
- **The Overnight Flight:** They board their **overnight flight from Toronto to Rio de Janeiro late on December 23.** Patti Anne keeps Gamaliel (six years old) cozy as the plane flies south, crossing the equator, while he sleeps soundly through the long journey. Patti Anne stays awake, looking out at the dark sky, praying for her family in New York and for Klára's mother.

### Day 2 (December 24): Morning Arrival in Rio & Quiet Beach Day
- **Morning Arrival:** The plane touches down at Galeão International Airport in Rio de Janeiro in the warm, bright morning of December 24. Gamaliel wakes up with absolute, pure childhood excitement to see the sun rising over the tropical palm trees.
- **Afternoon:** They check into the historic, luxury **Belmond Copacabana Palace** overlooking the beach. Gamaliel is completely mesmerized by the vast, deep blue Atlantic Ocean and the rhythmic, rolling waves.
- **Evening (Christmas Eve):** They enjoy a cozy, festive Christmas Eve dinner on their hotel balcony. They leave a tropical fruit treat out for Papai Noel. Patti Anne reads him a bedtime story about Brazil's natural wonders as he falls asleep early to the sound of the Pacific.

### Day 3 (December 25): Christmas Day & Gamaliel’s First Capoeira Class
- **Morning:** Christmas morning. Gamaliel wakes up to find a few beautifully wrapped gifts under their small in-room decorated palm tree. He is six years old, running around with absolute excitement.
- **The Beach of Ipanema:** They spend the warm, sunny Christmas morning on **Ipanema Beach**, enjoying the white sands and looking out at the majestic two Brothers mountains.
- **Afternoon:** Gamaliel has his **very first Capoeira class** on the beach. A local instructor guides him through the basic movements, part dance, part fight. Gamaliel giggles and tries his absolute best, his coordination and timing shining.
- **Evening:** A special Christmas dinner. Patti Anne FaceTimes Kiley and Julia in New York to wish the twins a merry Christmas, keeping her voice bright, full of love, and supportive.

### Day 4 (December 26): Exploring Corcovado and Christ the Redeemer
- **Morning:** They take the scenic cog train up through the lush Tijuca Forest to **Corcovado**.
- **The Summit:** They stand at the foot of the magnificent **Christ the Redeemer** statue. Gamaliel looks up at the colossal monument in absolute awe, holding Patti Anne’s hand tightly as they look down over the sweeping, breathtaking view of Guanabara Bay.
- **Evening:** Return to the hotel for a quiet dinner.

### Day 5 (December 27): Connecting with Paternal Roots in Santa Teresa
- **Morning:** Patti Anne takes Gamaliel on a historic yellow tram ride up the winding streets of the bohemian neighborhood of **Santa Teresa**.
- **Paternal Heritage:** This is the emotional peak of the trip. Patti Anne gently guides him through the streets where his father used to walk. She tells him sweet, age-appropriate stories about his father's childhood, his love for music, and his bravery.
- **Afternoon:** They sit in a shaded, quiet courtyard café. Gamaliel draws a picture of the yellow tram in his travel journal, feeling a deep, quiet connection to his roots.

### Day 6 (December 28): The Sugarloaf Mountain (Middle of the Vacation)
- **Morning:** **Scheduled exactly in the middle of their twelve-day vacation,** they take the glass-walled cable cars up to the summit of **Sugarloaf Mountain (Pão de Açúcar)**.
- **The View:** The two-stage cable ride is thrilling. Standing on the summit in the bright, warm afternoon sun, Gamaliel looks out at the ocean.
- **Sharing Joy with New York:** Patti Anne takes a beautiful photo of Gamaliel smiling proudly on the mountaintop and sends it to Kiley and Julia at the New York townhouse, sending their deepest, protective love. The photo brings a ray of warmth and comfort to Kiley during the crisis.
- **Evening:** A quiet, relaxing dinner by the hotel pool.

### Day 7 (December 29): Visiting the Selarón Steps
- **Morning:** They visit the famous, vibrant **Selarón Steps (Escadaria Selarón)** in Lapa.
- **The Colorful Tiles:** Gamaliel is fascinated by the thousands of brightly colored, hand-painted ceramic tiles from all over the world, pointing out the ones with fish and soccer balls.
- **Evening:** They have a cozy dinner at a traditional Brazilian churrascaria.

### Day 8 (December 30): Cable Car and Botanical Gardens
- **Morning:** They visit the lush **Rio de Janeiro Botanical Garden**, walking through the avenue of towering royal palms. Gamaliel counts the trees, his face full of wonder.
- **Afternoon:** They have a quiet picnic near the orchid house, letting Gamaliel draw in his journal.
- **Evening:** A quiet, restful evening in their room, packing some of their souvenirs.

### Day 9 (December 31): New Year’s Eve Copacabana Celebration
- **Morning:** A late, slow morning, letting Gamaliel rest and play.
- **Afternoon:** A gentle swim in the resort pool, keeping things relaxed.
- **Evening (New Year's Eve):** They experience the world-famous **Copacabana New Year's Eve Celebration**. Patti Anne, dressed in traditional white, takes Gamaliel down to the beach. They watch the magnificent, breathtaking fireworks over the ocean, with Gamaliel sitting securely on her shoulders, completely mesmerized by the lights.

### Day 10 (January 1): New Year's Day Rest and Lagoon Walk
- **Morning:** A late, slow New Year's Day breakfast in their room.
- **Afternoon:** They take a gentle, quiet walk around the **Rodrigo de Freitas Lagoon**, watching the paddleboats and enjoying the warm breeze.
- **Evening:** A quiet, comforting dinner at the hotel.

### Day 11 (January 2): The Final Beach Swim
- **Morning/Afternoon:** One final, long swim and play session at Copacabana Beach, with Gamaliel happily splashing in the warm waves, a confident and joyful little traveler.
- **Souvenirs:** They buy some special local souvenirs, including a small Brazilian instrument (a berimbau) for Gamaliel to practice his capoeira.
- **Evening:** They watch their final Rio sunset, taking a beautiful photo together on the sand.

### Day 12 (January 3): Departure to Toronto
- **Morning:** They check out of the Copacabana Palace, saying goodbye to the staff who made their stay so magical.
- **Afternoon:** They board their flight from Rio de Janeiro back to Toronto Pearson Airport.
- **Evening:** Arrive home in Vaughan. The search for Brandi Jessica is still unresolved in New York, and Klára's mother continues her heavy battle, but as Patti Anne holds her son close, she brings the unyielding warmth, strength, and protective love of their family, completely united.
