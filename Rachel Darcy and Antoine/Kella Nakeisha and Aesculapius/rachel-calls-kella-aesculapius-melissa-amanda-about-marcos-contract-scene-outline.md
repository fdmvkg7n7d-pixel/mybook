# SCENE OUTLINE: RACHEL CALLS KELLA, AESCULAPIUS, AND MELISSA AMANDA ABOUT MARCOS'S CONTRACT

## Placement

**When:** The same day Marcos Eduarte signs his professional contract with the Toronto Blue Jays.  
**Where:** Rachel's location TBD / Kella Nakeisha in **Mound House, Nevada**.  
**Characters:** Rachel Darcy Key, Kella Nakeisha Key-d'Aboville, Aesculapius, Melissa Amanda. Marcos Eduarte is present emotionally but not necessarily in the call.  
**Melissa Amanda's age:** Eighteen months.  
**Context:** Marcos has signed with the Toronto Blue Jays out of high school. Rachel begins telling the family. When she calls Kella Nakeisha, Kella is living in Mound House, Nevada with Aesculapius and eighteen-month-old Melissa Amanda. Their exact relationships to Kella are TBD. Kella and Aesculapius are very excited for Marcos, and Melissa Amanda participates in the excitement as a toddler.

---

## Scene Purpose

This scene establishes:

- Rachel calling Kella Nakeisha about Marcos's Toronto Blue Jays contract.
- Aesculapius and eighteen-month-old Melissa Amanda as part of Kella's immediate household in Mound House, Nevada.
- Kella and Aesculapius being genuinely excited for Marcos Eduarte, with Melissa Amanda reflecting the excitement in toddler ways.
- Kella's sisterly pride in Marcos as the sibling whose dream has become real.
- Another warm family response that contrasts with Armando Cortez Junior's cold, secretive, indifferent call from Bangkok.

---

## Emotional Core

**Shared excitement.**

Kella receives the news with real joy. Marcos's baseball dream is not abstract to her; she knows he has always been the sibling looking toward the mound while the rest of the family moved through dance, theater, gymnastics, and the arts. When Rachel tells her he has signed, Kella understands that this is not just a contract. It is Marcos becoming himself in public.

Aesculapius joins that joy fully. Melissa Amanda is only eighteen months old, so she does not understand the contract, but she understands the emotion in the room: Kella's voice lifting, Aesculapius reacting, the sudden brightness around the phone.

---

## Tone

Warm, excited, slightly breathless, and familial. The call can have overlapping adult voices, repetition of the news, and the happy chaos of people realizing something major has happened — plus Melissa Amanda's toddler sounds, clapping, or attempts to grab the phone.

---

## Scene Outline

### Beat 1 — Rachel Calls Kella

Rachel has already been carrying the emotional force of Marcos signing with the Toronto Blue Jays.

She calls Kella Nakeisha in Mound House, Nevada.

Kella answers in the middle of ordinary life with Aesculapius nearby and eighteen-month-old Melissa Amanda close enough to react to the adults' voices.

### Beat 2 — Rachel Says the News

Rachel tells her plainly:

> "Marcos signed. Toronto. The Blue Jays."

Kella may need Rachel to repeat it because the news is so large.

> "He signed? With Toronto?"

### Beat 3 — Kella Repeats It Out Loud

Kella turns toward Aesculapius, with Melissa Amanda nearby, and repeats the news:

> "Marcos signed with the Blue Jays."

This is the moment the call becomes shared.

Aesculapius registers the scale of the contract and the fact that Marcos is not going to university — he is going straight into professional baseball. Melissa Amanda reacts to the excitement rather than the facts: clapping, bouncing, repeating a word like "ball," or reaching for Kella's phone.

### Beat 4 — Kella's Sisterly Pride

Kella's pride should be specific.

She is not only proud because Marcos is successful. She is proud because she knows this dream was his own. In a family where so many children inherited or echoed the arts, Marcos chose the mound.

Possible line:

> "He made them see him."

Rachel hears that and feels understood.

### Beat 5 — Aesculapius and Melissa Amanda Join In

Aesculapius and Melissa Amanda should not feel like background names, but Melissa Amanda's age must shape her role.

Possible directions:

- Aesculapius asks what team level Marcos starts with or whether he goes to Dunedin first.
- Aesculapius offers congratulations for Rachel to pass on.
- Melissa Amanda claps because the adults are excited.
- Melissa Amanda says a toddler version of "ball" or "Marcos."
- Kella holds the phone where Melissa Amanda can babble toward Rachel.

### Beat 6 — Rachel Receives the Warmth

Rachel lets herself receive the excitement.

This is important because not every call will feel like this. Kella's joy gives Rachel a place to rest inside the news.

Rachel may say:

> "He'll love knowing you all reacted like this."

### Beat 7 — Message for Marcos

Kella gives Rachel a message for Marcos.

Aesculapius adds his own message. Melissa Amanda may babble, clap, or say one small word into the phone.

The call ends with Rachel carrying their excitement back into the signing day.
