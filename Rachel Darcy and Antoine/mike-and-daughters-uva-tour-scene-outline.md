# SCENE OUTLINE: MIKE TAKES HIS DAUGHTERS TO THE UNIVERSITY OF VIRGINIA TOUR

*Mike Key takes four of his daughters — Rachel Darcy (17), Kiley Rose (15), Sabrina Bryna (13), and Fallon Glenda (10) — to Charlottesville, Virginia for a tour of the University of Virginia. The trip is both a serious educational opportunity and a warm, bonding experience between father and daughters. Rachel Darcy is especially interested in studying dance at UVA.*

---

## CANON BACKDROP & PLACEMENT

- **Characters Present:**
  - **Mike Key** (father)
  - **Rachel Darcy Key** (17) — interested in studying dance
  - **Kiley Rose Key** (15)
  - **Sabrina Bryna Key** (13)
  - **Fallon Glenda Key** (10)
- **Location:** University of Virginia (UVA), Charlottesville, Virginia
- **Purpose:** Campus tour with a focus on academic and artistic programs
- **Tone:** Warm and bonding, while also serious and educational

---

## SCENE PURPOSE & THEMATIC CORE

This scene highlights Mike Key’s active role as a father who invests time in his daughters’ futures. It balances the excitement of a college visit with genuine father-daughter connection. Rachel Darcy’s interest in dance is foregrounded as she begins to seriously consider her path after high school.

---

## SCENE OUTLINE — BEATS & PROSE CUES

### Beat 1 — The Drive to Charlottesville

Mike drives the four girls from the family estate to Charlottesville. The car ride is lively. Rachel Darcy sits in the front seat, quietly reviewing information about UVA’s dance program on her tablet. Kiley Rose and Sabrina Bryna chat in the back, while Fallon Glenda occasionally asks questions about college life.

Mike keeps the mood light but purposeful, asking each daughter what they’re most curious about seeing on the tour.

### Beat 2 — Arrival on Campus

They arrive at the University of Virginia and join a guided campus tour. Mike walks alongside his daughters, listening attentively as the guide explains UVA’s history, academic programs, and student life. Rachel Darcy takes notes on her phone.

The group visits the Lawn, the Rotunda, and several academic buildings. Mike points out architectural details and shares quiet observations with his daughters.

### Beat 3 — The Dance Program Discussion

During the tour, the group visits the area near the dance facilities. Rachel Darcy becomes visibly more engaged. She asks the tour guide specific questions about the dance program, performance opportunities, and faculty.

Mike watches his daughter with quiet pride. Later, when they have a moment alone, he asks her gently:

> “You’ve been thinking about dance a lot lately, haven’t you?”

Rachel Darcy nods and replies seriously:

> “I think I want to study it properly. Not just as a hobby. UVA has a really good program.”

Mike listens without interrupting, then places a hand on her shoulder.

> “Then we’ll make sure you get all the information you need. This is your future, Rachel. I want you to take it seriously.”

### Beat 4 — Lunch on the Corner

After the formal tour, Mike takes the girls to lunch at a restaurant on The Corner (the student area near campus). The atmosphere is more relaxed. They sit outside, and the conversation flows between serious topics and lighthearted ones.

Mike asks each daughter what stood out to them during the tour. Fallon Glenda talks about the big library. Sabrina Bryna is impressed by the student life. Kiley Rose asks about sports programs. Rachel Darcy remains thoughtful, still processing what she saw.

### Beat 5 — A Private Moment with Rachel Darcy

While the younger girls are finishing their meals, Mike and Rachel Darcy step away for a short walk. Mike speaks to her with a mix of warmth and seriousness:

> “If dance is what you want, we’ll support you. But I also want you to understand that it’s a serious commitment. You have real talent, Rachel. I just want to make sure you’re choosing this for the right reasons.”

Rachel Darcy looks at her father and answers honestly:

> “I know it won’t be easy. But I’ve never wanted anything the way I want this.”

Mike nods, visibly moved by her clarity.

> “Then we’ll figure it out together.”

### Beat 6 — The Drive Home

On the way back, the car is quieter. Rachel Darcy stares out the window, clearly reflecting on the day. Mike glances at her in the rearview mirror with a soft, proud expression.

Kiley Rose eventually breaks the silence with a joke, and the mood lightens again. Mike smiles, grateful for the balance of seriousness and closeness with his daughters.
