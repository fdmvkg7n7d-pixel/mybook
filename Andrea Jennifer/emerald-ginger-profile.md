In the **third month of a pregnancy** (when Emerald Ginger is ~17), she becomes overwhelmed and frightened and **runs away from home**, leaving a brief note (*"I'm okay. Don't look for me. I'm sorry."*). She flees across the country to the **Las Vegas home of her cousin Brandi Jessica and Jamal**, who take her in without interrogation. There, when she is ready, she discloses what happened to a trusted adult who is not her parent (Brandi), who then helps her tell **Andrea Jennifer** — and Andrea Jennifer **immediately calls the police**.
