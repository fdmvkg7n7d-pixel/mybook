# Lÿsa Mandy's Sample Letters Home from French Woods

## Purpose

This file gives **sample child-voice letters** for Lÿsa Mandy's **second summer / second season** at French Woods.

She is:

- **eight years old**
- staying for **two sessions totaling six weeks**
- especially happy with **ballet, gymnastics, and swimming**

These letters are meant to sound specific, affectionate, and recognizably childlike rather than polished.

---

## Letter One — Early in the First Session

Dear Mommy and Charlie,

I got here okay and I know where more things are now because I was here before. The pool looked smaller than I remembered but then when I got in it felt the same. It was cold first and then not cold.

I saw a girl I knew from last summer and she remembered me too. That made me happy. I am doing ballet and gymnastics both so don't think I picked because I didn't. I wanted both again.

My counselor is nice and she remembered I like things explained regular and not too fast. That helped.

You can still do your trip because I am okay.

Love,
Lÿsa Mandy

---

## Letter Two — Later in the First Session

Dear Mommy and Charlie,

I did gymnastics and got the bars right better today and I also did ballet and my feet were pointed good. I am still doing swimming too. I like the swimming part when my hair is wet after and then the sun is warm.

I know the cabin rules already mostly so that part is easier this year. I remembered where to put my things.

One of the girls asked me if I like ballet more or gymnastics more and I said no, I like them the same. That is still true.

I am staying all the weeks so don't come early just because you miss me. You can miss me regular.

Love,
Lÿsa Mandy

---

## Letter Three — Early in the Second Session

Dear Mommy and Charlie,

Now it is the second part of camp and I already know how mornings work so I don't have to think about that as much. I like that.

I am glad I stayed for both sessions because I already had people to say hi to and I knew where swim was and where ballet was and where you wait for gymnastics.

I had a good swimming day and I did not want to get out but then I had gymnastics after so it was okay.

Please tell me if Amsterdam has pretty water. Also tell me if your hotel room is nice.

Love,
Lÿsa Mandy

---

## Letter Four — Later in the Six Weeks

Dear Mommy and Charlie,

I am still having a good camp and I am not tired of it. I thought maybe I would be tired by now but I am not.

I like ballet and gymnastics the same still. Ballet makes me feel tall and gymnastics makes me feel strong and swimming makes me feel loose after.

A counselor said I looked more grown this year because I already know where I am going. I think that is because it is my second summer.

When I come home I want to show you some things and maybe do them in the living room if there is enough room.

I love you and I am okay and I am staying all the way.

Love,
Lÿsa Mandy

---

## Parent-Reading Note

These letters should reassure Fallon and Charlie because they show:

- real detail
- real regulation
- familiar voice
- ongoing enthusiasm
- no sense that Lÿsa Mandy is merely enduring camp

The emotional function is not perfection. It is steadiness.
