## SCENE PURPOSE

This scene documents the arrival of **Lÿsa Mandy Key** at McMaster Children's Hospital. It establishes her birth on Fallon's 13th birthday, her subsequent **two-month hospital stay**, and the family's first Christmas spent in a hospital ward.

## BEFORE THE SCENE — THE RETURN TO ONTARIO

- After the disclosure in Rio and the arrest of the predatory teacher, Mike and Terri Anne made the decision to return to the **Carlisle mansion** in Ontario. They wanted Fallon to have access to the world-class neonatal and pediatric care at **McMaster Children's Hospital** in nearby Hamilton.
- It is Fallon Glenda's thirteenth birthday.

## SCENE ARC

### 1. The Labor at McMaster

- The birth happens in the high-risk maternity wing. Because Fallon is so young (13), the medical team is extensive. 
- Mike and Terri Anne are a constant presence. Mike has secured a private area to ensure Fallon’s privacy.

### 2. The NICU Transition

- **Lÿsa Mandy** is born in the afternoon. Because of complications related to the young age of the mother [or other medical reasons TBD], Lÿsa Mandy is immediately moved to the **NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit)**.
- Fallon’s first birthday as a mother is spent watching her daughter through the glass of an incubator.

### 3. The Two-Month Stay

- Lÿsa Mandy remains at McMaster for **two months** (December and January).
- **The Context of the Tube:** McMaster Hospital is already providing donor milk to Lÿsa Mandy through a nasogastric (NG) tube because her stomach is too small to handle direct nursing. Fallon watches the "hospital milk" go through the tube and feels a desperate need to provide her own.
- **The First Nursing (Mid-January 2047):** Six weeks after birth, Lÿsa Mandy is cleared for direct nursing. Fallon successfully nurses her for the first time, marking the end of the tube-feeding and milking-machine era. Fallon cries during the session—a release of the trauma and the exhaustion of the hospital stay. *(See `the-first-nursing-milestone-outline.md` and `the-first-nursing-milestone-prose.md`.)*

### 4. Christmas Day in the Ward (2045)

- Christmas Day 2045 is spent at McMaster. 
- **The First Hold:** After twenty-four days in the NICU, thirteen-year-old Fallon Glenda is allowed to hold Lÿsa Mandy for the first time. The arrival and the intervention of Mike Key against a judgmental doctor establish Fallon's maternal right. *(See `the-first-hold-christmas-scene-outline.md` and `the-first-hold-christmas-scene.md`.)*
- With the family geographically scattered (Kiley Rose in Natal, Jessica Isabella in Halifax, Rachel Darcy in Virginia), Patti Anne brings the younger sisters from Carlisle to the hospital waiting room, ensuring the "Key Family Christmas" still happens around Fallon.

### 5. The Closing Image — Going Home

- The scene ends with a flash-forward or a concluding beat in late January: the day Lÿsa Mandy is finally released from McMaster.
- Mike carries the car seat out to the SUV, with Fallon walking beside him, finally bringing her daughter to the Carlisle mansion.
