# Lÿsa Mandy's French Woods Friends and Counselors Notes

## Purpose

This file names a small starter circle of **friends and counselors** for Lÿsa Mandy's **second summer / second season** at French Woods.

It is meant to support future scenes involving:

- arrival
- social settling
- activity blocks
- letters home
- camp-specific memory details

---

## Lÿsa Mandy at Camp — Social Shape

Lÿsa Mandy does not need to be written as a loud or instant-social child to be socially successful.

At camp, her belonging works best through:

- recognition
- routine
- activity-based connection
- adults who are clear and calm
- peers who make room without forcing intensity

Her easiest shared languages are often:

- ballet
- gymnastics
- swimming
- remembered details from last summer

## Group Dynamic Note

The social world around Lÿsa Mandy should not feel overcrowded. A **small, believable circle** is better than a huge named cast.

Recommended emotional pattern:

- **Tessa** = recognition / continuity
- **Mina** = present-summer friendship
- **Mara** = cabin safety and structure
- **Elena** = ballet seriousness
- **Jo** = gymnastics confidence
- **Naomi** = swim calm and regulation
