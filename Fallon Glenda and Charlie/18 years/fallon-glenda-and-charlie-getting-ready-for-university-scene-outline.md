# SCENE OUTLINE: FALLON GLENDA AND CHARLIE — GETTING READY FOR UNIVERSITY

## Placement
**When:** Within the first week or two after returning from New York City to San Francisco.  
**Where:** The family home in San Francisco.  
**Context:** Fallon Glenda and Charlie are preparing for the upcoming university term at Stanford. Lÿsa Mandy (age eight) is simultaneously preparing to return to **Sacred Heart School** in San Francisco. This scene focuses on Fallon and Charlie’s university preparation while keeping Lÿsa Mandy’s school experience as a parallel thread.

---

## Scene Purpose

This scene establishes:
- Fallon and Charlie actively preparing for their university responsibilities at Stanford.
- The balance between their academic ambitions and their role as parents.
- A quiet, reflective moment as the family transitions from summer vacation back into structured routines.
- The contrast between Lÿsa Mandy attending Sacred Heart School and her parents attending Stanford University.

---

## Emotional Core

The emotional core is **partnership and shared ambition**.

Fallon and Charlie are preparing for university together, just as they have done everything else. The scene shows them supporting each other while also guiding Lÿsa Mandy through her own school preparations. It reinforces that university is part of the family’s ongoing story, not separate from it.

---

## Tone

Grounded, intimate, slightly nostalgic, and quietly determined. The scene should feel like the family is stepping back into their real lives with intention.

---

## Scene Outline

### Beat 1 — Evening at Home
A few days after returning from New York, the house is calm. Lÿsa Mandy is in her room laying out her Sacred Heart School uniform and new school supplies. In the kitchen, Fallon and Charlie have spread university documents across the table — course registration forms, reading lists, financial documents, and old notebooks from previous terms.

### Beat 2 — University Preparation
Fallon and Charlie are going through their Stanford materials. Fallon reviews her pre-med or medical-related coursework. Charlie organizes his own academic schedule. They speak in low voices about professors, deadlines, and how they will manage their time once the term begins.

### Beat 3 — Lÿsa Mandy Enters
Lÿsa Mandy comes into the kitchen wearing her new Sacred Heart plaid skirt, modeling it for them. She is excited about returning to school but also curious about what her parents are doing. Fallon invites her to sit with them.

### Beat 4 — Explaining the Difference
Fallon and Charlie gently explain the difference between Lÿsa Mandy’s school and their university:

- Sacred Heart is her school.
- Stanford is where they go for university (a much bigger, more advanced kind of school).

Lÿsa Mandy listens seriously, beginning to understand that her parents have their own “school” to attend.

### Beat 5 — Sharing Small Stories
Fallon and Charlie share brief, age-appropriate memories of their time at Stanford so far:
- How they met on campus.
- Late nights studying together while Lÿsa Mandy was small.
- The friends they made and the challenges they faced.

They keep the stories warm and light.

### Beat 6 — Practical Planning
They show Lÿsa Mandy their class schedules and explain that university requires a lot of reading, writing, and focus. Fallon mentions her goal of becoming a doctor. Charlie shares what he is studying. They discuss how they will help each other with childcare and household responsibilities during the busy term.

### Beat 7 — Emotional Undercurrent
Fallon looks at an old photo of herself at seventeen with baby Lÿsa Mandy and becomes quiet for a moment. Charlie reaches over and takes her hand. They share a brief look that acknowledges how far they have come — from young parents at Stanford to the family they are now.

Lÿsa Mandy notices but does not interrupt.

### Beat 8 — Including Lÿsa Mandy
Fallon asks Lÿsa Mandy if she would like to help organize the papers or choose a special notebook for her own schoolwork. Lÿsa Mandy takes the task seriously and helps sort documents. This small act makes her feel connected to her parents’ world.

### Beat 9 — Parallel School Preparations
Later that evening, the family works side by side:
- Lÿsa Mandy organizes her Sacred Heart supplies.
- Fallon and Charlie finalize their Stanford schedules.
- They talk about the upcoming weeks — Lÿsa Mandy’s first day at Sacred Heart, Fallon and Charlie’s first day back at Stanford.

The parallel preparation feels natural and connected.

### Beat 10 — Emotional Landing
Before bed, Fallon tucks Lÿsa Mandy in and says:

> “You’re going to Sacred Heart, and Daddy and I are going to Stanford. We’re all starting something new, but we’re doing it together.”

Lÿsa Mandy nods, understanding that her parents have their own important journey alongside hers.

---

## Key Dialogue Possibilities

**Lÿsa Mandy**
> “Is Stanford bigger than Sacred Heart?”
> “Will you have to wear uniforms too?”
> “Can I visit you at your school one day?”

**Fallon Glenda**
> “Stanford is much bigger. It’s where we go after high school to learn even more.”
> “We’re going to work very hard this year, but we’ll still be here for you every day.”

**Charlie**
> “Your mom is going to be a doctor one day. That’s why she studies so much at Stanford.”
> “We’re lucky we get to do this together as a family.”

---

## What This Scene Establishes

1. Fallon and Charlie are actively preparing for their university term at Stanford.
2. Lÿsa Mandy attends **Sacred Heart School**, while her parents attend Stanford University.
3. The family balances academic ambition with parenting through partnership and inclusion.
4. The return from New York transitions smoothly into the structured, goal-oriented rhythm of Bay Area life.
5. University preparation is treated as an ongoing, shared part of the family’s story.
