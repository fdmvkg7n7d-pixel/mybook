# SCENE OUTLINE: THE SURGERY DAY – REMOVING THE STONE

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára, Klárka Pavlínka (10), Simonka Káta (9), Dr. Marek (Surgeon)
- **Location:** University Hospital, Prague (Pediatric Surgery Wing).
- **Date:** Late Summer, circa 2079.
- **Status:** Outline

---

## THE SETTING
- **Hospital Atmosphere:** Clean, quiet, and high-tech, but softened by the pediatric wing’s efforts to remain child-friendly. 
- **The Stakes:** This is the day the "stone" is removed. While the prognosis for Secretory Breast Carcinoma is high, the reality of anesthesia and surgery for a nine-year-old is heavy.

---

## SCENE ARC

### 1. Morning at Home
- A quiet, focused morning in their Prague home. No breakfast for Simonka due to the fast.
- The atmosphere is one of "quiet mission." Gamaliel and Klára work in perfect sync, packing a bag with Simonka’s favorite stuffed animal, a tablet, and a soft button-up shirt for after the surgery.
- **The "Tighten" Hug:** Before leaving the house, Klárka gives Simonka a particularly long embrace. "I'll be waiting at the Academy for the call," she whispers.

### 2. Hospital Arrival and Pre-Op
- Gamaliel, Klára, and Simonka arrive at the hospital.
- **Bodily Autonomy & Consent:** The nursing staff and Dr. Marek continue the family's protocol. Every step—putting on the hospital gown, placing the ID band, checking vitals—is preceded by an "Ask." 
- Simonka is allowed to keep her own shorts on under the gown until the very last moment to maintain her sense of privacy and control.
- **Naturalism:** In the private pre-op curtained area, Simonka is shirtless under the open gown, comfortable and unafraid of her own body as she prepares for the "repair."

### 3. Into the Operating Room
- The anesthesiologist arrives. Simonka is placed on the gurney.
- **Klára’s Presence:** Because of the family’s advocacy for Simonka’s comfort and their status in the community, Klára is permitted to accompany Simonka directly into the Operating Room for the induction of anesthesia.
- Gamaliel and Klárka (10) walk with them to the doors of the surgical suite, sharing a final "tighten" hug with Simonka.
- Inside the OR, Klára holds Simonka’s hand and speaks softly to her as the anesthesia takes effect, ensuring the last thing Simonka sees is her mother’s face.

### 4. The Long Wait
- While the surgery takes place, Klára joins Gamaliel and Klárka in the surgical waiting room. 
- They don't talk much. Klárka sits between her parents, her hands gripping theirs. Occasionally, she practices small finger-strengthening exercises or stretches her feet, unable to be completely still.
- Gamaliel paces occasionally, his legs needing the movement; Klára sits with her feet flat on the floor, her eyes fixed on the doors, ready to return to the recovery area the moment she is called.

### 5. The Recovery Room (PACU)
- The moment the surgery is completed, Klára is led back to the Recovery Room. 
- She is there before Simonka even begins to wake, sitting by the bed and holding her hand.
- As Simonka starts to stir from the anesthesia, the very first thing she feels is the warmth of her mother's presence.
- **The First Check:** Simonka's eyes flutter open. "Mami?" Klára nods, her eyes wet with relief. "It's gone, Simi. The stone is all gone."
- Gamaliel and Klárka are brought in shortly after, completing the family circle in the recovery bay.

### 6. Healing as a Family
- A few hours later, once Simonka is stable in her room, the four of them are finally together in the hospital suite.
- The "circle" is now not only closed, but the "stone" that threatened it has been removed. 
- Klárka sits on the edge of the hospital bed, reading a book to Simonka or just watching her sleep, refusing to leave her side.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES
- **Simonka Káta (9):** The hero of the day. Her courage is rooted in the trust she has in her parents and her own agency.
- **Klárka Pavlínka (10):** The emotional sentinel. Her day at the Academy was a feat of endurance.
- **Klára:** Finding her "ground" again. The physical removal of the tumor helps her feel that the world is no longer tilted.
- **Gamaliel:** The strategist. He ensures the medical team respects Simonka’s autonomy at every turn.

---

## SAGA CONNECTIONS
- **Medical Resilience:** A successful chapter in the Key family's history of facing medical crises.
- **Dancer’s Discipline:** The girls' ability to stay calm and focused is a direct result of their ballet training.
- **Autonomy:** The surgery day is a "masterclass" in how the family handles consent in a medical environment.
