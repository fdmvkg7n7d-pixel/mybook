# SCENE OUTLINE: TUESDAY NORMALCY AND WEDNESDAY SPECIALIST

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klárka Pavlínka (10), Simonka Káta (9), Dr. Marek (Specialist)
- **Location:** The Ballet Academy (Prague) and University Hospital.
- **Date:** Tuesday and Wednesday after the Crete vacation.
- **Status:** Outline

---

## TUESDAY: THE "NORMAL" DAY

### 1. The Decision for Normalcy
- Because the specialist, Dr. Marek, has no appointments available on Tuesday, Gamaliel and Klára decide that Simonka should attend the summer camp at the Academy.
- The goal is to give her a day where she isn't "the girl with the stone," but just a dancer at camp.

### 2. Morning at the Academy
- Simonka and Klárka participate in the morning workshops. 
- **The Studio Atmosphere:** In the private, familiar studios of their own Academy, the sisters often practice shirtless in their dance shorts. It is a space where their bodies are tools for art, not just subjects for medicine.
- Klárka (10) stays close to Simonka (9), her protective energy channeled into helping Simonka with her combinations.

### 3. Lunch in Gamaliel’s Office
- Instead of eating with the other campers, the girls go to Gamaliel’s private office at the Academy for lunch.
- **The Sanctuary:** The office is filled with ballet history, books, and family photos. It feels like a safe haven within the school.
- They eat a quiet lunch together—perhaps more *rohlíky* and fruit, or takeout from a local Prague café.
- Gamaliel uses the time to check in with them emotionally, keeping the conversation light but supportive. They video-call Klára in Cape Town during the lunch break.

---

## WEDNESDAY: THE SPECIALIST APPOINTMENT

### 1. The Morning Split
- Both girls go to the Academy in the morning.
- After lunch, Gamaliel picks up Simonka from the studio. 
- **The "Tighten" Hug:** Klárka and Simonka share a fierce embrace at the studio door. Klárka’s grip is tight; she is the one left behind while Simonka goes to the "scary" place.

### 2. Meeting Dr. Marek
- The appointment at the University Hospital.
- **The Protocol:** Dr. Marek respects Simonka’s autonomy, asking for her permission before the physical exam.
- **The Discovery:** He confirms the mass. He uses the term **Secretory Breast Carcinoma** (Juvenile Breast Carcinoma). 
- He explains that it is a "slow-growing" tumor and that the best course of action is surgery to remove it. He is calm and optimistic, which helps settle Gamaliel’s nerves.

### 3. The Plan
- Dr. Marek outlines the timeline for the biopsy and the eventual surgery. 
- Simonka asks if she can still dance. Dr. Marek promises her she will be back in the studio as soon as she heals.

### 4. The Release
- After leaving Dr. Marek's office, as they walk through the quiet hospital corridor toward the car, the "brave" mask Simonka has been wearing finally slips.
- **The Breakdown:** Simonka stops walking and suddenly starts crying. It is a deep, quiet release of all the anxiety she has held since Crete.
- **The Comfort:** Gamaliel immediately drops to his knees to be at her level, pulling her into a tight, protective embrace. She cries into his shoulder, her small hands gripping his jacket.
- Gamaliel doesn't tell her to stop; he simply holds her, murmuring in Czech that he is there, that it’s okay to be scared, and that they will face the "stone" together. He stays there with her on the floor of the hospital until the sobbing turns into quiet hiccups.

### 5. Reunion
- Once she has calmed down, they return to the Academy to pick up Klárka.
- The sisters reunite with another "tighten" hug—a physical confirmation that they are still a unit, regardless of what the doctors say. Klárka immediately notices Simonka's red eyes and pulls her even closer, whispering something only the two of them can hear.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES
- **Gamaliel:** Managing the Academy, the camp, and the medical crisis simultaneously. His office is his "command center."
- **Simonka (9):** Appreciates the "normal" day on Tuesday. It gives her a sense of control before the hospital visit.
- **Klárka Pavlínka (10):** Her focus is entirely on Simonka. She is the first person Simonka sees when she leaves the studio and the first one she sees when she returns.

---

## SAGA CONNECTIONS
- **Academy Life:** Establishes the Ballet Academy as the heart of the family's daily life in Prague.
- **Bodily Autonomy:** Consistency in medical consent across different doctors.
- **Sisterly Bond:** The "Tighten" hug becomes the signature emotional gesture of this crisis.
