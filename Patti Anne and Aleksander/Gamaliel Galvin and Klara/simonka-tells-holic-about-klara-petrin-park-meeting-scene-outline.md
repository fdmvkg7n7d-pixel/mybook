# SCENE OUTLINE: SIMONKA TELLS HOLIC THAT KLÁRA WANTS TO MEET IN A NEUTRAL PLACE (HOLIC CHOOSES PETŘÍN PARK)

*This scene captures the moment Simonka brings Klára’s request to Holic. Holic responds by selecting Petřín Park himself — a location that appears neutral on the surface but carries layered meaning within his world.*

---

## CANON BACKDROP & HEART HISTORY

* **Simonka’s Current State:** She is deep in the GG (Good Girl) dynamic with Holic. She has recently told Gamaliel at Charles University that *“I chose this.”* She is trying to maintain the narrative that this life is her choice.
* **Klára’s Request:** Klára wants to meet Simonka face-to-face in a neutral, public place. She is not demanding Simonka come home — only that they talk.
* **Holic’s Control:** Even when Simonka frames the meeting as information, Holic retains full authority over whether it happens and where.
* **Petřín Park:** Holic chooses this park specifically. While it is public and open in many areas, it also contains more secluded paths where some members of his circle enjoy walking their pets. This is not lost on Simonka.

---

## BEAT 1 — THE SETTING: HOLIC’S APARTMENT / CLUB SPACE

* **Time:** Evening, a few days after Gamaliel’s visit to Charles University.
* **Location:** Holic’s private space. The atmosphere is controlled.
* **Simonka’s State:** She is in GG mode — careful, respectful, and prepared to deliver the message without demanding anything.

---

## BEAT 2 — SIMONKA PRESENTS THE REQUEST

* **The Delivery:** Simonka speaks calmly and respectfully.
  * “Klára wants to meet me. She said it doesn’t have to be at home. She only asked for a neutral place.”
* **Her Framing:** She makes it clear she is only passing along the request and will accept whatever Holic decides.
* **Holic’s Silence:** He does not answer immediately. He watches her, letting the weight of the request sit between them.

---

## BEAT 3 — HOLIC CHOOSES PETŘÍN PARK

* **The Suggestion:** After a long pause, Holic speaks. He does not ask for more details or debate the idea. Instead, he names the location himself:
  * “Petřín Park.”
* **His Reasoning (Surface Level):** He explains that it is public, large, and has many open areas — technically neutral.
* **The Subtext:** He then adds, almost casually, that some members of his circle enjoy walking their pets in the more secluded paths of the park. The comment lands with deliberate weight. It is both a practical observation and a reminder that even in a “public” park, their world can still reach her.
* **Simonka’s Reaction:** She absorbs the choice without protest. The fact that Holic selected the park — and specifically referenced the secluded areas — makes the power imbalance unmistakable.

---

## BEAT 4 — THE CONDITIONS

* **Holic Sets the Terms:** He does not simply agree. He dictates the conditions:
  * When the meeting can happen
  * How long it can last
  * Whether he (or someone else) will be nearby
  * That Simonka will report back everything that is said
* **Simonka’s Response:** She accepts the terms without argument, maintaining her GG posture.
* **The Emotional Undercurrent:** Even though she presented the request as neutral, Holic has taken complete ownership of the decision — including choosing a location that carries meaning within his world.

---

## BEAT 5 — AFTER THE CONVERSATION

* **Simonka’s Solitude:** Once the conversation ends, Simonka is left alone. The choice of Petřín Park — and especially the mention of the secluded pet-walking areas — lingers with her.
* **Internal Conflict:** She has successfully passed the message, but Holic’s selection of the park reinforces how little control she actually has, even over something as simple as where she meets her mother.

---

## WHAT THIS OUTLINE ADDS TO CANON

1. **Power Dynamics:** Holic does not merely grant permission — he takes control of the entire arrangement, including choosing the location.
2. **Petřín Park Symbolism:** The park becomes a location that exists in both the “normal” world and Holic’s world. The secluded areas where members walk pets add an unsettling layer.
3. **Simonka’s Position:** Reinforces that she is still operating within Holic’s rules, even when attempting to facilitate contact with her family.
4. **Klára’s Approach:** Highlights the contrast between Klára’s careful, open request and Holic’s total ownership of the response.
5. **Future Scene Potential:** Sets up the actual meeting in Petřín Park with the added tension that Holic chose the ground.
