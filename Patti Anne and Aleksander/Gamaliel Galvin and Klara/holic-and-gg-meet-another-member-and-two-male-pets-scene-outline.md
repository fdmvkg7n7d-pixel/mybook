# SCENE OUTLINE: HOLIC AND GG MEET ANOTHER MEMBER AND HIS TWO MALE PETS

*This scene takes place after the Petřín Park meeting. Holic and Simonka (as GG) encounter another member of the circle and his two male pets during a walk or social engagement. It deepens the world-building around the pet dynamic and shows Simonka’s position within it.*

---

## CANON BACKDROP & HEART HISTORY

* **Timing:** Later the same day or evening after Simonka’s meeting with Klára in Petřín Park.
* **Simonka’s State:** She is still in GG mode. The electric dog collar remains locked around her throat. She has just returned from seeing her mother and is expected to report the details of the conversation.
* **The Other Member:** A fellow dominant in Holic’s social circle who keeps two male pets. The pets are fully immersed in the dynamic — collared, trained, and treated as animals in public and private settings.
* **Purpose of the Scene:** To show the broader community Holic belongs to, contrast Simonka’s experience with that of the male pets, and explore how Holic uses these encounters.

---

## BEAT 1 — THE SETTING

* **Location:** A quiet café, park path, or private club lounge where members of the circle sometimes gather. The atmosphere is relaxed but hierarchical.
* **Holic and GG’s Arrival:** Holic arrives with Simonka walking slightly behind him in the proper GG posture. The electric collar is visible.
* **The Other Member:** He is already present with his two male pets. Both pets are on leashes, wearing full pet gear. They have thick, padded leather collars with name tags and small bells. Their hands are enclosed in soft leather mitts that prevent them from using their fingers. Short, realistic tail plugs are visible, and they wear minimal harness-style clothing that leaves most of their bodies exposed while emphasizing their animal status. One pet has a darker, more muscular build and moves with calm obedience; the other is slimmer and more playful in his movements, occasionally earning a light correction from his owner. Both are completely silent unless given permission to make pet sounds. They move in perfect sync with their owner, never pulling on their leashes.

---

## BEAT 2 — THE GREETING AND INTRODUCTIONS

* **Social Interaction:** Holic and the other member greet each other with the casual familiarity of long-time acquaintances in the scene.
* **The Pets:** The two male pets remain fully in character. They do not speak in human language. When their owner gives a soft command, one immediately drops into a perfect sit, back straight, hands in mitts resting on his thighs. The other nuzzles affectionately against his owner’s leg, earning a brief pat on the head. Both keep their eyes lowered unless looking at their dominant. They occasionally exchange small glances with each other — a subtle, wordless communication between pets. When Holic and GG arrive, both pets turn their attention to the newcomers with quiet curiosity, their bells making soft sounds as they move.
* **Simonka’s Position:** As GG, Simonka is expected to acknowledge the other dominant respectfully but does not interact with the pets unless instructed. She stands quietly beside Holic.

---

## BEAT 3 — CONVERSATION BETWEEN THE DOMINANTS

* **Topics:** The two men discuss recent events, club matters, or training techniques. The other member may comment on Simonka’s collar or ask how her training is progressing.
* **Holic’s Response:** Holic answers with quiet pride, perhaps demonstrating a small correction or command to show Simonka’s responsiveness.
* **The Male Pets:** The other member casually demonstrates his pets’ training. He gives one a quiet command (“Present”) and the pet immediately shifts into position — kneeling upright, chest forward, mittened hands behind his head, exposing his torso. The second pet is told to “Stay” and freezes perfectly still, even when a light breeze moves through the area. Later, one pet is allowed to crawl a short distance to retrieve a small item for his owner, moving with practiced grace on hands and knees. The bells on their collars chime softly with every movement. These displays are done with the same casual ease one might show off a well-trained dog.

* **The Triggering Moment:** At one point, the more playful male pet is given permission to approach GG. He crawls closer and attempts to mount her from behind, trying to access her bottom in typical pet-play mounting behavior. The sudden, explicit attempt — combined with the emotional weight of having just seen her mother — overwhelms Simonka. Her voice cracks as she says the safeword clearly and urgently: “Yellow.” The room goes still.

---

## BEAT 4 — SIMONKA’S INTERNAL EXPERIENCE

* **Observation:** Simonka watches the two male pets with a mixture of detachment and quiet recognition. She notices the way their mitts force them to move on knuckles and palms, the way their tail plugs shift with every step, and how their bells mark every movement like a constant reminder of their status. She sees how completely they have surrendered their human identities — even their eye contact with each other is limited and controlled.
* **Comparison:** She notes the differences between her GG role and their full pet identities. The male pets are not expected to speak or maintain any human dignity. One of them briefly meets her eyes with something like curiosity — or perhaps recognition — before quickly looking away when his owner notices. Simonka wonders if they see her as closer to them than she wants to admit.
* **Emotional Layer:** Even while maintaining perfect GG composure, Simonka feels the weight of the collar more acutely after having just spoken with her mother. Watching the two male pets perform so naturally makes the electric collar around her own throat feel heavier. The normalcy with which the other dominant treats his pets underscores how normalized this world has become for her — and how far she has already traveled into it.
* **The Yellow Moment:** When the playful male pet attempts to mount her and reach her bottom, the combination of the explicit pet-play behavior and her raw emotional state from the earlier meeting with Klára pushes her over the edge. Saying “Yellow” is the first time she has used a safeword in front of others in this context. Her voice is shaky but clear and urgent. The word hangs in the air, breaking the casual atmosphere of the gathering.

---

## BEAT 4.5 — HOLIC’S REACTION TO “YELLOW”

* **Immediate Response:** Holic reacts instantly and with calm authority. He steps forward, places a firm hand on the back of Simonka’s neck (just above the collar), and speaks in a low, controlled voice:
  * “Yellow. Understood.”
* **Handling the Other Pet:** He addresses the other dominant without raising his voice:
  * “Control your pet.”
  The other member reacts with visible anger. His face tightens and he snaps a sharp correction at the male pet, yanking the leash harder than necessary. The pet drops instantly into a submissive position, clearly startled by his owner’s sudden fury. The other dominant’s voice is low but edged with irritation as he mutters something under his breath about “bad timing” and “ruining the moment.”
* **Holic’s Tone with Simonka:** He does not scold her for using the safeword. Instead, he speaks to her quietly but firmly, in a way that reinforces both her safety and his control:
  * “Good girl. You used your word. That’s what it’s for.”
* **Holic’s Reaction to the Threat:** Holic remains completely calm. He does not raise his voice or show anger. Instead, he meets the other member’s glare with steady, cold authority and replies in a quiet, measured tone:
  * “Do what you need to do. The board will hear what actually happened.”
  His hand stays protectively on the back of Simonka’s neck the entire time, making it clear whose side he is on. The contrast between his composure and the other man’s anger is stark.
* **New Arrival:** Another member of the circle arrives with his own male pet. This pet is familiar — he was very friendly and affectionate toward GG at the last monthly club meeting. He immediately recognizes Simonka and gives her a warm, eager look, his bells jingling as he moves.
* **Alone Time:** After a short conversation between the dominants, Holic and the new member agree to let Simonka (GG) and the friendly male pet go off together for some private time. The two are permitted to leave the group and find a more secluded area of the location. The male pet leads the way with clear excitement, while Simonka follows in her GG posture, the electric collar still locked around her throat.

---

## BEAT 4.6 — SIMONKA AND THE FRIENDLY MALE PET — ALONE TIME

* **The Setting:** They move to a more private, secluded spot within the location — a quiet, dimly lit corner or side area where they can have relative privacy while still technically being within the circle’s space. The atmosphere is hushed and intimate.
* **The Male Pet’s Behavior:** Once away from his owner, the pet drops the more performative aspects of his role and becomes genuinely affectionate. He presses close to Simonka, resting his head against her thigh or shoulder, making soft, almost comforting sounds. His bells are quieter here. He seems to sense her emotional state and offers simple, wordless comfort through physical closeness.
* **Simonka’s Emotional State:** Still wearing the locked electric collar, Simonka is initially tense. The events of the day — seeing her mother, the mounting attempt, the angry threat about the board — have left her raw. The male pet’s gentle, non-demanding affection begins to crack through her GG composure. She allows herself to lean into him, her fingers hesitantly stroking the fur of his collar or the back of his neck.
* **Intimate Connection:** The moment becomes quietly emotional. The pet nuzzles into her, and Simonka finds herself whispering to him — not as GG, but as Simonka — soft, fragmented thoughts about how heavy the collar feels today and how strange it was to see her mother. He responds with more nuzzling and soft sounds, offering the kind of wordless empathy that only another submissive in this world can give. There is a sense of shared understanding and mutual vulnerability between them.
* **Physical Intimacy:** Their closeness grows more intimate. The pet curls against her, and Simonka allows herself to hold him properly, her arms around his back. The contact is tender rather than overtly sexual, though it carries an undercurrent of sensuality. For a few minutes, the electric collar and the rules feel slightly more distant.
* **Emotional Release:** Simonka’s eyes become glassy. She doesn’t cry, but the tension in her body eases in a way it hasn’t all day. The pet seems to understand and stays close, offering steady, quiet comfort.
* **Ending the Moment:** Eventually they are called back. Before they return, the male pet gives her one last affectionate nuzzle. Simonka straightens her posture, slips back into GG mode, and follows him out — but something small and human has passed between them.

---

## BEAT 4.7 — KLÁRA FOLLOWS AND WITNESSES

* **Klára’s Decision:** After the Petřín Park meeting, Klára does not immediately leave. She stays in the area, conflicted and unable to walk away. She witnesses far more than she intended.
* **What She Sees — The Earlier Confrontation:** From a hidden vantage point, Klára sees the entire earlier scene involving the two male pets. She watches one of them attempt to mount Simonka from behind. She hears her daughter clearly say “Yellow.” She sees Holic’s calm reaction and the other member’s angry threat to bring the incident to the board of injustice. She sees the electric collar locked around Simonka’s throat the entire time.
* **What She Sees — The Alone Time:** Later, when Simonka and the third, friendlier male pet are allowed to go off together, Klára follows them as well. She witnesses the intimate and emotional exchange — Simonka softening, whispering to the pet, and holding him tenderly. The contrast between the earlier aggressive pet interaction and this gentle one is stark.
* **Klára’s Emotional Reaction:** The cumulative sight is devastating, but Klára’s dominant feeling is one of harsh judgment. She looks very badly upon Simonka’s behavior — the way her daughter allowed the mounting attempt, used her safeword in front of others, and then later engaged in such intimate, emotional closeness with another pet while still wearing the locked electric collar. Klára feels a sharp, painful disappointment mixed with anger. She thinks of her own abusive father and how this scene would have looked to him. The memory of his cruelty and judgment rises in her, and she finds herself viewing Simonka through that same harsh lens. The thought makes Klára’s chest tighten with grief, anger, and self-loathing. She covers her mouth to stay silent, tears streaming down her face, but her expression is not only pained — it is also deeply disapproving.
* **The Aftermath:** Klára does not intervene. She stays hidden until everyone has left the area. Once alone, she remains in her hiding spot for a long time, processing everything she has witnessed. This experience may become a major turning point for Klára — either strengthening her determination to keep reaching out to Simonka or pushing her toward a more desperate or drastic action.

---

## BEAT 5 — HOLIC’S USE OF THE ENCOUNTER (AFTER THE YELLOW)

* **After the Meeting:** Once they are alone again, Holic may ask Simonka what she thought of the other member’s pets, or he may use the encounter as a teaching moment about expectations and behavior.
* **Report on Klára:** At some point, Holic asks Simonka to recount the conversation with her mother. He listens carefully, possibly while idly touching the collar’s receiver.

---

## BEAT 6 — CLOSING IMAGE

* **The Final Picture:** Holic and GG walk away from the encounter. Simonka remains in perfect posture beside him, the electric collar gleaming at her throat. Behind them, the other member continues his evening with his two male pets moving obediently at his side.

---

## WHAT THIS OUTLINE ADDS TO CANON

1. **World-Building:** Expands the pet dynamic beyond Simonka and Holic, showing a wider community and the existence of a “board of injustice.”
2. **Simonka’s Perspective:** Gives insight into how she perceives other submissives/pets and her own position within the hierarchy.
3. **Holic’s Character:** Shows how he uses social interactions to reinforce control and expectations, and how he handles threats from other members.
4. **Contrast:** Highlights the difference between Simonka’s GG role and full pet play.
5. **Emotional Continuity:** Ties the Petřín Park meeting to Simonka’s ongoing experience by having her interact with the community shortly afterward.
6. **New Development:** Introduces the possibility of Simonka spending private time with another pet, opening new emotional and relational territory.
