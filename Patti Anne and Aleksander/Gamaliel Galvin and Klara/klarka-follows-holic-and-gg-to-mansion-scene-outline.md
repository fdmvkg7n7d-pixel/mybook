# SCENE OUTLINE: KLARKA PAVLINKA FOLLOWS HOLIC AND GG TO THE MANSION

*This scene takes place after the events at the garden (Klára telling Gamaliel everything and the prison call). Klarka Pavlinka follows her mother’s trail and ends up following Holic and GG (Simonka) to Holic’s mansion. She did not witness the earlier events in the park.*

---

## CANON BACKDROP & HEART HISTORY

* **Timing:** This occurs later the same evening or the next day, after the prison call about Klára’s father. The friendly male pet “takes his time” during the alone time with GG, which delays Simonka and Holic’s departure and gives Klarka the opportunity to follow.
* **Klarka’s Motivation:** Klarka has become concerned about her mother (Klára) and has been keeping an eye on her. She follows Klára’s trail and ends up witnessing Holic and GG leaving the area.
* **Klarka Did Not Witness the Park Events:** She arrives too late to see the confrontation with the two male pets, the mounting attempt, or Simonka saying “Yellow.” She only sees Holic and GG departing together.

---

## BEAT 1 — KLARKA FOLLOWS KLÁRA

* **The Setting:** Klarka has been quietly following her mother since earlier in the day. She does not confront Klára but stays at a distance, concerned about her emotional state after the Petřín Park meeting.
* **Klarka’s Observation:** She sees Klára return home in tears and later witnesses the emotional scene in the garden with Gamaliel (from a distance). She does not hear the prison call but senses something significant has happened.

---

## BEAT 2 — KLARKA FOLLOWS HOLIC AND GG

* **The Trigger:** Later that evening (or the next day), Klarka sees Holic and GG (Simonka) leaving the area together. Because the friendly male pet “took his time” during the alone time with Simonka, their departure is delayed, giving Klarka the chance to follow them.
* **The Journey:** Klarka follows Holic and GG from a safe distance as they make their way to Holic’s mansion. She is careful not to be seen.
* **What She Sees:** She observes the dynamic between Holic and GG — Simonka walking slightly behind Holic in her GG posture, the electric collar still visible at her throat. Klarka does not see the earlier pet-related events, only the controlled, hierarchical way Holic and GG move together.

---

## BEAT 3 — ARRIVAL AT THE MANSION

* **The Location:** Holic’s mansion. It is large, private, and imposing. Klarka stays hidden outside, watching as Holic and GG arrive.
* **The Pole:** In the middle of the front yard stands a tall, sturdy pole. Klarka watches in shock as Holic ties Simonka (GG) to it. Simonka remains compliant, her hands secured above her head or behind her back, the electric collar still locked around her throat. The act is done openly in the front yard, visible to anyone passing by.
* **The Box Van:** Shortly after, another box van pulls up to the mansion. The owner opens the back and lets out **four male pets and one female pet**: **Knuckles**, **Pretzel**, **Falafel**, and **Wunjo** (the males), and **Juno** (the female). All five pets are wearing **dog collars** with bells and name tags. They do **not** have tail plugs (so they can go to the bathroom when needed). The four male pets (**Knuckles**, **Pretzel**, and **Falafel** are energetic; **Wunjo** is calmer) wear **harness-style clothing** that leaves most of their bodies exposed. **Juno** is tiny and moves with grace and dignity. They all move in trained, obedient ways, their bells jingling with every step as they are led toward the house. The scene becomes even more overwhelming as multiple pets are brought into the mansion.
* **Klarka’s Reaction:** Seeing her sister being tied to a pole in the middle of the front yard — still wearing the collar and in full GG mode — and then watching a box van unload four male pets and one female pet fills Klarka with **pure horror**. The realization that Simonka appears willing to be used by multiple people in succession (the five pets plus Holic) hits her like a physical blow. Her horror quickly turns into **intense anger**.
* **The Confrontation:** Unable to contain herself any longer, Klarka storms out of her hiding place and marches straight up to Holic’s front door. She begins **screaming** at the top of her lungs, calling out for Simonka and demanding that Holic come out. Her voice is raw with fury and distress as she shouts accusations and pleads for her sister. The sudden outburst shatters the controlled atmosphere of the mansion grounds.
* **Knuckles and GG:** While Klarka is screaming at the front door, **Knuckles and GG (Simonka)** begin having sex in the middle of Holic’s front yard, right next to the pole where Simonka was tied. The act is public and deliberate, happening openly as Klarka continues to scream. A **single tear** falls down Simonka’s right cheek, but she **continues** without stopping. The contrast between Klarka’s desperate shouting and the explicit scene unfolding in the yard makes the moment even more chaotic and humiliating.
* **Holic’s Reaction:** Holic stands nearby, watching the scene unfold with a calm, almost amused expression. He makes no move to stop either Klarka’s screaming or the act between Knuckles and GG. His silence and composure only add to the disturbing atmosphere. He calmly clarifies:
  * “There are no more pets here. I only have one — Good Girl.”
* **The Other Owner Emerges:** Another man steps out of the mansion. His name is **Matthias**. He is the owner of the five pets that arrived in the box van. He looks visibly irritated by Klarka’s screaming.
* **Holic’s Words to Klarka:** Holic turns to Klarka and speaks calmly but coldly:
  * “There is nothing you can do. Good Girl likes sleeping with different people.”
* **Matthias’s Anger:** Matthias becomes increasingly angry with Klarka. He glares at her and snaps:
  * “You have no right to come here and scream like this. This is none of your business.”
* **Klarka’s Hysteria:** Overwhelmed by everything she has seen — her sister tied to a pole, having sex in the yard, and being told she likes it — Klarka starts **crying hysterically**. Her screams turn into uncontrollable sobs.
* **Raphael Saves Her:** **Raphael** quickly arrives and pulls Klarka away from the mansion. He gets her out of the situation and takes her to safety, ending her confrontation with Holic and Matthias.

---

## END OF SCENE

*This scene concludes with Raphael rescuing Klarka Pavlinka from the confrontation at Holic’s mansion.*

---

## WHAT THIS OUTLINE ADDS TO CANON

1. **Klarka’s Role:** Establishes Klarka Pavlinka as an active observer who is concerned enough about her mother and sister to follow them.
2. **Timeline Clarity:** The “other pet takes his time” detail explains why Klarka is able to follow Holic and GG after the earlier events.
3. **Limited Knowledge:** Because Klarka did not witness the park confrontation, her perspective on Simonka’s situation is different from Klára’s.
4. **Future Potential:** This scene can lead to Klarka telling Gamaliel, Klára, or another family member what she has seen, or to her attempting to reach out to Simonka directly.
