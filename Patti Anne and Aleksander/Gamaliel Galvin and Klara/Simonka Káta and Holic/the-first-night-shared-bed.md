# SCENE OUTLINE: THE FIRST NIGHT – SHARED COMFORT

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Simonka Káta (9), Klárka Pavlínka (10), Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára
- **Location:** University Hospital, Prague (Private Patient Room).
- **Date:** The night of the surgery.
- **Status:** Outline

---

## THE SETTING
- The hospital room is dimmed, lit only by the soft glow of the medical monitors and a small nightlight.
- The hum of the hospital—distant footsteps, the quiet whir of the ventilation—is the only sound.
- Simonka is in the center of the large hospital bed, looking small against the white linens.

---

## SCENE ARC

### 1. The Vigil
- Gamaliel and Klára are in the room, resting in the nearby armchairs, but they are not really asleep. They are watching Simonka.
- Simonka is restless. The anesthesia is wearing off, leaving her in that strange, itchy space between sleep and wakefulness. She isn't in pain, but she is *unsettled*.

### 2. Klárka’s Approach
- Klárka has been lying on the window seat, unable to settle. She sees Simonka’s hand twitching on the covers.
- She stands up and walks to the side of the bed. She doesn't ask her parents; she looks directly at Simonka.
- "Simi?" she whispers.
- Simonka’s eyes open. They are wide and glassy. "Klárka."

### 3. Joining the Bed
- Klárka looks at her parents. They both nod—they know the "Rules of Engagement" and they know their daughters.
- **The Move:** Klárka carefully climbs into the hospital bed. She is mindful of the bandages on Simonka’s right side, moving with a dancer’s precision to find a space that doesn't disturb the surgical site.
- She slides under the thin hospital blanket and settles in on Simonka’s left side.

### 4. The Calming
- Simonka immediately turns toward her sister. She tucks her head into the hollow of Klárka’s shoulder.
- Klárka wraps an arm around her, her hand resting gently on Simonka’s back, avoiding the tender areas.
- **The Physical Shift:** The monitors reflect the change almost instantly. Simonka’s heart rate slows. Her breathing becomes deeper and more rhythmic. The "unsettled" energy leaves her body, replaced by the familiar weight of her sister.

### 5. Falling Asleep
- Klárka whispers something in Czech—perhaps a fragment of a song or just a repeated "It's okay, I'm here."
- Simonka’s eyes flutter shut. For the first time since the surgery, she is truly at rest.
- Within minutes, Klárka’s own breathing matches Simonka’s. They fall asleep together, a single unit of shared DNA and shared history.

### 6. The Parents’ Witness
- Gamaliel and Klára watch from the shadows of the room. 
- They see the power of the bond they’ve nurtured. In this high-tech medical environment, the most effective "medicine" in the room is the presence of the older sister.
- Klára leans her head on Gamaliel’s shoulder. The first night—the hardest night—is handled.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES
- **Simonka (9):** Reaching for her "anchor." The hospital bed feels less like a cage and more like a home once Klárka is in it.
- **Klárka (10):** Taking her role as the sentinel seriously. She isn't just sleeping; she is protecting.
- **Gamaliel/Klára:** Trusting the girls' bond enough to let them share the bed, even with the surgical risks.

---

## SAGA CONNECTIONS
- **The Circle of Inclusion:** A physical manifestation of the protocol.
- **Sisterly Bond:** Solidifies that Klárka is Simonka’s primary source of emotional regulation during trauma.
