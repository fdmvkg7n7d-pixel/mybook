# SCENE OUTLINE: CLEAR MARGINS – NO DUST BEHIND

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára, Klárka Pavlínka (10), Simonka Káta (9)
- **Location:** Gamaliel’s office, Mike Key Studio, Prague.
- **Date:** One week after surgery.
- **Status:** Outline

---

## THE SETTING
- The office is filled with the afternoon sun. Outside, the muffled sound of a piano from a lower-level studio provides a rhythmic backdrop.
- The tension in the room is thick but quiet. They are waiting for the final phone call from Dr. Marek.

---

## SCENE ARC

### 1. The Wait
- Gamaliel is sitting at his desk, his phone placed precisely in the center of the blotter.
- Klára is standing by the window, watching the students arrive for the afternoon classes. Her posture is "stage-ready"—tense, upright, controlled.
- Simonka and Klárka are on the rug in the corner, looking at a book of Czech fairytales. They are close together, shoulders touching.

### 2. The Phone Call
- The phone rings. Gamaliel puts it on speaker immediately.
- **Dr. Marek's Voice:** "Gamaliel, I have the final pathology results for Simonka."
- He uses the clinical terms: *Negative margins. No lymph node involvement.*
- He translates it for the parents: "The stone is completely gone. We checked the edges of where it sat, and there is nothing left. She is clear."

### 3. The Release
- Klára’s shoulders finally drop. She leans her forehead against the cool glass of the window for a second before turning back to the room.
- Gamaliel lets out a breath he feels he has been holding since Crete. He reaches across the desk and takes Klára’s hand as she walks over.

### 4. Explaining "No Dust"
- The girls have looked up, sensing the shift in energy.
- **The Ask:** Gamaliel beckons them over. "Simi, Klárka, come here."
- **The Explanation:** Gamaliel kneels so he's below them. "Dr. Marek just called. You know how we talked about the stone being removed? Well, they looked at the spot where the stone lived under a very powerful microscope."
- **The Metaphor:** "They wanted to see if the stone left any dust behind. And the news is perfect: there is no dust. The spot is clean. You are healthy, Simi."

### 5. The "Tighten" Hug
- Klárka doesn't wait for permission this time. She throws her arms around Simonka in a fierce, sobbing "tighten" hug. 
- Simonka is quieter, her face buried in her sister’s neck. She looks over at her parents—the "hero" of the story finally getting to be just a little girl again.
- Klára and Gamaliel wrap their arms around both girls, forming a four-person huddle in the middle of the office.

### 6. The Transition to Normalcy
- Klára wipes her eyes and kisses the top of Simonka’s head. "Tonight, we celebrate. Whatever you want for dinner."
- Simonka thinks for a moment. "Pizza. And ice cream. The big ones from the square."
- The "medical mode" is officially deactivated. They are a family again, not a patient and her caregivers.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES
- **Gamaliel:** The bridge between the clinical and the emotional. He holds the "no dust" metaphor steady for his daughters.
- **Klára:** The relief is physical. For her, "clear margins" means her children are safe from the shadows of the past and the threats of the present.
- **Simonka (9):** Reclaiming her health. The "no dust" concept makes sense to her; it means the repair is permanent.
- **Klárka (10):** The sentinel finally gets to stand down. Her relief is the most explosive.

---

## SAGA CONNECTIONS
- **Closure:** This scene ends the acute phase of the carcinoma arc.
- **The "No Dust" Metaphor:** Becomes a family shorthand for being "all clear" in future check-ups.
