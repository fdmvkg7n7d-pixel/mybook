# SCENE OUTLINE: DAY 2 – CAMP AND THE SPECIALIST

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klárka Pavlínka (10), Simonka Káta (9), Dr. Marek (Specialist)
- **Location:** The Ballet Academy (Prague) and University Hospital (Pediatric Oncology).
- **Date:** The Tuesday after the Crete vacation.
- **Status:** Outline

---

## SCENE ARC

### 1. Morning at the Academy
- Both girls attend the summer camp at their parents' Ballet Academy.
- **The "Academy Norm":** Within the private, safe space of their own school, the girls are comfortable. Klárka and Simonka often practice shirtless in the warm studios, focused on their form.
- Klárka (10) keeps a protective eye on Simonka (9) during the morning breaks, bringing her water and checking in with small, supportive touches.

### 2. The Mid-Day Parting
- Gamaliel arrives after lunch to pick up Simonka for her appointment with the specialist, Dr. Marek.
- **The "Tighten" Hug:** At the studio door, Klárka and Simonka embrace. It is a fierce, silent hug. Klárka doesn't want to let her sister go toward the "unknown" of the hospital. Gamaliel has to gently separate them, promising Klárka he will call as soon as they are done.

### 3. Arriving at the University Hospital
- The environment is more clinical than Dr. Jelena’s office. Gamaliel and Simonka navigate the large corridors.
- Simonka is quiet, holding Gamaliel’s hand. She wears her light jacket over her bare chest, ready for the exam.

### 4. Meeting Dr. Marek
- Dr. Marek is a specialist in pediatric tumors. He is calm, precise, and highly respectful of children.
- **The Ask:** He waits for Simonka to settle on the exam table. "Simonka, Dr. Jelena told me you found a very interesting stone. May I have your permission to examine it now?"
- **The Response:** Simonka nods and opens her jacket. 
- **The Examination:** Dr. Marek performs a very thorough palpation. He doesn't just feel the lump; he checks the architecture of the surrounding tissue. He explains what he is doing as he goes, which keeps Simonka's anxiety low.

### 5. The Diagnosis Discussion
- Dr. Marek sits with Gamaliel and Simonka. 
- He uses the term **Secretory Breast Carcinoma**. He explains it is a "slow" kind of stone that shouldn't be there, and the best way to deal with it is to "take it out."
- He shows them the plan for a biopsy and surgery. He speaks directly to Simonka, explaining that she will go to sleep for a little while and when she wakes up, the stone will be gone.

### 6. Reunion and the Call to Cape Town
- Gamaliel and Simonka return to the Academy to pick up Klárka.
- The sisters hug again—a "tighten" hug of relief that the first big hurdle is over.
- That evening, the three of them sit on the floor of the living room in Prague and video call Klára in Cape Town to share the specialist’s plan.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES
- **Klárka Pavlínka (10):** Her "protective" energy is at an all-time high. She is the anchor for her sister at the Academy.
- **Simonka Káta (9):** Brave and curious. She treats the specialist visit like a rehearsal—something to be focused on and completed.
- **Gamaliel:** Processing the "Carcinoma" word while maintaining his role as the calm leader. He is already thinking about the "Board of Injustice" or how to ensure the best possible care.

- **Bodily Autonomy:** Reaffirms the "Simonka's Body, Simonka's Choice" protocol in a high-stakes medical setting.
