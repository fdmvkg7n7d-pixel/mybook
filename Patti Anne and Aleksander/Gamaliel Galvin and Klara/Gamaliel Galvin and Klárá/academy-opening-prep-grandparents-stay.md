# SCENE OUTLINE: TWO WEEKS IN PRAGUE – THE ACADEMY OPENING

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Patti Anne Key, Aleksander, Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára, Simonka Káta (9), Klárka Pavlínka (10)
- **Location:** The Key-Dimitrov Apartment and Pražská Baletní Akademie, Prague.
- **Date:** Late August, circa 2079.
- **Status:** Outline

---

## THE SETTING
- Prague in late August is warm and bustling. The city is preparing for the start of the school year.
- **Pražská Baletní Akademie** is a hive of activity—contractors finishing the floors, mirrors being polished, and the new sign being mounted.
- The Key-Dimitrov apartment is filled with the energy of a family working toward a massive milestone.

---

## SCENE ARC

### 1. The Decision to Stay
- Initially, Patti Anne and Aleksander were only going to stay for a few days following Simonka’s surgery.
- However, seeing the monumental task of opening **Pražská Baletní Akademie** in September, they decide to extend their stay for a full two weeks.
- "You two focus on the studios," Patti Anne tells Gamaliel and Klára. "We’ll handle the home front."

### 2. The Grandparents on the Watch
- While Gamaliel and Klára spend twelve-hour days at the academy, Patti Anne and Aleksander take over the daily routines.
- **Recovery & Academics:** Patti Anne ensures Simonka stays on track with her recovery, monitoring her activity level and keeping her comfortable. She also helps with the girls' academic prep for their own school start.
- **The Home Front:** Aleksander takes over the grocery shopping and cooking, filling the apartment with the smells of Canadian comfort food and local Czech ingredients.

### 3. Visits to the Academy
- Occasionally, the grandparents take the girls to the new building to see the progress.
- **The Vision:** They walk through the corridors of **Pražská Baletní Akademie**. Simonka and Klárka watch their parents—not as "Mami and Papa," but as the Directors of an institution. 
- They see the **Mike Key Studio**, the name gleaming on the door. It’s a powerful moment for Patti Anne, seeing her father’s legacy live on in the heart of Prague.

### 4. Preparation for September
- The energy is frantic but focused. Gamaliel is finalizing the business side—registrations, payroll, and schedules—while Klára is auditioning the final few students and setting the curriculum.
- Patti Anne helps Klára with the "softer" side of the opening—organizing the orientation materials and ensuring the student welfare protocols (including the "Ask" for bodily autonomy) are clearly documented for the new faculty.

### 5. Evening Debriefs
- Every night, the six of them gather for dinner at the apartment.
- Gamaliel and Klára are exhausted but exhilarated. They share stories of the day's progress, while the girls share stories of their adventures with "Babi and Děda."
- The two weeks act as a bridge—from the trauma of the "stone" to the triumph of the opening.

### 6. The "Tighten" Departure
- At the end of the two weeks, the academy is ready for its September 1st opening.
- As Patti Anne and Aleksander prepare to head to the airport, there is a round of fierce "tighten" hugs.
- The grandparents leave knowing that the family is not just healthy, but flourishing. They’ve helped build the foundation for the next chapter of the Prague legacy.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES
- **Gamaliel & Klára:** Grateful for the support. They couldn't have finished the academy prep while managing Simonka’s recovery without the help.
- **Patti Anne:** Finding deep satisfaction in being the "support staff" for her son’s dream.
- **Aleksander:** The quiet engine of the household, ensuring everything runs smoothly so the artists can work.
- **Simonka (9):** Fully recovered and ready for the new school year. The two weeks with her grandparents have solidified her sense of security.

---

## SAGA CONNECTIONS
- **Pražská Baletní Akademie:** Formalizes the name and the scale of the institution.
- **The Key-Dimitrov Legacy:** Shows the intergenerational effort required to build the family's future.
- **September 1st:** A recurring date of significance in the saga for new beginnings.
