# Bruno and GG

## Overview
A short story / scene collection following the relationship between two dogs: Bruno and Good Girl (GG).

**Bruno**  
- Male dog with a notably dual temperament.  
- Angry, territorial, and aggressive toward other dogs.  
- Extremely gentle, patient, and affectionate with his lover GG.

**Good Girl (GG)**  
- Female dog, often referred to as GG.  
- Frequently kept very busy by her owner(s) with weekend activities.  
- Described by one owner as a "professional at getting mounted."  
- Eager to demonstrate her skills for Holic.  
- Central to a proposed breeding arrangement with Bruno.

**Holic**  
- Person who picks up Good Girl on Sunday evening.  
- GG shows strong excitement and affection toward him.  
- GG actively shows him how she takes Bruno.  
- Thoughtful about the breeding proposal — wants time to consider and defers to GG's decision.  
- Plans to discuss the deal with Simonka Kata at his mansion.

**Simonka Kata**  
- Person Holic will discuss the breeding deal with at his mansion.

## Key Scenes

### The Run (Weekend)
The owner had kept GG very busy for the whole weekend. Despite the packed schedule, the owner still decided to let both dogs out for a run in the enclosed dog area.

As soon as they were released inside the run, Bruno took GG immediately — mounting her using her third hole.

### Sunday Pickup (~6:00 PM)
On Sunday around six o'clock, Holic arrives to pick up Good Girl.

GG is very excited to see Holic.

The other owner tells Holic that GG is a professional at getting mounted.

### GG Shows Holic How She Takes Bruno
Shortly after the pickup, GG takes the initiative to show Holic exactly how she takes Bruno.

She demonstrates her experience and skill for him, actively performing and showing off the act (including the third-hole mounting).

During the demonstration, Holic puts on a blindfold.

Afterward, Holic puts a blindfold on GG for another test (per the club's rules). Bruno does not come along. He gives her commands and guidance in his limousine and at the mansion so she can heighten her other senses. GG feels a little bit weird being blindfolded but lets Holic lead her anywhere. Specific commands include leading her to the washing station to wash mud off her body, "Get into the crate," "Go to his bedroom and sleep on the floor," and crawling to the attached kennel if bathroom is needed. The test continues at the mansion until morning (in preparation for discussing the breeding deal with Simonka Kata).

### The Other Owner Proposes a Breeding Deal
During the Sunday pickup, the other owner proposes a deal to Holic: GG will get pregnant with Bruno. Holic can keep the baby (the puppy).

While they are talking, GG is busy with Bruno.

Holic says he needs to think about the deal and emphasizes that it is ultimately GG's decision. He will discuss it with Simonka Kata at his mansion.

## Character Notes
- Bruno shows strong protective and mating instincts specifically toward GG, remaining gentle with her.
- GG is responsive to Bruno even when tired from a busy weekend.
- GG becomes highly enthusiastic and affectionate when Holic comes to collect her.
- GG deliberately shows off her sexual skills and experience to Holic.
- The other owner is interested in arranging a pregnancy between GG and Bruno and offers that Holic can keep the resulting puppy.
- Holic is cautious and respectful, insisting on time to consider and that GG's choice matters. He plans to talk it over with Simonka Kata.
- Simonka Kata is the person Holic will consult at his mansion regarding the breeding deal.
- The handling of GG at the mansion (blindfold test, commands, crating, kennel access) follows the club's rules.
