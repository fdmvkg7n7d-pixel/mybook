# SCENE OUTLINE: THE SEPARATION AFTER CRETE

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára, Klárka Pavlínka (10), Simonka Káta (9)
- **Location:** The family home in Prague / The front door.
- **Date:** Immediately following the Crete vacation (Summer, circa 2079).
- **Status:** Outline

---

## THE CONTEXT
- The vacation in Crete has ended. The discovery of Simonka’s lump hangs over the family, but the scheduled commitments remain.
- **Klára’s Departure:** Klára has left for Cape Town. 
- **The Ballet Academy Summer Camp:** This is a **day camp** held at Gamaliel and Klára’s own Ballet Academy in Prague. The girls do not stay overnight; they commute from home with Gamaliel.

---

## SCENE ARC

### 1. The Morning Routine (Day 1)
- Gamaliel wakes the girls in their home in Prague. The house feels different without Klára.
- They prepare for the day: dance bags, water bottles, and light summer clothes.

### 2. The Goodbye at the Front Door (Klára's Departure)
- This is the moment Klára leaves for the airport.
- **The Sisters' Hug:** At the front door, Klárka Pavlínka and Simonka Káta embrace. It is a "tighten" hug—a deep, fierce squeeze that expresses their shared anxiety about the "stone" and their mother's departure. They hold on longer than usual.
- Klára hugs them both, promising to call as soon as she lands in South Africa.

### 3. Heading to the Academy (Day 1)
- Gamaliel takes both girls to the Ballet Academy. 
- Klárka (10) joins the senior intensive group.
- Simonka (9) stays with Gamaliel for the morning before her appointment with the regular pediatrician, Dr. Jelena.

### 4. The Logistical Flow
- After the appointment with Dr. Jelena (where the urgent referral is made), Gamaliel and Simonka return home.
- They pick up Klárka from the Academy at the end of the day. The sisters reunite with another "tighten" hug, as if checking that the other is still okay.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES
- **Gamaliel:** Stepping fully into the "anchor" role. He is the calm, steady presence for both girls as the family splits.
- **Klára:** Torn between her professional commitment in Cape Town and her maternal need to be with Simonka. She trusts Gamaliel implicitly but feels the weight of the distance.
- **Klárka Pavlínka (10):** Hiding her own anxiety behind her protective older-sister persona. The hug at the door is her way of saying "be brave" to Simonka.
- **Simonka Káta (9):** Feeling the absence of her mother and the upcoming medical reality. She clings to Klárka at the door, finding comfort in their shared physicality.

---

## SAGA CONNECTIONS
- **Family Resilience:** Shows the Key family’s ability to manage complex logistics during a crisis.
- **Sisterly Bond:** A key moment defining the lifelong relationship between Klárka and Simonka.
- **Prague Academy:** Mentions the summer camp at the Ballet Academy, linking the family’s personal life to their professional legacy.
