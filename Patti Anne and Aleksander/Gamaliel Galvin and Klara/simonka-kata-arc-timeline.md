# Simonka Káta / Holic / Family Discovery — Arc Timeline

## Purpose

This file organizes the current Simonka Káta arc in chronological order, especially the sequence around Holic Bartunek, GG, Klárka Pavlînka finding the diary, Gamaliel and Klára learning the truth, and the later mansion-visit fallout.

**Tone guardrail:** The arc should remain emotionally and ethically focused. The scenes may contain adult kink/BDSM context, but the family-discovery thread works best when it stays **non-explicit**, centered on autonomy, fear, shame, consent, coercion, secrecy, and the limits of rescue.

# I. Long-Range Backstory Before Holic

## 1. Childhood / Family Ground

- Simonka Káta is the **youngest daughter** of Gamaliel Galvin Key and Klára Key.
- She is **fifteen months younger** than Klárka Pavlînka.
- She grows up in Prague inside the ballet academy her parents built from Mike Key and Terri Anne's legacy.
- Unlike her parents and sister, Simonka chooses **psychology / academia** rather than ballet.
- She studies at **Charles University**.
- The family doctrine around love, rescue, dogs, open doors, and bodily autonomy all shape the crisis later.
