# SCENE OUTLINE: THE SECRET PACT AND SEPTEMBER FIRST

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Simonka Káta (9), Klárka Pavlínka (10), Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára, Patti Anne Key, Aleksander
- **Location:** The Key-Dimitrov Apartment / Pražská Baletní Akademie.
- **Date:** August 31st and September 1st, circa 2079.
- **Status:** Outline

---

## THE SETTING
- The evening before the grand opening of the academy and the start of the school year.
- The apartment is quiet, filled with the scent of fresh flowers and ironed clothes.
- The tension is no longer about health, but about social perception.

---

## SCENE ARC

### 1. The Night Before: The Pact
- Simonka is trying on her outfit for the Grand Opening. She looks at herself in the mirror, checking to see if any hint of the bandages or the scar shows through the fabric. 
- **The Request:** She turns to her family. "I don't want anyone to know," she says, her voice small but firm. "I don't want to be 'the sick girl' at school or at the Academy. I want to be just Simonka."
- **The Agreement:** Gamaliel and Klára immediately agree. They recognize her need for a clean slate. Klárka nods solemnly, taking her role as the "secret-keeper" just as seriously as her role as "protector."

### 2. The Grand Opening (September 1st)
- **Pražská Baletní Akademie** officially opens its doors. The crowd is filled with local dignitaries, excited parents, and new students.
- Simonka is a "perfect" daughter—polite, smiling, and poised. She wears a dress that keeps her shoulders and chest fully covered. 
- Inside, she feels a strange thrill. She is carrying a massive secret. Everyone sees the Director's daughter; no one knows about the "stone" or the hospital room. This is her first experience with the power of a hidden life.

### 3. The First Day of School (Academic)
- The girls head to their regular academic school in Prague.
- Simonka feels "hyper-aware." When a friend asks why she was away during the summer, she gives a practiced, vague answer about "family travels" in Crete and Australia. 
- She watches her classmates, wondering what secrets they are carrying. She realizes that everyone has a "public" face and a "private" one.

### 4. The Dance Block: The New Routine
- While the other girls head to the studio at the Academy, Simonka goes to Gamaliel's office as planned.
- To her classmates, she is "taking a break from dance to focus on advanced academics." She lets them believe this. It feels better than telling them the truth.
- Klárka stays at the studio, her eyes darting toward the office window occasionally, ensuring the secret is safe and her sister is okay.

### 5. The Grandparent Departure
- After the opening ceremony, it’s time for Patti Anne and Aleksander to leave.
- In the quiet of the apartment, away from the public eye, Patti Anne gives Simonka one last hug. "You are more than your secrets, Simi. But your secrets are yours to keep."
- They leave, and the "Prague Four" are left to navigate their new normal.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES
- **Simonka (9):** Embracing her secretiveness. It gives her a sense of control and power in a world that recently felt very out of control.
- **Klárka (10):** The silent sentinel. She is the only peer who knows the truth, and she guards it fiercely.
- **Gamaliel & Klára:** Balancing their pride in the Academy with their commitment to Simonka's privacy.

---

## SAGA CONNECTIONS
- **Foundational Secrecy:** This is the origin of Simonka’s "intensely secretive" personality described in the Holic/GG arc.
- **Public vs. Private:** Establishes the theme of a family legacy (the Academy) masking a private medical reality.
- **Academic Path:** Her "academic excuse" for not dancing becomes the first step toward her life of the mind at Charles University.
