# SCENE OUTLINE: PATTI ANNE AND ALEKSANDER’S CHRISTMAS VISIT AND TRAVELS

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Characters:** Patti Anne Key, Aleksander, Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára Key, Klárka Pavlînka (6), Simonka Káta (5), Palmer Peter, Salena Sarai
- **Locations:** Prague (Gamaliel and Klára’s home and ballet academy), Saudi Arabia (Palmer Peter), Australia (Salena Sarai’s trip)
- **Date:** Christmas period
- **Status:** New Scene Outline

---

## THE SETTING

- Patti Anne and Aleksander travel to Prague to spend Christmas with Gamaliel, Klára, and the girls (Klárka and Simonka).
- This is the first Christmas after the twins (Palmer Peter and Salena Sarai) have turned eighteen and left home.
- After Christmas in Prague, Patti Anne and Aleksander plan to visit Palmer Peter in Saudi Arabia.
- Salena Sarai is taking an extended trip across Australia, starting in Sydney, before beginning university in Perth in early February.

---

## STORY ARC

### 1. Arrival in Prague for Christmas

- Patti Anne and Aleksander arrive in Prague a few days before Christmas.
- They stay with Gamaliel and Klára.
- The visit is warm but carries the emotional weight of the twins having recently left home.
- Klárka and Simonka are excited to see their grandparents and show them around the ballet academy where they have just started attending.

### 2. Christmas in Prague

- The family spends Christmas together in Prague.
- Gamaliel and Klára host, balancing the celebration with the girls’ OI accommodations (medication, careful physical interactions, and modified activities).
- Patti Anne and Aleksander are attentive to the girls’ needs without being overly emotional or dramatic.
- There are quiet moments where the absence of the twins is felt, but the focus remains on the present family.

### 3. Post-Christmas Plans

- After Christmas, Patti Anne and Aleksander prepare to continue their travels.
- Their next stop is Saudi Arabia to visit Palmer Peter.
- They discuss their itinerary with Gamaliel and Klára before departing.

### 4. Patti Anne and Aleksander Visit Palmer Peter in Saudi Arabia

- Patti Anne and Aleksander travel to Saudi Arabia to see Palmer Peter.
- Palmer Peter is preparing to begin a business degree at university in Saudi Arabia, with studies starting in September.
- The visit allows them to check on their son and maintain family connection across distances before his academic year begins.

### 5. Salena Sarai’s Extended Trip in Australia

- Salena Sarai begins her journey in Sydney and takes approximately two months to travel across Australia.
- She is exploring the country independently before starting university in Perth at the beginning of February.
- The family is aware of her plans and supports her decision to take this extended journey before committing to her studies.

### 6. Family Reflections

- Back in Prague, Gamaliel and Klára reflect on the changes in the family.
- With the twins now living independently (Palmer Peter preparing for university in Saudi Arabia, Salena Sarai traveling across Australia), the household feels smaller but still full with the two young daughters.
- The Christmas visit and the parents’ subsequent travels highlight the family’s ongoing commitment to staying connected despite geographic distance.
