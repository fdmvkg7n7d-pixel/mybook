# SCENE OUTLINE: GAMALIEL RETURNS – KLÁRA’S EMOTIONAL REUNION AND SUMMER CAMP DECISION

## SCENE INFORMATION

- **Characters:** Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára Key, Klárka Pavlînka (6), Simonka Káta (5)
- **Location:** The family apartment in Prague
- **Date:** Tuesday afternoon (two days after the ER visit), circa 2079
- **Status:** New Scene Outline

---

## THE SETTING

- Tuesday afternoon. Gamaliel returns from his trip to Paris (ballet academy auditions).
- The apartment is quiet. Klárka is resting on the couch with her right foot buddy-taped. Simonka sits close beside her.
- Gamaliel enters with his suitcase, expecting a normal homecoming.

---

## STORY ARC

### 1. Gamaliel’s Arrival

- Gamaliel comes through the door.
- He immediately senses that something is off. The usual energy is missing.
- He sees Klárka’s taped foot and the serious expressions on everyone’s faces.

### 2. Klára’s Emotional Breakdown

- Klára walks to him without speaking.
- As soon as Gamaliel opens his arms, she collapses into them.
- She begins to cry — deep, shaking sobs she has been holding in since Sunday.
- Gamaliel holds her carefully, realizing this is not a normal “I missed you” moment.

### 3. The Story Unfolds

- Through tears, Klára tells Gamaliel everything that has happened:
  - Sunday morning: Klárka breaking her foot while running with Simonka.
  - The Emergency Room visit and the doctor’s probing questions.
  - Klára disclosing her own childhood trauma at age seven.
  - Monday’s follow-up appointment with Dr. Jelena.
  - The formal diagnosis of **Osteogenesis Imperfecta Type 1** for both girls.

### 4. Gamaliel’s Reaction

- Gamaliel is shocked but stays calm and steady for Klára.
- He listens fully, asking gentle clarifying questions.
- He expresses relief that Klára was honest with the ER doctor and acted quickly.

### 5. The Girls’ Stubborn Insistence on Summer Camp

- After the initial emotional release, Klára explains the girls’ position.
- Both Klárka and Simonka have been very clear: they still want to attend the **summer camp** at the ballet academy.
- Klára tells Gamaliel, with a mix of exhaustion and reluctant admiration:

  > “They’re being so stubborn about it. Klárka especially. She says she’s not going to miss summer camp just because of a broken toe. Simonka backs her up completely. They keep saying they want to go — no matter what. They’re digging their heels in.”

- The girls’ stubbornness is the driving force. They refuse to be sidelined or treated as fragile, even with the new diagnosis. They want to participate in the familiar world of the academy on their own terms.

### 6. Family Discussion

- Gamaliel and Klára talk about how to handle the girls’ determination.
- They acknowledge that while safety must come first, completely removing the girls from the academy would likely cause more emotional harm than good.
- They decide they will support the girls’ wish to attend, but with clear modifications and extra precautions in place.

### 7. Speaking with the Girls

- Gamaliel and Klára sit down with Klárka and Simonka that afternoon.
- They acknowledge the girls’ stubbornness with gentle humor and seriousness.
- They explain that they can go to summer camp, but certain activities will be adapted because of the OI diagnosis.
- Both girls accept the conditions, satisfied that they are not being excluded.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES

- **Klára:** Emotionally exhausted and overwhelmed. Her breakdown in Gamaliel’s arms shows how much she has been carrying alone. She presents the girls’ stubbornness with a mix of frustration and pride.
- **Gamaliel:** Supportive and grounding. He absorbs the news and focuses on finding a practical path forward.
- **Klárka Pavlînka (6):** Fiercely stubborn and determined. She refuses to let the diagnosis or injury stop her from doing what she loves.
- **Simonka Káta (5):** Quietly but firmly aligned with her sister. Her stubbornness shows up as quiet solidarity.
