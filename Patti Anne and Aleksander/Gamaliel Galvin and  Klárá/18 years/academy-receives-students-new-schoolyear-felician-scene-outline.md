# Scene Outline: The Academy Receives Students for the New Schoolyear — Felician Arrives

## Scene Information
- **Characters:** Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára Key, Klárka Pavlînka, Felician, Svjetlana, Agrippine, Jelka, Celestina, academy teachers, dorm staff, office staff, new and returning students.
- **Location:** Pražská Baletní Akademie, Prague.
- **Date:** September first, opening morning of the new schoolyear.
- **Status:** New scene outline.

---

## The Setting
- The academy is receiving students for the new schoolyear.
- The morning is completely crazy.
- Students are arriving with luggage, garment bags, pointe shoes, water bottles, digital medical records, scholarship confirmations, security wristband assignments, and anxious parents.
- Returning students think they know where they are going, but the new schedules have changed everything.
- New students are trying to look brave while secretly watching every doorway.
- Parents are asking questions faster than the staff can answer them.
- Dorm staff are checking room assignments.
- Office staff are correcting spelling mistakes on badges and iPad records.
- Teachers are trying to keep the hallways moving.
- Gamaliel and Klára are both everywhere at once.

---

## Story Arc

### Beat 1: The Front Entrance Chaos
- The scene opens at the front entrance of Pražská Baletní Akademie.
- The doors are open.
- Students are arriving in waves.
- Suitcases roll over the floor.
- Parents stop in the doorway to ask questions.
- Younger students freeze because the building suddenly feels larger than it did during the tour.
- Older students move through the chaos with practiced confidence.
- Staff members call names from iPads.
- Several students answer at once because too many names sound similar in the crowded hallway.
- The school feels alive, overcrowded, excited, and slightly out of control.

### Beat 2: Gamaliel Controls the System
- Gamaliel stations himself near the registration tables.
- He keeps the digital administrative side moving.
- He checks rosters on his iPad.
- He solves schedule problems.
- He sends staff to the dormitories, studios, classrooms, and uniform room.
- He notices which parents need reassurance and which students need to be moved quickly before they panic.
- His calmness becomes the center of the entrance hall.
- The crazier the morning becomes, the quieter and more precise he gets.

### Beat 3: Klára Receives the Children
- Klára works closer to the students than to the paperwork.
- She watches faces, posture, hands, and breathing.
- She can tell which children are excited, which children are frightened, and which children are pretending not to be frightened.
- She redirects overwhelmed students before they shut down.
- She sends confident returning students to help carry bags.
- She greets new students by name whenever she can.
- Her job is not only to receive paperwork.
- Her job is to receive children into the life of the school.

### Beat 4: Felician Appears in the Crowd
- Felician arrives with Jelka and Celestina.
- She is one of the new students entering after Klára’s audition.
- Her name has already been added to the school’s accepted-student list on the academy iPads.
- She is also listed as a full-scholarship student.
- Jelka carries part of the luggage.
- Celestina carries the iPad confirmation with the scholarship and enrollment documents already loaded.
- Felician tries to stand like a serious academy student even though the entrance hall is overwhelming.
- She recognizes the building from the audition tour, but this morning feels different.
- During the audition, she was one child on one floor with Klára watching her.
- Today, she is one student among hundreds.

### Beat 5: The Name Problem
- An office staff member cannot find Felician’s name quickly enough on the first iPad roster.
- The moment is small, but Felician feels it immediately.
- Jelka stiffens.
- Celestina reaches for the iPad confirmation.
- The staff member swipes from the scholarship list to the dormitory list.
- The hallway noise makes the mistake feel larger than it is.
- Klára notices from across the entrance hall.
- She crosses over before Felician can feel lost.
- Klára confirms Felician’s name herself.
- Klára makes clear that Felician belongs there.
- The correction is immediate and public enough for Felician to hear it.
- The staff member updates the roster properly on the iPad.
- Felician’s name is now secure in the morning’s digital system.

### Beat 6: Klára Remembers the Audition
- Klára remembers the audition that brought Felician into the academy.
- She remembers that Klárka was supposed to run it.
- She remembers stepping in when Klárka could not.
- She remembers seeing enough in Felician to accept her.
- She remembers offering the scholarship before the parents had to ask.
- That memory sits underneath the chaos of the morning.
- Felician is not just another new student to Klára.
- Felician is a promise the academy made.

### Beat 7: Felician Crosses the Threshold
- Once her name is confirmed, Felician is issued her student iPad, dorm assignment, class schedule, and academy security wristband.
- Her scholarship status is checked off.
- Her uniform fitting time is marked.
- Her first studio assignment is circled.
- Her room number is displayed clearly on the iPad so Jelka and Celestina can see it.
- A staff member gives Felician her security wristband.
- The wristband marks her as a registered academy student for the new schoolyear.
- The wristband controls access to the correct dormitory side.
- The wristband identifies whether a student belongs to the girl dormitory or the boy dormitory.
- The wristband also identifies the student’s grade-level security group.
- The grade-level security groups are Grade One to Grade Five, Grade Six to Grade Eight, and Grade Nine to Grade Twelve.
- Felician’s wristband places her with the girl dormitory and the Grade One to Grade Five group.
- Felician holds still while the wristband is put on her wrist.
- Felician holds the iPad carefully because it feels official.
- The iPad and security wristband turn the dream from the audition into a schoolyear.
- She steps fully into the academy as a student.

### Beat 8: Klárka Sees Felician
- Klárka appears from one of the corridors, already pulled into the madness of the first morning.
- She sees Felician near the registration tables.
- The recognition is immediate.
- Felician was the audition Klárka could not conduct.
- Felician is also proof that the academy continued when Klárka was struggling.
- Klárka does not make the moment heavy.
- She gives Felician a teacher’s acknowledgment and keeps moving.
- Felician straightens because Klárka has seen her.
- For Felician, that brief recognition matters almost as much as the paperwork.

### Beat 9: Klára Sees the Girls on the Bench
- Later in the morning, Klára sees Felician, Svjetlana, and Agrippine sitting together on one of the benches outside.
- The bench is near the academy entrance, close enough to the flow of arriving students but far enough from the worst of the chaos.
- The girls are wearing their new security wristbands.
- Klára can tell from the wristbands that they are assigned to the girl dormitory.
- Klára can also see their grade-level security group at a glance.
- The three girls look like they are trying to make themselves small while the schoolyear begins around them.
- Klára notices the stillness immediately.
- She decides she should talk with them.
- She crosses the outside area without making the approach feel like a reprimand.
- Felician notices Klára first and becomes visibly nervous.
- Svjetlana and Agrippine look to Felician because she is the one who has already met Klára through the audition.
- Klára sits or stands near them in a way that makes conversation possible without trapping them.
- The conversation begins as a check-in about their first morning, their dorm assignments, and whether they know where they are supposed to go next.

### Beat 10: Felician Apologizes for Watching Practice
- In the middle of the conversation, Felician apologizes.
- She explains that she went into a studio to watch a practice class.
- She is worried that she broke a rule.
- She is worried that being new means she should not have entered without being invited.
- Svjetlana and Agrippine become quiet because they also watched or followed her in.
- Felician’s apology is sincere and anxious.
- She is not trying to make an excuse.
- She is trying to protect herself and the other girls before someone else corrects them.
- Klára understands immediately that the girls were curious, not disruptive.
- Klára reassures them that they were allowed to watch the practice class.
- Klára explains that watching is part of learning at the academy.
- Students learn by dancing, but they also learn by observing older students, teachers, corrections, posture, musicality, discipline, and studio etiquette.
- Klára makes clear that the rule is not that they must never watch.
- The rule is that they must watch respectfully, stay quiet, remain out of the dancers’ way, and leave if a teacher asks them to leave.
- Felician relaxes when she realizes she has not damaged her place at the school.
- Svjetlana and Agrippine relax with her.
- The conversation turns the outside bench from a hiding place into a first small community.

### Beat 11: Jelka and Celestina Let Go
- Jelka and Celestina are guided toward the parent check-out point.
- The academy makes the separation orderly because lingering parents make the hallways worse.
- Jelka trusts the school more quickly.
- Celestina needs one more look at Felician before she leaves.
- Felician is nervous, but she does not ask to go home.
- She has her iPad.
- She has her schedule loaded on the iPad.
- She has her security wristband on her wrist.
- She has her name in the system.
- She has Klára’s recognition from the audition.
- She lets the staff lead her toward the dormitory wing.

### Beat 12: The School Swallows the Morning
- The entrance hall keeps filling.
- Another student has the wrong shoes.
- Another parent forgot to upload a medical form.
- A dorm key stops working.
- A teacher needs two missing students for placement class.
- A suitcase breaks open near the stairs.
- Someone cries in the corner and is immediately collected by a dorm supervisor.
- The chaos does not stop.
- Gamaliel keeps the system moving.
- Klára keeps receiving the children.
- Felician disappears into the flow of the academy, no longer a visitor or an auditioning child, but a student.

### Beat 13: Final Image
- By late morning, the entrance hall is still noisy but more organized.
- The first wave of students has reached dorms, classrooms, and studios.
- Felician’s name is no longer an open question.
- Her student iPad has been issued.
- Her academy security wristband has been given.
- Her scholarship has been checked.
- Her dorm placement has been confirmed.
- Klára sees Felician once more outside with Svjetlana and Agrippine, their security wristbands visible and their faces less frightened because the bench conversation has settled them.
- The academy has received her.
- The new schoolyear has begun.
