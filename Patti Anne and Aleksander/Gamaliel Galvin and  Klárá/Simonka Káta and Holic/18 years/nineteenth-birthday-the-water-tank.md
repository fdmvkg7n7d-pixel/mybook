# SCENE OUTLINE: THE WATER TANK – WEIGHTLESSNESS AND INTENSITY

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Simonka Káta (19), Alois
- **Location:** The Outbuilding, Alois’s Farm.
- **Date:** The day of Simonka’s Nineteenth Birthday (August 2089).
- **Status:** Outline

---

## THE SETTING
- A private, temperature-controlled outbuilding near the farm house.
- At the center of the room is a massive, custom-built **water tank** holding **five thousand gallons** of water. It is a deep, enclosed structure with a heavy lid and a small viewing window.
- The scale of the tank allows for complete freedom of movement—it is a small, private ocean rather than a mere tub.
- The room is silent except for the low hum of the tank’s heater and filtration system. The air is humid and smells of clean, warm water.

---

## SCENE ARC

### 1. The Sanctuary of the Tank
- Following the intensity of the night in the barn, Alois brings Simonka here.
- The water tank is her favorite part of the farm. With **five thousand gallons** of space, it is a vast, weightless playground. For a girl with **Osteogenesis Imperfecta**, this volume of water is the only place where she is truly free from the constraints of her body.
- **The Physics of Peace:** Inside the tank, the laws of gravity—the laws that threaten her bones every day—are suspended. The sheer depth of the 5,000 gallons means she can float, dive, and hang in mid-water without ever touching the bottom or the sides.

### 2. The Induction
- Simonka undresses and climbs into the tank. The water is precisely body-temperature.
- **The Transition:** As she lowers herself in, she feels the "load" leave her skeleton. Her joints relax. The anxiety of impact that she carries in her muscles—the "dancer's guard"—finally melts away.
- Alois closes the lid. The world outside disappears.

### 3. Sensory Immersion
- Simonka floats in the darkness. The silence is absolute, broken only by the sound of her own heartbeat and her slow, rhythmic breathing.
- **The "Stone" and the "Scar":** In the water, she cannot feel the scar from her operation. She is just a conscious mind floating in a warm void.
- This is the "life of the mind" she loves, but translated into a physical sensation. She is alone with her thoughts, her history, and her body, but her body feels light and invincible.

### 4. The Shared Immersion: Drahoslava
- **The Entry:** The lid of the tank opens briefly, letting in a sliver of light before **Drahoslava** slides into the warm water beside Simonka.
- **The Enclosure:** The space, already intimate, becomes a world built for two. The displacement of the water as Drahoslava settles in creates a slow, rhythmic wave that Simonka feels against her whole body.
- **The Connection:** Drahoslava understands the "Rules." Her hands find Simonka under the surface, moving with the heavy, liquid resistance that the tank provides.

### 5. Different and Unique Things (TBD)
- **The Exploration:** With five thousand gallons of space, Simonka and Drahoslava explore the unique physics of the deep water.
- **The Potential:** Because of the depth and weightlessness, they can experiment with movements and connections that the "land" world does not allow.
- **The Scale:** The specific activities, positions, and sensory experiments they engage in are **TBD**—to be explored as the saga develops. The focus remains on the freedom the 5,000-gallon void provides for Simonka’s fragile skeleton and her analytical mind.

### 6. Erotic Weightlessness
- The encounter is defined by the blurred boundaries of their bodies in the water. Every touch is magnified by the sensory deprivation of the tank.
- For Simonka, the pleasure is intensified by the safety. With Drahoslava, she isn't just "fragile Simonka"; she is part of a fluid, powerful engine of sensation.
- They move with a slow-motion intensity, the water acting as both a cushion and a conductor for their energy.

### 6. Emerging
- When the session ends, Simonka emerges from the tank.
- **The Return of Gravity:** As she steps out, she feels the weight of her bones return. It is a grounding, sobering moment, but she carries the "stillness" of the tank with her.
- Alois wraps her in a large, warm towel, applying the firm, steady pressure she loves. She is nineteen, she is safe, and she has found a way to be intense without being broken.
