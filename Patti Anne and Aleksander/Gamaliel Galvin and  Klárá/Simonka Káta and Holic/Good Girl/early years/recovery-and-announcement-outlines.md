# Recovery and Announcement Outlines

This file contains the outlines for Klárka Pavlînka's recovery and the intimate announcement of her pregnancy following the trauma at the Holic mansion.

---

## Scene 1: The Sanctuary (Recovery)

**Location:** Klárka’s bedroom/family home.
**Characters:** Klárka Pavlînka, Raphael, and occasionally Klárá.

**The Beats:**
1. **The Heavy Silence:** The scene opens with the oppressive quiet of a room designed for rest. Klárka is in bed, surrounded by comforts—soft blankets, dimmed lights, and the absence of the "outside" world.
2. **The Physical Toll:** The focus is on the lingering effects of the shock. She is exhausted, her sleep interrupted by flashes of the imagery from Holic's mansion (the pole, the words "Good Girl"). The nausea of the pregnancy and the nausea of the trauma blend into one continuous state of fragility.
3. **Raphael’s Vigil:** Raphael is the constant. He is not just her partner but her anchor. The scene emphasizes the quiet, domestic tenderness: him bringing her tea, reading to her, or simply holding her hand while she stares at the ceiling.
4. **The Shift in Perspective:** A moment of internal realization for Klárka. She places a hand on her stomach. The horror of what she saw Simonka become creates a fierce, protective instinct for her own unborn child. The pregnancy, which was a secret, becomes her primary reason to heal.
5. **The Maternal Care:** Klárá enters, providing a different kind of support—the practical, grounding care of a mother. She ensures Klárka is eating and resting, treating her with a softness that acknowledges both the pregnancy and the trauma.

---

## Scene 2: The Inner Circle (The Announcement)

**Location:** The family living area/dining room.
**Characters:** Klárka Pavlînka, Raphael, Gamaliel Galvin, and Klárá.

**The Beats:**
1. **The Controlled Environment:** The atmosphere is one of careful curation. The family has gathered to support Klárka, but there is a palpable tension in the air—the "elephant in the room" being the Simonka situation.
2. **The Dual Purpose:** The meeting is framed as a check-in on Klárka’s health and a final confirmation of her absence from the academy’s new building opening.
3. **The Announcement:** In a quiet, focused moment, Raphael and Klárka share the news: **the pregnancy is official.**
4. **The Intimate Reaction:** Because the circle is so small, the reaction is raw and immediate. 
    *   **Gamaliel and Klárá** are overcome with a complex mix of sudden joy and profound worry. There are tears, but they are tempered by the weight of the current crisis.
5. **The Collision of Emotions:** The joy of the new life is directly contrasted with the "death" of the Simonka they knew. The announcement happens in the shadow of the Holic mansion, making the baby feel like a beacon of hope in a very dark week.
6. **The Protective Pact:** A decision is made between the four of them to keep the pregnancy an absolute secret for the time being. They agree that Klárka needs total peace to recover from the trauma, and any public attention or "celebration" would be too taxing.
7. **The Simonka Shadow:** A heavy, shared silence as they acknowledge the tragedy of Simonka. They discuss the pain of knowing a new generation is coming while another is being lost to the "Good Girl" persona.
8. **The Final Note:** The scene ends with a sense of tight-knit family unity. The four of them form a protective shell around Klárka and the baby, agreeing to face the fallout of the Simonka situation as a unified front.
