# Arrival at Holic’s Mansion — Scene Outline

## Placement

**When:** Immediately after Holic picks Good Girl up from the clinic.  
**Where:** Holic Bartunek’s mansion.  
**Focus:** Their arrival and Good Girl’s reaction when placed in the run.

---

## Characters

- **Holic Bartunek** — Good Girl’s owner and steward.
- **Good Girl (Simonka Káta / GG)** — Recovering after the trauma.

---

## Scene Outline

### Beat 1: Arrival at the Mansion

Holic’s car pulls up in front of the mansion. He gets out first, still holding Good Girl’s leash. Good Girl crawls out of the car behind him, moving slowly due to her injuries. The leash remains clipped to her dog collar.

Holic walks at a steady pace toward the side entrance of the mansion. Good Girl crawls beside him without resistance.

### Beat 2: The Run

Holic leads Good Girl to the large private run attached directly to the kitchen — a fenced outdoor area connected by a wide open doorway. He opens the gate and steps inside.

Good Girl follows him in.

Once inside, Holic unclips the leash from her collar. He looks down at her.

> “Stay here for now.”

He turns and walks toward the gate to leave.

### Beat 3: Good Girl’s Reaction

As soon as Holic steps away, Good Girl begins to **whimper**.

It starts quietly — a soft, broken sound from the back of her throat. As Holic reaches the gate, the whimpering grows louder and more distressed. She does not bark or speak. She only whimpers, her body trembling slightly as she remains on all fours.

Holic stops at the gate.

He turns around and looks at her. Good Girl is staring at him with wide, frightened eyes, still whimpering. The sound is raw and vulnerable — the sound of someone who is terrified of being left alone again.

### Beat 4: Holic’s Response

Holic stands still for several seconds, watching her.

A dark thought crosses his mind:

**Those bastards. What kind of men do this to a girl?**

He slowly walks back into the run and crouches down in front of her. He places a hand on the back of her neck.

> “You don’t have to stay out here,” he says quietly. “You can come into the mansion.”

Good Girl’s whimpering slowly quiets. She lifts her head and looks at him, still trembling.

Holic stands up and turns toward the open doorway that leads into the kitchen.

Without needing further instruction, Good Girl begins crawling forward. She crawls out of the run, through the kitchen, and continues down the hallway. Holic walks behind her at a slow pace, watching her.

Good Girl crawls all the way to Holic’s bedroom. She stops beside the bed and remains on all fours, waiting.

---

## Emotional Notes

- Good Girl’s whimpering reveals deep fear of abandonment after the trauma.
- Holic shows unexpected patience and gentleness.
- This moment highlights how much the recent events have affected her sense of safety.
