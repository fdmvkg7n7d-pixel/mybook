# Holic Picks Up Good Girl from the Clinic — Scene Outline

## Placement

**When:** The morning after Holic’s 9:00 AM visit to the clinic.  
**Where:** The Collegium Club’s private medical facility.  
**Focus:** Holic arriving to pick up Good Girl and take her home.

---

## Characters

- **Holic Bartunek** — Good Girl’s owner and steward.
- **Good Girl (Simonka Káta / GG)** — Recovering after the trauma.
- **The Club Doctor**
- **The Assistant Doctor**

---

## Scene Outline

### Beat 1: Holic’s Arrival

Holic arrives at the medical facility just before 10:00 AM. He walks directly to the recovery area.

### Beat 2: Finding Good Girl

Good Girl is still inside one of the cages.

She is completely naked, curled up on the padded floor. Her bandages are fresh. When she hears Holic’s footsteps, she slowly lifts her head.

### Beat 3: The Assistant Doctor Explains

The assistant doctor approaches Holic quietly.

> “She spent most of yesterday in the run with the other pets. She was comforting them. Even though she was injured, she kept trying to calm the others down. She only returned to the cage late last night.”

Holic listens in silence, his expression unreadable. He looks at Good Girl inside the cage.

### Beat 4: Taking Her Home

Holic walks over to the cage and opens the door. He crouches down.

> “It’s time to go home, Simonka Káta.”

Good Girl stares at him for a moment, then slowly crawls out of the cage toward him. She presses her forehead against his leg.

Holic gently helps her to her feet and supports her as they walk out of the medical facility.

Before they leave, the doctor hands him a bag with her medications.

> “Call us if anything changes.”

### Beat 5: Leaving the Clinic

As they step outside into the morning light, Good Girl suddenly stops crawling. She looks up at Holic and begins to bark — short, soft, grateful barks.

It is her way of saying thank you.

Holic stops walking and looks down at her. For a moment, he says nothing. Then he speaks quietly:

> “You’re welcome.”

He gently tugs the leash once, and they continue walking — Good Girl crawling, Holic walking — toward the waiting car.

---

## Emotional Notes

- Good Girl’s instinct to comfort other pets, even while injured, reveals her deep conditioning and gentle nature.
- Holic is quietly affected by what he learns about her behavior in the run.
- The pickup marks the beginning of her return to his care.
