# SCENE OUTLINE: THE CONSENT TO TOUCH

## SCENE INFORMATION
- **Characters:** Gamaliel Galvin Key, Klára, Simonka Káta (9), Klárka Pavlínka (10)
- **Location:** The girls' bedroom in the stone villa, Vamos, Crete.
- **Date:** The second-to-last day of their vacation (Summer, circa 2079).
- **Status:** Outline

---

## THE SETTING
- The girls' bedroom is a cool, white-walled room with two twin beds and wooden shutters half-closed against the late afternoon sun.
- Simonka and Klárka are both shirtless, still in their shorts from the day's swimming.
- The atmosphere is intimate and domestic, suddenly sharpened by the disclosure.

---

## SCENE ARC

### 1. The Disclosure in the Room
- The family has gathered in the girls' bedroom as they start to think about packing.
- Simonka is sitting on her bed. She looks at her parents and then at her sister before speaking.
- "I found a stone," she says quietly, pointing to the lump near her nipple.

### 2. Klárka’s Reaction
- Klárka (10) stops what she is doing immediately. She moves to sit on the edge of Simonka’s bed, her face a mix of curiosity and instant, fierce protectiveness.
- She looks at the lump but doesn't touch it. She looks at her parents, waiting to see how they will react.

### 3. The Pause for Consent (Klára)
- Klára kneels on the floor by the bed to be at eye level with Simonka. She acknowledges Klárka’s presence with a small nod, keeping the older sister included.
- **The Ask:** Klára looks Simonka in the eye. "Simi, thank you for telling us. To understand what it is, I would like to feel it with my fingers. Is it okay if I touch you there?"
- **The Response:** Simonka looks at Klárka, then back at her mother, and nods.
- **The Touch:** Klára gently palpates the lump while Klárka watches with focused intensity, as if she is trying to learn how to check for it herself.

### 4. The Second Ask (Gamaliel)
- Gamaliel stands by the foot of the bed. He makes sure Klárka feels part of the circle.
- **The Ask:** "Simi, would it be alright if Papa felt it too? It helps me know what to tell the doctors back in Prague."
- **Simonka’s Choice:** Simonka takes a moment, then reaches out and takes Klárka’s hand for support. "Yes, Papa. You can feel it."
- **The Touch:** Gamaliel feels the lump. He is careful to keep his movements slow and predictable.

### 5. Including Klárka
- Klárka asks, "Can I feel it?"
- Gamaliel and Klára look at each other. They turn the question to Simonka. "It's Simonka's body, Klárka. You have to ask her."
- Klárka asks her sister softly. Simonka agrees. Klárka touches the "stone" with one finger, her eyes wide. This shared touch binds the sisters together in the secret.

### 6. Affirming Autonomy and Family Unity
- Once the examination is over, they all sit together on the bed.
- Gamaliel says, "Thank you for letting us check, Simi. It’s your body, and you’re the boss of who touches it."
- He looks at both girls. "We are going to take care of this as a family. When we get to Prague, we'll go to the big hospital and find out exactly what the stone is."
- The "medical mode" begins, but the bedroom remains a sanctuary of trust.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES
- **Simonka (9):** Feels empowered by the process. Because her parents asked, the "stone" feels like something they are investigating *with* her, rather than something happening *to* her.
- **Klára:** Using her history with social workers and therapists to ensure Simonka never feels like a "subject" or an "object" of medical concern.
- **Gamaliel:** Deeply disciplined. His training as a dancer makes him acutely aware of the sanctity of the body and the importance of permission.

---

## SAGA CONNECTIONS
- **Foundational Trust:** This scene establishes the "Rules of Engagement" for Simonka’s medical journey.
- **Bodily Agency:** Directly contrasts with the later "Holic" storylines where the concept of "Good Girl" and service are explored; here, we see the healthy root of her autonomy.
